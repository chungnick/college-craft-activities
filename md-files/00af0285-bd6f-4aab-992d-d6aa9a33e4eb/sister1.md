# Programs for Minors

## Overview

The Programs for Minors Office provides oversight for university-sponsored programs, activities, camps and events (PACE) involving minor participants to ensure compliance with regulations and to provide a positive environment that promotes health, wellness, safety and security.

If you have questions about hosting a PACE for minors, please contact the Programs for Minors office at [ypp@utdallas.edu](mailto:ypp@utdallas.edu) or call [972-883-3815](tel:+19728833815).

## Child Abuse Hotline

Texas law requires anyone with knowledge of suspected child abuse or neglect to immediately report it to the Department of Family and Protective Services (DFPS). Reports can be made at any time by phone at [1-800-252-5400](tel:+18002525400) or online at [Texas Abuse Hotline](https://www.txabusehotline.org/Login/Default.aspx).
