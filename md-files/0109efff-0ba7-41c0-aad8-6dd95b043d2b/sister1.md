## Academic Year 2025-2026

Attendance and participation are required for students to successfully complete their courses. Therefore, all NYU Precollege students are required to attend the program for the full duration of the term.  If the dates of the program do not match a student’s calendar, the student is strongly encouraged to consider another [NYU high school program](/admissions/high-school-and-middle-school-programs.html) that better fits their schedule.

Below are highlighted dates and deadlines for each NYU Precollege term. For more details such as university holidays and course registration deadlines, please see the [NYU Academic Calendar](/students/student-information-and-resources/registration-records-and-graduation/academic-calendar.html).

### Fall 2025 Term

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Application Opens | June 17, 2025 |
| Application Deadline | August 15, 2025 |
| First Day of Classes | September 2, 2025 |
| Last Day of Classes | December 11, 2025 |
| Exam Period | December 15-19, 2025 |

### Spring 2026 Term

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Application Opens | November 1, 2025 |
| Application Deadline | January 5, 2026 |
| First Day of Classes | January 20, 2026 |
| Last Day of Classes | May 5, 2026 |
| Exam Period | May 7-13, 2026 |

### Summer 2026 Term

#### **Students living on campus are recommended to apply by May 1 in order to meet the June 1 housing registration deadline.**

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Application Opens | February 1, 2026 |
| Application Deadline for International Students Coming to Campus | March 13, 2026 |
| Housing Registration Deadline for Residential Students | June 1, 2026 |
| Application Deadline for U.S. and Online Students | June 15, 2026 |
| Move-In Day for Residential Students | June 30, 2026 |
| First Day of Classes | July 1, 2026 |
| Last Day of Classes & Exams | August 12, 2026 |
| Move-Out Day for Residential Students | August 12, 2026 |
