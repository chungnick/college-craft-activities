# Additional Opportunities for Pre\-Medical Students

The Office of Post Baccalaureate and Outreach Programs offers opportunities for undergraduate students to interact with UCSF faculty, students, and staff. We offer an annual admissions workshop in March and participate in multiple pre\-medical and medical student organization conferences. We are proud to partner with Kaiser Permanente's Medical Scholars Pathway Program, which supports community college students interested in careers in health sciences, Additionally, we host virtual open houses that provide information on general medical school admissions prerequisites and UCSF programs. Learn more about our upcoming outreach events below.
