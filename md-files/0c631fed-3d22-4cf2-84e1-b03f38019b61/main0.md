## Regeneron ISEF 2026 - Phoenix, Arizona

Regeneron ISEF is coming to Phoenix, Arizona! The Regeneron ISEF event week will be held from May 9-15, 2026 at the Phoenix Convention Center.

More than 500 volunteers will be needed across a spectrum of activities! Learn more about volunteering **[here](https://www.societyforscience.org/isef/volunteers/)** and **[sign up to get notified when the 2026 ISEF Volunteer Registration goes live](https://societyforscience.tfaforms.net/976)!**

More than 1000 judges are needed to determine the winners of the 2026 Regeneron ISEF! Learn more about serving as a Grand Award judge [**here**](https://www.societyforscience.org/isef/grand-award/) and [**sign up to be notified when the judge application opens here!**](https://societyforscience.tfaforms.net/989)

Interested in competing? Use the links below to find an Affiliated Fair in your area, learn about the [**Updated 2026 International Rules**](https://www.societyforscience.org/isef/international-rules/), and find answers to Frequently Asked Questions.

[### Volunteer for 2026](https://www.societyforscience.org/isef/volunteers/)

Sign up to support the 2026 Regeneron ISEF in Phoenix, Arizona, in May 2026 as interpreters and event volunteers.

[### Serve as a Grand Award Judge in 2026](https://www.societyforscience.org/isef/grand-award/)

Learn more about Grand Award Judging at Regeneron ISEF.

[### Visit the Regeneron ISEF Virtual Fair](https://partner.projectboard.world/isef/home)

Learn more about projects and finalists from Regeneron ISEF

[### Awards](https://www.societyforscience.org/isef/awards/)

Regeneron ISEF finalists compete for nearly $9 million in awards, prizes and scholarships.

[### Special Award Organizations (SAO)](https://www.societyforscience.org/isef/special-award-organizations/)

More than 45 professional organizations give out Special Awards at Regeneron ISEF.

[### Learn about Sponsorship](https://www.societyforscience.org/isef-sponsorship/)

Join the exclusive group of forward-thinking corporations, foundations, organizations, and individuals that provide support for ISEF.

[### Regeneron ISEF Categories](https://www.societyforscience.org/isef/categories-and-subcategories/all-categories/)

Regeneron ISEF finalists compete across 22 different scientific categories.

[### Education Outreach Day](https://www.societyforscience.org/isef/education-outreach-day/)

Education Outreach Day brings local middle and high school students to ISEF to meet the ISEF Finalists, participate in STEM activities, and create their own scientific questions.

[### Scientific Integrity](https://www.societyforscience.org/scientific-integrity/)

Society for Science expects students competing in Regeneron ISEF to hold themselves to the highest ethical standards.
