## **Summer 2026 Dates and Timeline:**

### **Session 1: June 21 - July 11**

* [History 217: The Road to the American Revolution](/as/niahd/precol/courses/history217/index.php)
* [History 218: The Road to the U. S. Civil War](/as/niahd/precol/courses/history218/index.php)

### **Session 2: July 12 - August 1**

* [History 218: The Road to the U. S. Civil War](/as/niahd/precol/courses/history218/index.php)
* [History 219: American Independence](/as/niahd/precol/courses/history219/index.php)

Timeline

|  |
| --- |
| **Summer 2026 Application Timeline**  * **15 November 2025:** Application Portal Opens * **Late February 2026:** NIAHD will begin to send admissions decisions to students whose applications are complete    + NIAHD will make admission decisions for applicants requesting [Financial Assistance](/as/niahd/precol/finance/assistance/index.php) after we receive both the completed program application and financial assistance application   + NIAHD will review applications in the order in which they are completed and will continue to admit students throughout the spring until the program is full.  Admission to NIAHD's Pre-College Program is not guaranteed, and space in our Program is limited. * **1 May 2026:** Deadline for International Students * **15 May 2026:**  Application deadline for Domestic Students applying to Session 1 * **6 June 2026:** Application deadline for Domestic Students applying to Session 2 * **Late Applicants:** NIAHD will continue to accept and review applications until the Program is filled.  However, please be aware that anyone applying after the deadline will have less time to submit payment and registration forms. |
