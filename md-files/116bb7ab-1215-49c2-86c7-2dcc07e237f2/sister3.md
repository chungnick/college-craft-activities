## Your bright future begins now.

Take the next step toward your future at UVA Wise, apply now with no application fee.

Apply

Apply to UVA Wise as a traditional freshman, transfer, non-degree, or graduate student with the UVA Wise Application.

[Apply Now](https://apply.uvawise.edu/apply)

## Online Teaching Licensure and Recertification Classes

The [Center for Teaching Excellence](https://cte.uvawise.edu/ "Center for Teaching Excellence") (CTE) offers undergraduate-level courses that meet the needs of those with (1) a provisional teaching license who are seeking full licensure and those with (2) full teaching licenses seeking recertification. [Learn more about coursework offerings](https://cte.uvawise.edu/licensure-courses "Center for Teaching Excellence") and [registering for classes](https://cte.uvawise.edu/registration).
