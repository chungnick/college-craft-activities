# Summer Leaders Academy Fees and Payment Policies

## 2026 Tuition Rates

We offer a wide range of pricing options determined by choice of credit versus non-credit bearing courses and commuter versus residential program fees.

| Tuition and Program Fee | Resident | Commuter |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1-Week Track: Immersions 1, 2, and 3 (NON-CREDIT) | $2,086 | $1,418 |
| 2-Week Track: Immersions 2 and 3 (NON-CREDIT) | $4,332 | $3,125 |
| Immersions 2 and 3: 3-CREDIT | $5,039 | $3,832 |
| Immersions 2 and 3: 4-CREDIT | $6,173 | $4,966 |

**Additional Information:**

* Tuition
  + 1 Week, non-credit: $1,199
  + 2 Week, non-credit: $2,695
  + 3-Credit: $3,402
  + 4-Credit: $4,536
* Residential rates include three meals a day; housing at Rose Hill; afternoon, evening, and weekend events; and intercampus transportation.
  + 1 Week: $887
  + 2 Week: $1,637
* Commuter rates include lunches;  afternoon, evening, and weekend events; and intercampus transportation.
  + 1 Week: $219
  + 2 Week: $430

### Withdrawal and Refund Policies

Withdrawal requests must be made in writing via email to [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#6f1c1a02020a1d030a0e0b0a1d1c2f09001d0b070e02410a0b1a). Application fees and deposits are non-refundable. Our general refund policies are below, as are specific dates for the Summer 2026 program.

Withdrawal requests received at least three weeks prior to the first day of class will be eligible for a 100% tuition refund (after the forfeit of the application fee and deposit).

Withdrawal requests received less than three weeks prior to the first day of class, but prior to the second day of class, will be eligible for a 40% tuition refund (after the forfeit of the application fee and deposit).

Withdrawal requests received on the second day of class or later are not eligible for a refund. Please refer to the chart below for our complete refund schedule.

| Course Start Date | 100% Refund Withdrawal | 40% Refund Withdrawal | 0% Refund Withdrawal |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Immersion 1: June 22 | On or before June 1 | June 2-June 22 | On or after June 23 |
| Immersion 2: July 6 | On or before June 15 | June 16-July 6 | On or after July 7 |
| Immersion 3: July 20 | On or before June 29 | June 30-July 20 | On or after July 21 |
