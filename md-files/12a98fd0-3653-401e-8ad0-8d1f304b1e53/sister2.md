Planning for summer at Yale? This page outlines tuition and fees for Yale Summer Session programs.

> [point] Tuition and fees depend on the program, length and location of the selected program.
>
> * For **New Haven (Yale campus)** and **Online** programs and courses, see details below.
> * For **Yale Summer Session Programs Abroad** (taught outside the U.S.), please visit [Yale Study Abroad](https://studyabroad.yale.edu/yale-summer/yss-programs-abroad-costs-funding).

## Summer 2026 Tuition

Below are the tuition rates for Summer 2026 Yale Summer Session courses. Tuition and fees are subject to change.

| Description | Amount |
| --- | --- |
| 1-credit course (in person or online)\* | $5,480 |
| 1.5-credit foreign language course | $5,480 |
| 0.5-credit science laboratory | $2,740 |
| 2-credit course | $10,705 |
| Yale Summer Session Acting Program (2-credit course) | $10,705 |
| English Language Institute: Intensive English or Postgraduate Seminar | $5,535 |
| English Language Institute: Business or Law Seminar or University Preparation Program | $6,780 |

**\* One Yale College credit equates to four semester hours.**

For information about Yale Writers' Workshop and Yale Young Writers' Workshop costs, [please see the program page](/academics/yale-writers-workshop-dates-and-costs "Yale Writers' Workshop: Dates and Costs").

## Summer 2026 Fees

| **Description** | **Amount** |
| --- | --- |
| Application Fee (all students - nonrefundable) | $80 |
| CISI Health Insurance Fee (visiting international students who receive an I-20 from YSS - nonrefundable) | Depends on session length, see below |
| Technology Fee (online courses only) | $85 |
| I-20 Processing Fee (visiting international students who receive an I-20 from YSS - nonrefundable) | $125 |
| For visiting students (non-Yale students), a non-refundable $500 deposit and $50 administrative fee are due by the posted deadline to confirm enrollment when admitted. $500 is applied toward tuition and reflected on the student bill. Nonpayment by the payment deadline will result in withdrawal from all courses. | $550 |

### CISI Health Insurance Fee

The CISI health insurance fee applies to visiting international students who receive an I-20 from Yale Summer Session (YSS) and are living both on and off campus. The CISI fee provides comprehensive health insurance coverage to visiting international students for the duration of your session, ensuring you have access to necessary medical care while studying at Yale. The coverage begins on your program's start date and lasts for the full length of your session. For detailed information about the CISI health insurance plan, including coverage specifics, benefits, and how to access care, please [visit our Visiting Students Living on Campus page.](/admitted-students/visiting-students-living-campus "Visiting Students Living On Campus")

> #### CISI 2026 Fees:
>
> * 5 weeks: $140
> * 10 weeks: $280

Please note that the CISI Health Insurance differs from the Yale Health fee. The Yale Health fee gives all students living on campus access to limited care at the Yale Health Center.

## Summer 2026 Residential Program Fees

Residential Program Fee includes your dorm room in a residential college, meals, and student activities (rates subject to change).

| Program | Room & Board | Yale Health Fee (required for all visiting students) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Session A or B (5 weeks) | $4,380 | $175 |
| Session A & B (10 weeks) | $8,760 | $350 |

Visit [Payment and Refunds](/apply/payment-and-refunds "Payment and Refunds") for your payment options and [Financial Assistance](/apply/financial-assistance "Financial Assistance") for more information.
