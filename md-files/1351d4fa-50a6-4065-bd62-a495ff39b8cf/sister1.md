## Engineering Discovery for Girls

Come Discover what Engineering can do for YOU. Aspiring engineers and tomorrow’s problem
solvers are encouraged to participate in this extraordinary camp! Enjoy a week of
learning, hands-on activities, highlighting amazing engineering projects, group problem
solving and networking with your fellow friends and peers. Engineering Discovery Camp
for Girls is the perfect opportunity to engage with other young forward-thinking and
compassionate engineers! Don’t know if this camp is for you? Come and discover how
YOU can be an engineer!

Starts Monday at 1pm and ends Friday at 1pm, 8am - 5pm each day with evening functions
and activities unless otherwise noted.

Open to all participants regardless of gender and geared towards rising 9th - 12th
grade students.
