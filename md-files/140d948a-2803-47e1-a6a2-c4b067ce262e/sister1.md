### Welcome to rocsummeryouth.com! Your Resource for Youth Employment Programs in Monroe County, NY!

Here, at **[rocsummeryouth.com](https://rocsummeryouth.com/)**, you’ll find all the information you need about the **Summer Youth Employment Program (SYEP)** and the **Summer of Opportunity Program (SOOP)**, two amazing initiatives that provide youth with valuable work experience and skill-building opportunities.  Whether you’re a **[youth](https://rocsummeryouth.com/youth/)** interested in applying, a **[parent or guardian](https://rocsummeryouth.com/parents/)** seeking guidance, or an **[employer](https://rocsummeryouth.com/employers/)** eager to hire program participants, we’ve got you covered. We’re excited to help you get involved! Check back starting February 9th to apply for summer 2026!

### Good News!

If you meet income guidelines, you can apply for the **ROC Your Job After School Employment Program!** Positions are available October 1, 2025-June 30, 2026. Click **[here](https://rocyourjob.com/)** to learn more about ROC Your Job!
