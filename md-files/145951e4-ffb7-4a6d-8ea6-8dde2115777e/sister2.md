This is the Ladders for Leaders application and not an application for the Summer Youth Employment (SYEP). Submitting an application does not guarantee placement in the Ladders for Leaders program. Accepted students are required to complete up to 30 hours of pre-employment training and must interview with potential employers without guarantee of receiving an internship offer. Those interested in applying to SYEP must submit a separate application when the application becomes available.

The deadline to submit applications is 1/16/2026. There are 2 days left before the deadline.

## About Ladders for Leaders

Ladders for Leaders is a nationally recognized program that offers high school and college students the opportunity to participate in paid professional summer internships with leading corporations, nonprofit organizations, and government agencies in New York City.

## WHO IS ELIGIBLE?

#### High school juniors and seniors, or college students aged 16 to 24

#### NYC resident

#### Prior work experience, either paid or volunteer

#### Legally allowed to work in New York City

## WHAT DOES THE PROGRAM OFFER?

#### Ladders for Leaders will give youth a unique opportunity to explore their interests and discover new ones through:

#### Pre-employment Training

Receive help with résumé and cover letter writing, and interviewing skills. Learn essential workplace readiness skills and business etiquette.

#### Opportunity to join our growing Alumni Network

Broad network of alumni, post-internship opportunities and networking events open only to past participants of the program.

#### Paid Summer Internships

Paid internship opportunities available in a variety of industries to accommodate student interests.

#### Financial Literacy

Feel empowered learning financial literacy skills this summer.

## UPLIFTING COMMUNITIES SINCE *1963*

For over six decades, the Summer Youth Employment Program has provided invaluable work experience and career exploration opportunities to generations of young New Yorkers.

In our [YouTube series](https://youtube.com/playlist?list=PLxwevIQHWXx3fJTkMeZVqcztOzD-vQEO5&si=wa-oIUxrhnOh87If), discover a variety of viewpoints, listen to participant success stories, and understand how employers have been instrumental in shaping the future workforce of the City.
