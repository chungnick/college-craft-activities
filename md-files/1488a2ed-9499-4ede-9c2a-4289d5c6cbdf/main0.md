# Credit Course Options

#### Credit Options for Current 10th & 11th Grade Students

**Applications available March 1 – June 15**  
**2026 Term dates June 29 – August 10, 2026**

Are you a current 10th or 11th grade student (2025-2026 school year) who is interested in earning college credit this summer?

Choose from Duke University courses from across the arts and sciences! A selection of six-week undergraduate courses in Summer Session Term 2 (June 29 – August 10) are available to academically qualified high school students. **Participants must be 16-years-old by the first day of the term (June 29) to be eligible**. There are no exceptions to this university policy.

Credit options for high school students include courses at Duke University for **local** commuter students and online courses for non-commuters, including international high school students.

If you are interested in a non-credit residential experience at Duke, please visit the [Duke Pre-College website](https://learnmore.duke.edu/precollege/all-programs).
