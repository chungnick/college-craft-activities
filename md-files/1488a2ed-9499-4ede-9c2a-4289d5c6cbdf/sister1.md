# Tuition, Fees & Payment

### Tuition for 2026

Tuition for undergraduates and visiting students, including high school students.

* **$3,145** for regular or non-science lab course

### Required fees for Summer 2026

* Transcript Fee **$120** - All students are required to pay a one-time transcript fee the first time they take a Duke course for credit. This fee covers lifetime, unlimited copies of your Duke transcript.

### Payment

Payment of tuition and fees is made directly to the Duke University Bursar’s Office. Note that Duke does not accept credit card payments.  Information on payment options can be found on the [Bursar's Office website](https://finance.duke.edu/bursar/Payments).

**Bill date –Term 2: May 15**

**Due date –Term 2: June 12**

\*If you are enrolled in a course after the billing date, the Summer Session office will provide directions on how you can make your payment.
