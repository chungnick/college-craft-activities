# Summer Session Tuition and Fees

## 2026 Tuition

* *Undergraduate* -  $1,134 per credit
* *Graduate Arts and Sciences*- $1,688 per credit (2025; 2026 rates forthcoming)
* *Graduate Language Reading*- $1,688 per credit (2025; 2026 rates forthcoming)

## Registration Fee

There is no registration fee, nor are there additional fees for facilities usage.

## Lab and Course Fees (estimated)

* *Language Lab -* $25
* *COMM Film Studies Course -*$50
* *Labs in Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Psychology, Video/Graphic Design, and Graduate Computer Science -*$75
* *Photography -*$150

## Other Fees

* *International Student Partner Program - $200.00*
* *Housing at LC Session I or II (double room) -* TBA
* *Housing at LC Session I or II (single room) -*  TBA
* *Housing at RH Session I or II (double room) -* $1,326 (estimated)
* *Returned check surcharge -* $35
* *Meals* Available on a Food Plan or Pay-As-You-Go.

## Policies Regarding Refunds

* Students must follow policies regarding [course withdrawals](/summer-session/registration-and-housing/policies-and-procedures/withdrawals-and-refunds/)[,](/info/21527/policies_and_procedures/3343/withdrawals_and_refunds) [changes (Add/Drop)](/summer-session/registration-and-housing/policies-and-procedures/attendance-and-late-registration/) or [refunds](/summer-session/registration-and-housing/policies-and-procedures/withdrawals-and-refunds/).
* ***NONREFUNDABLE FEES**:* Laboratory, special course fees, and maintenance of matriculation after classes begin.
