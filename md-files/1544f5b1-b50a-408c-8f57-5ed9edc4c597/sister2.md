# Summer Session Calendar

# **2026 CALENDAR**

---

## Session I: May 26 - June 25, 2026

**Thursday, May 14**  
Last day for non-matriculated student registration for Summer Session I

**Monday, May 25**University Closed - Memorial Day Weekend

**Tuesday, May 26**  
Undergraduate and GSAS (T, Th) Summer Session I classes begin

**Wednesday, May 27**  
Last day to add/drop Summer Session I classes*;*GSAS (M, W) classes begin

**Friday, May 29**  
GSAS (M, W) make-up class

**Thursday, June 18**  
Undergraduate and GSAS last day to withdraw without incurring a WF; Last day to designate a course pass/fail or change class to audit

**Friday, June 19**  
University Closed – Juneteenth (observed)

**Wednesday, June 24**  
GSAS (M, W) Summer Session I final exam

**Thursday, June 25**  
Undergraduate and GSAS (T, Th) Summer Session I final exam

## Session II: June 30 - August 4, 2026

**Thursday, June 11**  
Last day for non-matriculated student registration for Summer Session II

**Tuesday, June 30**  
Undergraduate and GSAS (T, Th) Summer Session II classes begin

**Wednesday, July 1**  
Last day to add/drop Summer Session II classes; GSAS (M, W) Summer Session II classes begin

**Thursday, July 2**University Closed - University Holiday

**Friday, July 3**  
University Closed - Independence Day

**Wednesday, July 22**  
Undergraduate and GSAS Summer Session II last day to withdraw without incurring a WF; last day to designate a course pass/fail or change class to audit

**Monday, August 3**  
4-credit undergraduate and GSAS (M, W) Summer Session II classes final exam

**Tuesday, August 4**  
3-credit undergraduate and GSAS (T, Th) Summer Session II classes final exam

**Friday, Sept. 18**  
Undergraduate and GSAS deadline for removal of ABS, INC, NGR grades incurred during Summer Session

## Session III, Online and Intensive: May 26 - August 4, 2026

**Tuesday, May 26**  
All special-format classes begin

**Friday, May 29**  
Last day to add/drop

**Wednesday, July 22**  
Last day to withdraw without incurring WF Undergraduate and GSAS Session III; last day to designate a course pass/fail or change class to audit

**Tuesday, August 4**  
Undergraduate and GSAS final exam

**Important Reminders:**

**Add/Drop**: This is the deadline by which you can make changes to your schedule without your transcript reflecting previous registrations. Courses dropped after this date will be shown as "W," Withdrawn, on your transcript. Please consult the [Withdrawals](/summer-session/registration-and-housing/policies-and-procedures/withdrawals-and-refunds/) section regarding tuition refund policies.

***WF***: This designation reflects withdrawal in a state of failure for the course. WF is factored as a 0 in the GPA.

**RH, LC**: Refers to undergraduate courses at the RH (Bronx) and LC (Manhattan) campuses.

**GSAS**: Refers to Graduate School of Arts and Sciences classes only. For information on Fordham University's other graduate programs, please visit the following pages: Graduate Religion, Graduate Education AP Institutes, [Graduate Social Service](https://www.fordham.edu/info/20348/graduate_school_of_social_service).
