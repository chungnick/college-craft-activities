# Summer Session

June 20, 2026 — August 16, 2026

## This summer, experience Stanford

By combining challenging academics with a rich array of extra-curricular programming, Stanford Summer Session shares the university's culture of innovation, academic excellence, and global responsibility.

## Innovation and Entrepreneurship

INTEREST AREA

Effect change and question well-worn patterns of thought. Find connections others don't. Test a concept, prototype a new product, and scale your idea.

[Explore all the summer courses we offer](/courses)

## Explore. Discover. Transform.

Courses

We offer foundational core courses and quintessentially Stanford classes from over thirty departments. We have grouped these into seven interest areas.

[Explore our course offerings](/courses)

### Natural sciences

Interest Area

Biology, physiology, chemistry, physics. Explore the natural world.

[Natural sciences courses](https://summer-preview.stanford.edu/courses?interests=Natural+Sciences)

### Creativity and design

Interest Area

Studio art, film production, music, and making. Express yourself.

[Creativity and design courses](https://summer-preview.stanford.edu/courses?interests=Creativity+and+Design)

### Innovation and entrepreneurship

Interest Area

Game theory, design thinking, management science, and technology entrepreneurship. Test your idea and learn to scale it.

[Innovation and entrepreneurship courses](https://summer-preview.stanford.edu/courses?interests=Innovation+and+Entrepreneurship)

### Math and data science

Interest Area

Probability and statistics, data mining and analysis, multivariable calculus, and differential equations. Understand and analyze data.

[Math and data science courses](https://summer-preview.stanford.edu/courses?interests=Math+and+Data+Science)

### Computer science and engineering

Interest Area

Programming abstractions, designing algorithms, energy essentials, circuits, and more.

[Computer science and engineering courses](https://summer-preview.stanford.edu/courses?interests=Computer+Science+and+Engineering)

### Personal development

Interest Area

Tennis, swimming, wellness, and study skills. Round out your class schedule with some attention to your well-being.

[Personal development courses](https://summer-preview.stanford.edu/courses?interests=Personal+Development)

### Social sciences and humanities

Interest Area

Languages, philosophy, economics, political science, history, and more. Explore the human experience.

[Social sciences and humanities courses](https://summer-preview.stanford.edu/courses?interests=Social+Sciences+and+Humanities)

### Writing and public speaking

Interest Area

Creative expression, public speaking, communication, and more. Learn to tell a story or persuade others.

[Writing and public speaking courses](https://summer-preview.stanford.edu/courses?interests=Writing+and+Public+Speaking)
