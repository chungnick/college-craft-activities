# Dates and Deadlines

We get it. This is the info you really need. It's all here—academic deadlines, Summer Quarter holidays, when bills will be posted. Trust us, we don't want you to miss anything either.

## Application Deadlines

### High School

| Application Open and Deadline | Date |
| --- | --- |
| Application opens | November 3, 2025 (Mon) |
| Early Decision submission deadline | December 15, 2025 (Mon, 9.p.m. PST) |
| Early Decision decision release date | January 15, 2026 (Thu) |
| High School Regular Decision submission deadline | January 20, 2026 (Tue, 9 p.m. PST) |
| High School Regular Decision decision release date | February 2, 2026 (Mon) |
| High School Late Application submission deadline | March 2, 2026 (Mon) |
| High School Late Application decision release date | March 9, 2026 (Mon) |

### University Level

| Application Open and Deadline | Date |
| --- | --- |
| Application opens | November 3, 2025 (Mon) |
| Early Decision submission deadline | December 15, 2025 (Mon, 9.p.m. PST) |
| Early Decision decision release date | January 15, 2026 (Thu) |
| University Level Regular Decision and Merit-based Tuition Grant deadline | February 13, 2026 (Fri, 9 p.m. PST) |
| University Level Regular Decision decision release date | March 4, 2026 (Wed) |
| International Applicant (needing an I-20) submission deadline | April 27, 2026 (Mon, 9 p.m. PDT) |
| University Level Rolling Admission submission deadline | May 22, 2026 (Fri, 9 p.m. PDT) |
| University Level Rolling Admission decision release date | 10 business days from submission |

## Academic Calendar

| Event | Date |
| --- | --- |
| Courses Posted | First week in February |
| Axess opens for course enrollment | April 6, 2026 (Mon) |
| Summer Bill posted | May 25, 2026 (Mon) |
| Final deadline to submit Health Requirements to Vaden Patient Portal | June 1, 2026 (Mon) |
| Cardinal Care Waiver deadline | June 15, 2026 (Mon) |
| Summer Bill due\* | June 20, 2026 (Sat) |
| Summer Session Program Mandatory Check-in | June 20, 2026 (Sat) |
| First day of quarter; instruction begins | June 22, 2026 (Mon) |
| Preliminary Study List deadline^ | June 22, 2026 (Mon, 5 p.m. PDT) |
| Final Study List deadline^^ | July 6, 2026 (Mon, 5 p.m. PDT) |
| Last day of classes | August 13, 2026 (Thu) |
| End-Quarter Examinations | August 14–15, 2026 (Fri & Sat) |
| Move out | August 16, 2026 (Sun, 8:00 a.m. PDT) |

\*If your full payment is not received by the published due date, a late payment penalty of 1% of the amount past due may be assessed.  
^Students must be enrolled in at least one course by this deadline. Last day to submit Annulment of Summer Registration to withdraw from the University with a full tuition refund.  
^^Final day to add or drop a class; no drops are permitted after this point. Last day for tuition reassessment for dropped courses or units. Students may withdraw from a course until the Course Withdrawal deadline and a 'W' notation will appear on the transcript.
