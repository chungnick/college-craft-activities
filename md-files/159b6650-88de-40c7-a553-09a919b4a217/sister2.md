## What will the program cost?

Stanford University sets tuition rates for all students. These estimates include Stanford's per-unit tuition of $1,505 per unit, the Application Fee, Program Fee, the Campus Health Services fee, and a Document Fee\*. Where applicable, estimates also include residential and meal fees.

### Tuition & Fees Calculator

Use our calculator to tailor your estimated cost to your specific circumstances.

[Calculate](/cost/tuition-fees-calculator)

### Commuter Student Estimate

Attending as a commuter^ and taking the minimum 3 units? Costs start at $5,312

### Residential Student Estimate

Living on campus and taking the minimum 8 credit-bearing units (typically 2-3 courses)? Costs start at $18,771

*\*The $250 document fee is a one-time fee charged to all students who are not returners to Summer Session. This fee offsets the cost of maintaining student records, processing transcript requests, and other registration-related services.*  
*^High School commuter costs start at $8,226 with a minimum 5 units.*

A very limited number of tuition grants will be available to undergraduate and graduate students only.
