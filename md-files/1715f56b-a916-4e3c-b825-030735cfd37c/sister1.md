## Choosing Your Major

We’re at the forefront of teaching and research in STEM, agriculture, business, design, education, humanities and social sciences, natural resources, textiles and veterinary medicine.

[Explore the NC State Catalog](https://catalog.ncsu.edu/)

When you apply to NC State, you’ll be asked to choose your preference for a first and second choice major.  
  
You should **select two different majors** from **different academic** [**colleges**](#our-colleges) based on your interests. If we determine you’re not competitive for your first-choice major, you’ll be reviewed based on your second-choice major.   
  
If you *do not* select a second-choice major, you will be reviewed for admission based on your first-choice major only.

## Pre-Professional Programs

Do you see medical school, law school or veterinary school in your future? You’ll build a strong foundation right here.

* ****Pre-Professional Advising**:** Our dedicated [pre-law](https://prelaw.dasa.ncsu.edu/), [pre-health](https://prehealth.dasa.ncsu.edu/) and [pre-vet](https://cals.ncsu.edu/vetpac) advisors can assist with prerequisite course selection, help with application materials, offer interview tips and more.
* ****Career Fairs:**** Build connections through career fairs, including our Health Professions Fair and our Law School and Graduate Programs Fair, that bring in representatives from leading medical and law schools across the country.
* ****Student Organizations:**** Go the extra mile toward your professional degree by getting involved with student organizations like the [Pre-Law Students’ Association](https://getinvolved.ncsu.edu/organization/551), [Pre-Health Club](https://prehealth.dasa.ncsu.edu/student-organizations-and-societies/pre-health-club/), [VetPAC](https://cals.ncsu.edu/vetpac/) and more.

### **Pre-Law Advising**

The Pre-Law Advising program is designed to help you explore the legal profession, build competitive law school applications and navigate the path to law school success. Offered by [Pre-Professional Services](https://careers.dasa.ncsu.edu/pre-professional) through the Career Development Center, the program provides one-on-one advising, LSAT preparation guidance, help with application materials and access to networking opportunities.

At NC State, there is no designated “Pre-Law” major. Students from any major can pursue a Pre-Law track with the help of pre-professional advisors. Beyond advising, the program coordinates co-curricular supports and experiential opportunities. Students can engage with the [Pre-Law Students’ Association (PLSA)](https://prelaw.dasa.ncsu.edu/pre-law-student-association/), attend the annual [law school fair](https://careers.dasa.ncsu.edu/gain-experience/career-fairs/), participate in seminars and workshops and pursue nearby internships or shadowing experiences.

[Prepare for law school](https://prelaw.dasa.ncsu.edu/)

### Pre-Health Advising

Our [Pre-Health Advising program](https://prehealth.dasa.ncsu.edu/) is a dedicated support system for students pursuing careers in health professions. Whether your goal is to pursue medical, dental, optometry, pharmacy, physician assistant or another health-related graduate program, the Pre-Health Advising office provides expert guidance and resources to help you prepare.

Because there is no formal “Pre-Health” major at NC State, students are encouraged to choose the major that most interests them. Advisors will assist students in strategically selecting courses to fulfill prerequisite requirements for health professional schools (e.g. biology, chemistry, physics, calculus, biochemistry). Students can also get involved in student organizations and other health outreach opportunities, like the [Pre-Health club](https://getinvolved.ncsu.edu/organization/550) or the annual [health professions fair](https://app.careerfairplus.com/ncsu_nc).

[Prepare for a health profession](https://prehealth.dasa.ncsu.edu/)

### **Pre-Vet** Advising

NC State’s [Veterinary Professions Advising Center (VetPAC)](https://cals.ncsu.edu/vetpac/) provides specialized guidance for students pursuing careers in veterinary medicine. Because there is no designated “Pre-Vet” major, VetPAC helps students across all disciplines identify prerequisite courses, build strong applications and prepare for interviews and career pathways. The center also connects students with experiential learning opportunities including internships, animal units, research labs and community partnerships, ensuring you gain hands-on exposure to veterinary practice.

Beyond one-on-one advising, VetPAC coordinates [annual events](https://cals.ncsu.edu/vetpac/how-can-we-help/vetpac-annual-events/), including seminars and an Annual Networking Event that hosts over 60 veterinarians and CVM admissions representatives. The program also leads initiatives such as [VetCAMP](https://cals.ncsu.edu/vetpac/vetcamp/), a multi-day summer camp for prospective High School students.

[Prepare for veterinary school](https://cals.ncsu.edu/vetpac/)

## Career Development

We’re known for producing real-world-ready graduates. Take advantage of our resources to get a head start on your career — and get the hands-on training you need to grab attention in a competitive workforce.

Our [Career Development Center](https://careers.dasa.ncsu.edu/) is filled with skilled staff who will work with you to map out your career path, review your resume and cover letter, conduct mock interviews, and support you in anything else you need to achieve your professional goals.

$71K

median starting salary for our undergraduates

Students can team up with our career counselors as soon as they arrive on campus, and many academic colleges have their own career development resources, too.

### Career Fairs

Every year, we bring leading employers to our campus by the hundreds through our [career fairs and events](https://careers.dasa.ncsu.edu/gain-experience/career-fairs/). In fact, our Engineering Career Fair is **the largest of its kind on the East Coast**.

Top recruiters seek out NC State students because they know they can rely on our Wolfpack when there’s a job to be done. And you can begin making meaningful contacts from day one.

### Internship and Co-op Opportunities

Get assistance in landing everything from internships to co-ops to entry-level positions.

Our online job portal, ePACK, connects students with **job openings and upcoming recruitment events**. Plus, our innovative Centennial Campus puts you within steps of employers ready to offer on-the-job experience. An added bonus? Many majors across the university will give course credit for your internship.

We’ve also got one of the [largest cooperative education programs](https://careers.dasa.ncsu.edu/overview/) in the state, where students put their knowledge into practice as they alternate semesters of full-time study and full-time work.
