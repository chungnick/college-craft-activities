# Program Timeline

#### First Year

Summer prior: 6 week Lab Rotation (optional)  
  
Trainees are encouraged (but not required) to spend 6 weeks of the summer prior to their first year completing a laboratory rotation at UCSF, which can later be applied toward the two-rotation requirement for graduate school. The MSTP office can provide advice and assistance in arranging your rotation, and stipend support from the MSTP grant is available beginning June 15.

* MSTP Faculty Advisor Assignments are made in the first year.
* First-year SOM curriculum allows trainees time during the Essential Core to take graduate courses
* MSTP Big Sib Assignment

#### Third Year

* Join Thesis Lab by the start of third year.
* Complete Qualifying Examinations by end of third year (encouraged)
* Form Thesis Committee, advance to candidacy (one member **must** be from the MSTP Council)
* Thesis Committee Meetings - **MSTPS are required to meet with their Thesis Committee every six months.**
* Apply for F30/31 Fellowship**\*** (some time during PhD years)
* Clinical Preceptorship: MED 160.04 (1-3 Quarters)
* Clinical Case Reviews (Optional)
* Attend MSTP Annual Retreat**\***

#### Fourth Year

* Complete Qualifying Examinations (required by end of 4th year).
* Form Thesis Committee (one member **must** be from the MSTP Council)
* Thesis Committee Meeting every 6 months.**\***
* Apply for F30/31 Fellowship**\*** (some time during PhD years)
* Clinical Preceptorship: MED 160.04 (1-3 Quarters)
* Clinical Case Reviews (Optional)
* Apply for F30/31 Fellowship**\*** (some time during PhD years)
* Attend MSTP Annual Retreat**\***

#### Fifth and Sixth Years

* Thesis Project, Complete PhD Requirements
* Thesis Committee Meeting every 6 months.**\***
* Projected PhD Completion every January
* Clinical Preceptorship: MED 160.04 (1-3 Quarters)
* Clinical Case Reviews (Optional)
* PhD Completion by end of 6th Year (Encouraged)
* Attend MSTP Annual Retreat**\***

#### Sixth through Eighth Years

* Complete PhD
* Late March/Early April - "Into the Clinic" Course: MED 170.36 (Taught by MSTP Med 3/4s**\***)
* Return to School of Medicine
* Complete MD Requirements
* National Board Examination: USMLE Step 2
* Apply for Residencies
* Attend MTP Annual Retreat**\***
* Graduation!

#### Year-Round MSTP Events

* Weekly MSTP Basic Science Faculty Seminar
* MSTP socials hosted by each class
* MSTP Vertical Mentoring Groups organized by research interest
* MSTP Community of Underrepresented Physician Scientists (CUPS) Quarterly Events
* MSTP Women's Physician Scientists Committee Quarterly Events
* MSTP Trefethen Visiting Professor Seminar and MSTP Banquet
* Clinical Advisors Program for MSTP's returning to SOM
