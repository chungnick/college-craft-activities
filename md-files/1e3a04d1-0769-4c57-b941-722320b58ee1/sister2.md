Live on a college campus. Sightsee like a pro. Ignite your passion.

Summer Discovery stands as the premier global leader in pre-college academic programs, offering transformative experiences for students in high school and middle school. Summer Discovery has a rich heritage of partnership directly with exceptional universities to bring unparalleled access to university-curated curriculum with hand-selected instructors and faculty.

Summer Discovery also offers a proprietary academic curriculum for gifted and talented students in grades

1-12.

Our programs provide students worldwide with an unparalleled summer journey, blending world-class academics, immersive travel, and vibrant residential life at top-tier university campuses. We are dedicated to igniting students' passions, encouraging them to become explorers, adventurers, and lifelong learners.

Summer Discovery currently has served over 100,000 students and has been leading the way in innovative educational experiences for high school students for nearly 60 years . Summer Discovery alumni share,

* 84% felt more prepared for college after their Summer Discovery program,
* 86% apply to a top-tier university, and
* 88% are leveraging their Summer Discovery experience to strengthen their college portfolio.
