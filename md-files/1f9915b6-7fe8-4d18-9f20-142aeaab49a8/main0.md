Discover a unique summer learning experience at our academies on the UT campus. Tailored for high school students entering grades 10–12, each program is taught by UT faculty and focuses on a specialized area of computer science, offering hands-on experiences and expert mentorship in a dynamic setting. Registration opens this Spring.

## Open-Enrollment Academies

Explore computer science this summer through hands-on academies led by UT faculty. Designed for high school students entering grades 10–12, each program focuses on a specialized area of computing with real-world applications and mentorship from experts.

## Academy for Game Development

Create, code, and collaborate at the Academy for Game Development. Explore how games are built from concept to launch and learn what it takes to turn ideas into interactive experiences.

[Develop Games](/engage/outreach/academies/game-development)

## Academy for iOS App Development

Design and develop your own mobile app at the Academy for iOS App Development. Learn how creativity and coding come together to solve real-world challenges.

[Build Your App](/engage/outreach/academies/ios-development)

## Academy for Machine Learning

Explore the future of computing at the Academy for Machine Learning. Learn Python, experiment with AI tools and see how data-driven technology shapes our world.

[Start Computing](/engage/outreach/academies/machine-learning)

## Academy for Robotics

Build, program, and race robots at the Academy for Robotics. Experience the excitement of hands-on engineering while learning how technology powers innovation.

[Plug into Robotics](/engage/outreach/academies/robotics)

## Scholars Academies

Build new skills, strengthen what you know and experience life as a Longhorn while learning the ropes of programming or enhancing your existing skills in the Scholars Academies. These free residential programs for high school students expand access to computing education and inspire future innovators to pursue their potential with confidence. Join us right here on the UT campus.

## Academy for All

Experience computer science at the Academy for All, a one-week summer program at UT Computer Science. Build coding and teamwork skills, explore tech careers and connect with peers who share your curiosity for innovation.

[Get Coding](/engage/outreach/academies/all)

## Academy for Women

At the Academy for Women, high school students gain real-world coding experience and explore the impact of technology through a supportive learning community at UT Computer Science.

[Get Hands-on](/engage/outreach/academies/women)

## Get the inside track!

Join our email list now to be the first to know when enrollment or applications open for our academies. Don't miss out — participation is a limited opportunity, and our academies fill up fast!
