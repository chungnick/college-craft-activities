* Emerging Engineer Camp

Unleash your potential at Kent State's Emerging Engineer Camps!

Get hands-on with experiments, explore real-world engineering, and complete an exciting design project. Whether you're in middle or high school, these camps are your ticket to a summer of discovery and fun in STEM!

**When:** June 22-26, 2026

**Middle School Camp:** Grades 6-8 | June 22-24

**High School Camp:** Grades 9-12 | June 22-26

**Time:** 8:30 am - 3:30 pm (lunch provided)

**Location:** Aeronautics and Engineering Building - Kent Campus

Join us for a summer where fun meets the future of engineering!

For details, please contact Cassy Mendenhall, [cmenden6@kent.edu](mailto:cmenden6@kent.edu).

The 2026 Daily Schedules will be available soon.  The theme this summer is aerospace!
