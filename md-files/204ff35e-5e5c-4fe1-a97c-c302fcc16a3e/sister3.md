# College of Aeronautics and Engineering Camps

* [Kent](/)
* [College of Aeronautics and Engineering](/cae)
* [K-12 and Educators](/cae/k-12-and-educators)
* College of Aeronautics and Engineering Camps

Dive into our STEM summer camps focused on aviation and engineering. Each camp is tailored to spark curiosity and foster innovation, blending essential concepts in science, technology, engineering, mathematics, and aviation. Through practical exercises and challenges, participants will explore key principles and real-world applications. Join us to gain hands-on experience and prepare for a future of opportunities in STEM fields.

* [Aviation Career Education (A.C.E.) Academy](/cae/aviation-career-education-ace-academy)

  The Six District Educational Compact partners with Kent State University's College of Aeronautics and Engineering to offer this unique, tuition-based, week-long experience exploring aviation and advanced air mobility.

  [Read More](/cae/aviation-career-education-ace-academy)
* [Emerging Engineer Camp](/cae/emerging-engineer-camp)

  Get hands-on with experiments, explore real-world engineering, and complete an exciting design project. Whether you're in middle or high school, these camps are your ticket to a summer of discovery and fun in STEM!

  [Read More](/cae/emerging-engineer-camp)
* [Nikki Kukwa Aeronautics Memorial Camp](/cae/nikki-kukwa-aeronautics-memorial-camp)

  In this camp, 20 female high school students will receive a free, three-day camp experience while staying in dorms on Kent State University’s Kent Campus.  The camp includes a tour of a regional airport, experience at KSU’s Air Traffic Control Lab and a flight out of the KSU Airport.

  [Read More](/cae/nikki-kukwa-aeronautics-memorial-camp)
