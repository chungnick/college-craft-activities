## **Welcome**

[Cornell AgriTech](https://agritech.cals.cornell.edu/) in Geneva, New York offers a Summer Research Scholars Program where undergraduate students participate in exciting [projects](https://cals.cornell.edu/cornell-agritech/our-expertise/student-programs/summer-scholars/projects) in **agricultural research, food and beverage entrepreneurship,** and **business development**.

For all internship programs, students have the opportunity to work with faculty, their graduate students, postdocs, and senior level staff on projects to jumpstart future career paths.

## About Geneva

Geneva, New York is the home city of Cornell AgriTech.

### In the Finger Lakes wine region

Located at the northern end of the beautiful and scenic 55 km-long Seneca Lake, Geneva is midway between Rochester, Syracuse, and Ithaca (home of [Cornell University](http://www.cornell.edu/)).

Geneva is close to the primary fruit and vegetable production areas of New York State, and is also in the heart of the Finger Lakes wine region.
