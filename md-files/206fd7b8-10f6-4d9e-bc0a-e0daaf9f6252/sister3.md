5. Application Process

##### We are now accepting [online applications](https://app.smartsheet.com/b/form/d430af92a683468eb948aa6c2ee5b4ae "Cornell AgriTech Summer Scholar online application") for the 2026 program

###### The submission deadline for all application related material is as follows:

* Applications (including Statement of Interest) - due date February 2, 2026
* Unofficial Transcripts - due date February 2, 2026  
  **Please Note: After clicking submit on your application you will receive one email to complete your personal statement and submit your unofficial transcript.**
* Letters of Recommendation - due date February 9, 2026
  + **It is very important that you inform your referees which projects you are applying for so they can be specific in their endorsement as to your skills and attributes for your selection.**

###### If you have questions about the application process, please contact:

* Our Program: agritech\_scholar [at] cornell.edu (agritech\_scholar[at]cornell[dot]edu)

###### If you have department specific questions, please contact:

* For Entomology: Holly Hancharik - hak3 [at] cornell.edu (hak3[at]cornell[dot]edu)
* For Horticulture: Amy Andersen- ada10 [at] cornell.edu (ada10[at]cornell[dot]edu)
* For Plant Pathology/Plant Microbe-Biology: Christine Dean - cad295 [at] cornell.edu (cad295[at]cornell[dot]edu)
* For Food Science: Sarah Lincoln - sjl38 [at] cornell.edu (sjl38[at]cornell[dot]edu)
* For Food & Beverage Entrepreneurship and Business Development: Melissa Hegarty - mh2566 [at] cornell.edu (mh2566[at]cornell[dot]edu)

Only United States *citizens* or *permanent residents* are eligible for USDA NIFA funds in this program. Non-US citizens, especially Cornell CALS students, may be eligible for funding from other sources.
