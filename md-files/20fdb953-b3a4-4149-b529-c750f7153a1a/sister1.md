# Native American Science & Engineering Program Application

### **Our NASEP application is live!**

[Apply Here](https://slate.admissions.arizona.edu/portal/apply-nasep)

### **Summer Dates:** June 7-13, 2026 at the University of Arizona - Tucson, AZ

---

Using Dr. Shawn Secatero’s “American Indian Well-Being Model in Higher Education,” NASEP provides activities aimed at holistically developing students. Program highlights have included an excursion to Biosphere II, networking with indigenous professionals from IBM and Raytheon, and building a computer “from scratch.”

* **Program dates:** June 7-13, 2026
* **Eligibility:**
  + Rising High School Juniors & Seniors
  + Enrolled Member or Descendant of AI/AN or Native Hawaiian Tribe
  + Completing Physics, Chemistry, & Pre-Calculus with a “C” or better
  + Must attend the summer dates of June 7-13, 2026
  + Attend NASEP events throughout the year

Please contact us at [nasep@arizona.edu](mailto:nasep@arizona.edu) with any questions. Share widely. Thank you!

[Download our 2026 Flyer](https://nasep.arizona.edu/sites/default/files/2025-06/NASEP-2026-Flyer.pdf)
