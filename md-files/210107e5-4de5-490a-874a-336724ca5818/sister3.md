# High School Programs

High school students in grades 9th-12th can participate in the following programs: Upward Bound, Future Success Program, Talent Search, ACT test-taking and preparation, Upward Bound Math and Science and MPS GEAR UP Program. Programs are free to those who qualify.

**Note:** Some programs have target schools, and/or target areas that they serve. Please go to the drop-down menu at the top of the page to learn more about each of the programs mentioned above, services offered and contact information.
