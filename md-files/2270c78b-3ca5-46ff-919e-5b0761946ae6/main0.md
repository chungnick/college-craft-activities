# Join CReSIS for high-school summer camps

---

## STEM camp for high schoolers - totally free of charge

Through the ARISE grant, high school students will have the opportunity to study math, physics, aerospace engineering, and computer science with accomplished scientists and engineers. Students will gain valuable exposure to hands-on research while building a solid understanding of the principles behind classroom experience while living with their peers in campus dorms at the University of Kansas. Students who participate in these camps will be eligible for future scholarships and research opportunities.

Click the links below to learn more about these camps and apply:

### [AATEE camp, July 13-25](https://cresis.ku.edu/aatee)

### [LEAPES camp, June 22-27 (applications closed)](https://cresis.ku.edu/leapes)
