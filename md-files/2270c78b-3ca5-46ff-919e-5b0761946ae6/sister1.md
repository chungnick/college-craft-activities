# AATEE Camp at KU - July 13-25, 2025

---

## Applications are open!

This is a FREE camp for rising seniors interested in STEM!  
When: July 13-25, 2025  
Where: University of Kansas in Lawrence, KS

[Apply now](https://kusurvey.ca1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_4OyRFLqteaeJ250)[Apply now](https://kusurvey.ca1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_4OyRFLqteaeJ250)

---

---

---

## Activities include

* Instruction in applied physics
* Collaborative projects in real-world research environments, including autonomous race cars and drones
* Modern computer programming, such as Python and robot operating systems

* Introductory programing of open-source autopilot systems like NVIDIA’s Racer RTX, Dronecode, and ArduPilot, with actual validation experiments
* Career advice and an introduction to college life in beautiful Lawrence, KS
* Fun activities, including a day at Worlds of Fun and Oceans of Fun

## Explore aerospace with top engineers

The Advanced Aerospace Technologies Exploration Experience (AATEE) camp is a two-week experience for rising high-school seniors in rural Kansas who are interested in aerospace and computer science, organized by CReSIS at KU and REC at K-State as a part of the ARISE grant.

[More about CReSIS](https://cresis.ku.edu/)

[More about REC](https://coe.k-state.edu/academics/departments/rural-education-center//)

[More about the ARISE grant](https://cresis.ku.edu/arise)
