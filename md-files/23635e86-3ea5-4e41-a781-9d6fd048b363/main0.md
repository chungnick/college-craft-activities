## An unforgettable summer

Our Yale programme includes an unforgettable day in New York City, giving students the chance to soak up world-class art, iconic landmarks, buzzing streets, and the unmistakable energy that makes NYC one of the most exciting cities on the planet.

* Explore The Metropolitan Museum of Art’s legendary collections
* Walk through the greenery and skyline views of Central Park
* Stroll along Fifth Avenue for shopping and sightseeing
* Experience the bright lights and atmosphere of Times Square
* Visit Bryant Park and the New York Public Library

## Make connections to last a lifetime

Our social timetables is all about allowing students to make new friends and explore new ventures, surrounded by their historic setting. Students can expect creative workshops, group outings, relaxed welcome events, and trips exploring local culture in this atmospheric college town.

* Creative workshops that spark collaboration and fresh ideas
* Group outings to museums, galleries, and Yale faculty
* Welcome activities that help everyone connect from day one
* Journalism club, Debate society and grand final

## Enjoy your favourites, or try something new

Our sports programme allows friendly competition during down-time. Students can dive into football, badminton, classic lawn games, capture the flag, and our energetic park “Olympics,” held at the Payne Whitney Gym.

* Football matches and friendly tournaments
* Badminton and other fast-paced mini-games
* Traditional lawn games in a relaxed setting
* “Olympics” with lively challenges and team spirit

## End your summer in style

Graduation is the culmination of your efforts, bringing everyone together in a beautiful New Haven venue. It’s a chance to reflect on achievements, enjoy a special dinner, and end the programme with an upbeat celebration alongside new friends.

* Graduation ceremony held in an elegant New Haven location
* Recognition of accomplishments and memorable programme moments
* Celebration dinner shared with classmates and mentors
* A lively end-of-programme party to close the experience
* A joyful atmosphere to mark an inspiring summer
