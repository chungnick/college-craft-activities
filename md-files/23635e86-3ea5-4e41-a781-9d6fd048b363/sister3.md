## Three iconic universities. One unparalleled experience.

#### Yale University: Where Global Leaders Begin Their Journey

Live and study at Yale University's historic campus and experience the tradition and innovation which continue to shape global leaders.

* Founded in 1701, Yale is one of the world’s most prestigious Ivy League universities
* Alumni include five U.S. Presidents and global leaders across politics and business
* Consistently ranked among the top Ivy League universities globally

#### Visit Cambridge Massachusetts: home of Harvard University

Enjoy an exclusive, privately guided journey to Massachusetts, famous world-wide for its education system.

* Founded in 1636, Harvard is older than the United States
* Alumni include 8 U.S. Presidents and over 150 Nobel laureates
* Harvard Business School produces more FTSE 100 CEOs than any non-UK university

#### Columbia University: Learn in the Heart of New York City

Live and learn in Manhattan, at Columbia's classical campus which provides the perfect backdrop for exploring leadership.

* Founded in 1754, Columbia University is a world-leading Ivy League institution in New York City
* Alumni include Franklin D. Roosevelt, Ruth Bader Ginsburg, and Barack Obama
* Manhattan campus places students at the centre of global finance, politics, culture, and innovation

### Manuscript Library

Experience history at the Beinecke Rare Book & Manuscript Library, the literary archive of Yale University. One of the largest buildings dedicated to rare books, the library contains more than a million books — a book-lover’s dream!

### Museums & Galleries

Yale is a centre of culture with many fascinating museums and galleries to explore. The Knights of Columbus Museum, the New Haven Museum, and the New Haven Art Museum for a taste of Yale’s history.
