# Application

Please use the following link to access the 2025-2026 Application. The application is due September 26th at 5 PM.

<https://harvard.az1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_5sYOI2QHtsN6LYy>

Our virtual Student Information Session will take place on September 17th, 2025 at 7 PM via zoom

zoom link: <https://tinyurl.com/HPREP-Info-2025>

Password: HPREP

---

**Application Eligibility:**

* Must be a high school student (9th-12th grade) who is able to make the commitment to attend the listed program dates.
* Students who have previously completed the HPREP program are **not** eligible to reapply.
* We encourage students who were not admitted in previous years to reapply in subsequent years.
* We are committed to serving local students and request that applicants attend schools in the greater Boston area.

**2025-2026 Program Dates:**November 1, November 8, November 15, December 6, December 13, January 10, January 17, January 24, January 31, February 7

**Application criteria:** Our goal is to identify high school students who face roadblocks in entering the fields of science or medicine, who are passionate about exploring health and science and who will gain the most from the HPREP experience. We seek to create “a level playing field” in our consideration of your application. We understand that our applicants come from different backgrounds, grade levels, study interests, and life experiences. Therefore, we take into consideration all aspects of your application in order to make holistic admissions decisions.

**You can email us at hprep@hms.harvard.edu with any questions, or view our [FAQ](https://hprep.wordpress.com/faqs/) page to see if your question has been answered.**

**Application-related FAQs**

**Where should the essay questions be submitted?**  
You will complete the essay questions within the application linked above.

**When are the essay questions due?**  
The essay questions are due with the application, on September 26th, 4:59 PM. As you fill out the application, you will encounter a section that allows you to submit your responses to the essay questions.

**Is there a word limit for the essays?**  
Depending on the question, the limit ranges from 50-500 words.

**When will I hear back about whether I am admitted?**  
You should hear back by email in early October. Please check your email frequently and check the spam folder.

For current graduate, medical, and dental students, please review the information in our [Volunteer](https://hprep.wordpress.com/support/volunteer/) section if you are interested in getting involved.
