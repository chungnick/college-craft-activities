#### Cost

The 2026 cost for the program is $600. This amount covers all programming costs and room and board. Payment is due after acceptance to the program. You will be notified of acceptance.

#### Scholarships

The Conservation Districts in each county will often sponsor participants as well. Call [Mark Davis](mailto:MarkJ.Davis@ky.gov) with the Kentucky Division of Conservation at 502-782-6594 for more information.
