### Student Interns

[Eligible students](https://www.saturdayacademy.org/ase-eligibility) can...

* Apply for an internship
* Attend free professional development workshops
* Interview with mentor organizations
* Participate in Orientation, Midsummer Conference & [Symposium](https://www.saturdayacademy.org/ase-symposium-presentations-2023)
* Contribute to one or more projects for the mentor organization
* Write an internship summary & thank you letters
* Receive high school credit
* **Read more about the internship in the [ASE Experience](https://www.saturdayacademy.org/ase-faq)**
