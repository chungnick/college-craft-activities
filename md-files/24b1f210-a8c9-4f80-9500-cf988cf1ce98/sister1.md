## What is UB Summer Academy?

Calling all our currently enrolled UB high school students ready to embark on an incredible journey of self-discovery and growth! Our Summer Academy will equip you with the tools, experiences, and friendships that will propel you towards a bright future. Summer Academy is a unique experience where students make lifelong friends and experience SDSU, and greater San Diego, having a blast while developing crucial skills. ## Summer Academy 2025

### Embarking on a Journey, Navigating an Everchanging World

In July 2025, students were immersed in an enriching, exciting summer camp program designed to give young scholars an opportunity to experience college life. With both remote instruction and on-campus components, rising 10th-12th graders stayed in freshman dorms at SDSU, attended lectures, and completed research projects reflective of a public health concern in their community. Participants in Summer Academy not only became masters of a topic of their choosing, they also learned important skills to guide them towards their post-secondary education goals. This year, fun activities and field trips included a trip to a Padres game, an evening in Balboa Park touring museums, on-campus pool days and movie nights, to name a few.
