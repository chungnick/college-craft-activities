# VPSE Science Technology Engineering Math (STEM)

[PennVPSE Home](https://vpse.upenn.edu/ "PennVPSE Home")VPSE Science Technology Engineering Math (STEM)

## 

The VPSE STEM Academy provides K-12 students from Philadelphia with unique opportunities to engage in innovative STEM projects, develop essential skills in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, and collaborate on hands-on learning experiences.
