# General Tuition and Fee Information

Ohio's only "Best Buy" according to Fiske Guide to Colleges 2018

Ohio University is committed to providing an affordable higher education option for students. The OHIO Guarantee ensures that tuition and fees for undergraduate students remain unchanged for four academic years at Ohio University. In-state and out-of-state tuition costs are an estimate only and should not be used for billing purposes.

[Ohio University Tuition & Fees](https://www.ohio.edu/bursar/tuition-fees)

## OHIO Guarantee

The [OHIO Guarantee](https://www.ohio.edu/guarantee) is designed to help you budget for college. At Ohio University tuition, housing, dining, and fee rates established at students enrollment remain unchanged for 12 consecutive semesters (four years). This includes all [commonly assessed fees](https://www.ohio.edu/guarantee/fixed-tuition-and-fees) such as the general fee, technology fee, etc. Learn more about the OHIO Guarantee and what it means to you.

For more detailed regarding tuition and program costs, visit the [Bursar’s Office website](https://www.ohio.edu/bursar/tuition-fees) or call [740.593.4130](tel:7405934130).

[OHIO Guarantee](https://www.ohio.edu/guarantee)
