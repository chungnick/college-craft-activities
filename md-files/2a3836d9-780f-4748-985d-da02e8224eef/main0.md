## WHAT ARE SIBLING CIRCLES?

Sibling Circles is a drop-in workshop series focused on mental health and well-being. Through educational healing spaces, participants process life events, engage in open discussion and creative expression, and build skills to navigate the world in ways that uphold the importance of safety and uplift the power of collective care.

## WHO SHOULD APPLY?

Young women and gender-expansive youth ages 14-22 who are currently, or have previously, participated in an SNLP program. There are no academic or skill criteria for admission into our programs.

## HOW DO YOU APPLY?

Sibling Circles are offered to current and previous Nashers only. The availability of workshops is shared directly with program participants.

## MORE DETAILS

* Sibling Circles **runs year round from July to May** with sign-ups happening each spring, summer, and fall.
* **Weekly sessions take place Monday - Saturday afternoons** depending on our Wellness Team’s availability
* Workshops are mostly **virtual and led by Bachelor and Masters of Social Work students from NYC and New Jersey universities**

* ## SOCIO-EMOTIONAL SKILL BUILDING

  Learn to recognize and manage emotions and build healthy relationships friends and family
* ## MIND & BODY CONNECTION

  Explore the benefits of meditation and positive self-talk and practice holding space for yourself and others
* ## VALUES EXPLORATION

  Examine the values that matter most to you and discuss what that means for the decisions you make everyday, both big and small.
