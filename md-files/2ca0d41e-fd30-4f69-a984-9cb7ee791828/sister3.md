### **Important Registration Announcement**

To register your child for KTU Summer Camps, please click the **"Enroll Yourself"** button and fill out the form using **your child's name** but **your contact information**. This ensures you are listed as the primary contact.
  
Need assistance? You can also register over the phone at **[817-272-2581](tel:8172722581)**.

  
Set sail on an interstellar journey at our Dungeons & Dragons Camp! This immersive camp invites young storytellers and adventurers to explore the imaginative world of Dungeons & Dragons. Participants will learn the basics of gameplay and storytelling as they embark on thrilling quests across the Astral Sea, encountering dragons, wizards, and mythical creatures. Using pre-generated characters, campers can dive straight into the action while developing valuable skills such as teamwork, creativity, and critical thinking through collaborative play.
  

*KTU follows the 2025–2026 academic calendar for grade level placement.*
