# Cost of Attendance

## Commuter and Residential Options

Local-area students who choose to commute are expected to live near UCSB and have reliable transportation to campus each day, including weekends. Residential students live in the Santa Cruz residence hall with other pre-college students. They enjoy meals in the campus dining commons and partake in a wide variety of supervised social activities outside of their academic experience. For more details on the commuter and residential options, please explore [Student Life](/programs/research-mentorship-program/student-life).

## 2026 Program Cost Breakdown

## Commuter Option

* $75Application Fee (non-refundable; due at time of submission)
* $1,000 Enrollment Deposit (non-refundable; applied to the Tuition and Program Fees)
* $5,600 Tuition and Program Fees
* **= $5,675 Total**

## Residential Option

* $75Application Fee (non-refundable; due at time of submission)
* $1,000 Enrollment Deposit (non-refundable; applied to the Tuition and Program Fees)
* $5,600 Tuition and Program Fees
* $7,599 Housing and Meal Fees (These fees apply to residential students only, and are collected by Summer Discovery. Visit the link for details regarding fees and payment schedule.)
* **= $13,274 Total**

[Fee Payment and Deadlines](#collapse-5964)

The **application fee** is due at submission of application. This fee is non-refundable.

The **enrollment deposit** is due at time of acceptance confirmation. Enrollment deposits are applied to the tuition and program fees. This fee is non-refundable.

The remainder of the **tuition and program fees** are due on or before **May 8, 2026**.

Summer Discovery collects fees for housing and meals. Residential students should contact them directly for information.

You will be given the option to complete your tuition payment by credit card or eCheck. A 2.75% convenience fee will apply when paying by credit card. This fee is charged by our payment processor. Transactions completed by eCheck do not incur a processing fee but will require that you provide your bank routing number and account number.

[Withdrawal & Refunds](#collapse-712)

To withdraw, the student must notify pre-college staff via email ([precollege@summer.ucsb.edu](mailto: precollege@summer.ucsb.edu)) prior to the start of the program. Refunds cannot be considered until a formal email request is received.

Application fees and enrollment deposits are non-refundable. The remaining tuition and program fees are refundable according to the following deadlines:

Full refund through **May 15, 2026**

50% refund through **May 22, 2026**

No refund after **May 22, 2026**

## Financial Aid

A limited number of need-based scholarships are available and awarded based on qualifications and availability. Priority is given to California residents. If you are interested in applying for a scholarship, please indicate your interest within your program application. Your guardian will receive an email to complete the scholarship application if you are admitted into the program.
