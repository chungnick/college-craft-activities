## Get revved up for college.

Please read the following admissions information before starting your online application. You may be contacted for an audition or additional application materials. You can find important dates on the [Tisch Special Programs Admissions Calendar](/special-programs/admissions/admissions-calendar).

### How do I apply?

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Step 1** | **[Confirm Your Eligibility](#step-1)** |
| **Step 2** | **[Prepare Your Application](#step-2)** |
| **Step 3** | **[Submit Your Application](#step-3)** |
| **Step 4** | **[Review Your Admissions Decision](#step-4)** |

-

### [STEP 1: CONFIRM YOUR ELIGIBILITY](#)

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **REQUIREMENT** | **CRITERIA** |
| **Academics** | * You must a current freshman, sophomore or junior in high school.  * High school seniors are not eligible.  * There is no minimum GPA requirement.  * You cannot apply to a track you have previously participated in. |
| **Committment** | * This program requires a 14-week commitment on Saturdays, 9 a.m. to 6 p.m. ET (hours vary by department but will fall within this time).  * You must be available for the entire duration of the program. Students who miss class for any reason may be dismissed from the program.  * This a collaborative, rigorous, and competitive program so your presence is keenly felt. You should arrive ready to absorb, challenge, and internalize what is being offered. |

-

### [STEP 2: PREPARE YOUR APPLICATION](#)

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Online Application** | * You must submit an online application form.  * Please type your name exactly as it appears on your birth certificate/passport in your application and supporting documents. |
| **Written Responses** | * You must answer 2 questions. The prompt for Part A is the same for all tracks, and the prompt for Part B is specific for your chosen track (i.e. Dancers, Filmmakers, etc.) |
| **Creative Resume** | * This résumé should showcase your training, awards, projects, community service, and work experience, whether in or outside of school.  * You may also include URL links to your online media (i.e. video, music, photography, etc.)  * Please check [NYU’s Visual Guide](https://tisch.nyu.edu/content/dam/tisch/student-affairs/CareerDevelopment/CareerDocs/Tisch%20Resume%20Guide%20%26%20Samples.pdf) to a successful résumé. |
| **Transcript** | * Upload a copy of your transcript in PDF format. Your transcript must display your full name, school/institution, courses, and grades for **every year that you have been in high school.**  * **Homeschooled Applicants:** Please have your parent/guardian in charge of your homeschooling provide a document detailing your coursework and academic achievement.  * If you have any difficulties providing a transcript, please contact <tisch.futureartists@nyu.edu> prior to the application deadline. |
| **English Language Proficiency** | * Required if English is not your native language.  * You are exempt from this requirement if (at the time of your application submission), you have completed three (3) or more full-time consecutive academic years in a curriculum where the only language of instruction was English.  * For more information on which exams we accept [click here](https://www.nyu.edu/admissions/undergraduate-admissions/how-to-apply/standardized-tests/english-language-testing.html). |
| **Recommendations** | * In the application portal, please enter the names and email addresses of one (1) recommender. Once you submit their information, they will be sent instructions via email. Please give your reference 1-2 weeks to complete their recommendation.  * Your recommendation should be from someone who is familiar with your interest and experience in the arts.  * We will only accept references from the following:   + High school representative   + Community group leader   + Mentor   + Coach |

-

### [STEP 3: SUBMIT YOUR APPLICATION](#)

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Application** | * Fill out the online application form [here](https://connect.nyu.edu/register/?id=530cd02c-91c5-4a1e-bc3c-7f882d6f25ed).  * After submitting the form, you will receive an email with next steps to finalize your account and complete you application. |
| **Deadline** | * All applications must be completed and submitted by the application deadline.  * Please visit our [Admissions Calendar](https://tisch.nyu.edu/special-programs/admissions/admissions-calendar) to keep track of important dates and deadlines. |

-

### [STEP 4: REVIEW YOUR ADMISSIONS DECISION](#)

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Notification** | * Final status notifications will be sent on the dates listed in our [Admissions Calendar](https://tisch.nyu.edu/special-programs/admissions/admissions-calendar).  * Notifications are communicated via email to the primary email address used for the application. |
| **Confirmation** | * If accepted, you will be required to confirm or decline your participation.  * Please confirm your participation by the deadline found in your acceptance letter. |

### APPLICATION POLICIES

###### 

###### **AI USAGE**

* To make sure your application truly represents you, we strongly recommend against using AI tools.

* Your application gives us a chance to learn about you; we truly value hearing about your unique experiences, ideas, and goals.

* Please share your genuine self and let your personal story make your application stand out.

[Apply](https://connect.nyu.edu/register/?id=530cd02c-91c5-4a1e-bc3c-7f882d6f25ed)

If you are having difficulty with the online application, please contact us at [tisch.futureartists@nyu.edu](mailto:Tisch.futureartists@nyu.edu) or 212-998-1899.
