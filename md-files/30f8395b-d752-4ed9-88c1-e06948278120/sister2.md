# Dates & Deadlines

## Important 2026 Dates for Summer Edge Students

**April 1**: Registration opens for Summer Edge.

**May 1**: Summer course enrollment opens for registered students ([see add/swap deadlines for each session](https://summer.ucsc.edu/summer-student-guide/academic-calendar/)).

**TBD**: [UC SHIP Waiver](https://healthcenter.ucsc.edu/billing-insurance/index.html#waive) opens for students enrolled in summer.

**June 15**: Registration closes for Summer Edge.

**June 22**: Session 1, 8-week and 10-week classes begin.

**June 30**: Deadline to submit 2025-2026 FAFSA or Dream App for summer aid (this is in addition to the 2026-2027 app submitted for fall).

**TBD**: Deadline to submit [UC SHIP Waiver](https://healthcenter.ucsc.edu/billing-insurance/index.html#waive) for summer quarter.

**TBD**: Late Waiver Period for UC SHIP. (Approved waivers must pay a late fee).

**July 24**: Move-in Date for students who are living on campus for the Edge Residential Experience.

**July 27**: Session 2 classes begin.

[See Summer Academic Dates and Deadlines](https://summer.ucsc.edu/summer-student-guide/academic-calendar/)
