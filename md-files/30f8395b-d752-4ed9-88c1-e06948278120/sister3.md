## High school student: One 5-credit course

These estimates are for a 5-week, 5-credit program.

| Type of Expense | Cost\* |
| --- | --- |
| Tuition for 5 Credits | $1,395 *($279 per credit)* |
| Campus Fee | $344.90 |
| **Total** | **$1,739.90** |

Cost estimates are for a 5-week, 5-credit program.

*\*Estimates are based on 2025 costs.*

## High school student: Two 5-credit courses

These estimates are for a 10-credit program.

| Type of Expense | Cost\* |
| --- | --- |
| Tuition for 10 Credits | $2,790 *($279 per credit)* |
| Campus Fee | $344.90 |
| **Total** | **$3,134.90** |

Cost estimates for a 10-credit program.

*\*Estimates are based on 2025 costs.*

To calculate your tuition for more than 10 credits, multiply the total number of credits x the cost per credit and then add the Campus Fee.

## Financial aid & scholarships

* UC Santa Cruz Financial Aid or Scholarships are not available for high school students
* Some companies specialize in high school student scholarships, including  [scholarships.com](https://www.scholarships.com/financial-aid/college-scholarships/scholarships-by-type/scholarships-for-summer/)

Tuition and fees have been set regardless of the method of instruction and will not be refunded in the event instruction occurs remotely for any part of the Academic Year.  Figures for tuition and fees represent currently approved amounts and may not be final.  Actual tuition and fees are subject to change by the University of California as determined to be necessary or appropriate.  Final approved tuition and fee levels may differ from the amounts presented.
