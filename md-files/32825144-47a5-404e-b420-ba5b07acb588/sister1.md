# Summer Lab and Research Experiences

* [Educational Outreach Overview](https://pme.uchicago.edu/academics/educational-outreach)
* [K-12 Educational Outreach Programs](https://pme.uchicago.edu/educational-outreach/k-12-educational-outreach-programs)
* [Summer Lab and Research Experiences](https://pme.uchicago.edu/educational-outreach/summer-lab-and-research-experiences)
* [Public Outreach Events](https://pme.uchicago.edu/educational-outreach/educational-outreach-public-outreach-programs)

The UChicago PME hosts a range of summer programs where participants can engage in authentic, immersive, and intensive mentored research experiences. Whether you are an undergraduate student looking to develop or advance your research skills, a community college student interested in exploring bachelors’ degrees in STEM, a high school teacher wanting to learning about emerging quantum technologies and their applications, or a high school student ready to step into a real-world research environment, these programs will provide the structured and formal mentorship to support your learning.

* [UChicago PME Research Experience for Undergraduates (REU)](https://pme.uchicago.edu/educational-outreach/research-experiences-undergraduates)
* [UChicago PME City Colleges of Chicago Summer Program](https://pme.uchicago.edu/educational-outreach/pme-ccc-summer-program)
* [After School Matters STEM Lab Internship](https://pme.uchicago.edu/educational-outreach/after-school-matters)
* [TeachQuantum](https://pme.uchicago.edu/educational-outreach/teachquantum)
