# 2026 Summer Journalism at NYU Courses

### 

**Please note**: ***There are no prerequisites for any of these courses except for The Beat and** **Journalistic Inquiry** **courses.*** If you are a visiting student and are interested in taking one of these courses, please email [journalism.summer@nyu.edu](mailto:journalism.summer@nyu.edu) about assessing your previous journalism experience and whether the prerequisite can be waived.

*Course information subject to change. Please check back frequently for updates. To view a course description and syllabus, click on each course’s title. All courses are 4 credits unless otherwise noted.*

**Special Session Toolkits:** Four weeks, mostly contained in the months of June or July  
**Session 1:** May 18 – June 30, 2026  
**Session 2:** July 1 – August 12, 2026
