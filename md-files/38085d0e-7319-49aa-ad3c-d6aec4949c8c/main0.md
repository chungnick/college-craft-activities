**Terp Young Scholars**

This summer, join the University of Maryland’s Terp Young Scholars program for a transformative pre-college experience. In just three weeks, high school students (rising grade 10 to graduating grade 12) will have the opportunity to challenge their academic curiosity, connect with faculty, and earn three university credits.

Flexible Learning Options:

* **Campus**: Maximize the daily experience of UMD’s exceptional campus.
* **Online**: Engage in interactive virtual classes from anywhere.
