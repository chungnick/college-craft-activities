# Online High School Workshops Application Requirements

* [Online High School Workshops Application FAQs](/special-programs/admissions/tisch-online-high-school-workshops-application-faqs)
* [Connect With Us](/special-programs/contact-us)
* [SMS/Live Chat](/special-programs/contact-us/sms-live-chat)

Please read the following admissions information before starting your online application. You can find important dates on the [Tisch Special Programs Admissions Calendar](https://tisch.nyu.edu/special-programs/admissions/admissions-calendar).

### How do I apply?

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Step 1** | **[Confirm Your Eligibility](#step-1)** |
| **Step 2** | **[Prepare Your Application](#step-2)** |
| **Step 3** | **[Submit your Appplication](#step-3)** |
| **Step 4** | **[Review Your Admissions Decision](#step-4)** |

-

### [STEP 1: CONFIRM YOUR ELIGIBILITY](#)

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **REQUIREMENT** | **CRITERIA** |
| **Academics** | * You must be a current high school student (freshman, sophomore, junior, or senior) at the time of your application.  * Minimum 3.0 cumulative GPA on a 4.0 unweighted scale. |
| **Technology** | * Students are required to supply their own technology for this program.  * Students must use a desktop or laptop computer with a webcam, microphone, and audio capability.  * This online course works best with a fast, reliable internet connection, and requires Chrome web browser. |

-

### [STEP 2: PREPARE YOUR APPLICATION](#)

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Written Responses** | * You must answer 3 questions in addition to any program-specific prompts.  * Each repsonse must have a maximum of 250 words. Be clear and succinct. |
| **Creative Résumé** | * This résumé should showcase your training, awards, projects, community service, and work experience, whether in or outside of school.  * You may also include URL hyperlinks to your online media (i.e. video, music, photography, etc.)  * Please check [NYU’s Visual Guide](https://tisch.nyu.edu/content/dam/tisch/student-affairs/CareerDevelopment/CareerDocs/Tisch%20Resume%20Guide%20%26%20Samples.pdf) to a successful résumé. |
| **Transcript** | * Upload a copy of your transcript in PDF format. Your transcript must display your full name, school/institution, courses, and grades for **every semester/year that you have been in high school**.  * **Homeschooled Applicants:** Please have your parent/guardian in charge of your homeschooling email a document detailing your coursework and academic achievement in to [tisch.special.admissions@nyu.edu](mailto:tisch.special.admissions@nyu.edu).  * **Non-US/Non-English Transcripts:** You must submit your official transcript AND its official English translation. Please upload both documents in PDF format. |
| **English Language Proficiency** | * Required if English is not your native language.  * You are exempt from this requirement if (at the time of your application submission), you have completed three (3) or more full-time consecutive academic years in a curriculum where the only language of instruction was English.  * For more information on which exams we accept [click here](https://www.nyu.edu/admissions/undergraduate-admissions/how-to-apply/standardized-tests/english-language-testing.html). |

-

### [STEP 3: SUBMIT YOUR APPLICATION](#)

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Program Application** | * You can access our application on [Slideroom](https://tischspecialprograms.slideroom.com/#/login).  * You must complete all required fields AND fill out the **NYU Tisch Supplemental Profile Form**.  * There is a $75 non-refundable application fee\* |
| **Scholarship Application**  (Summer Only) | * Tisch has a limited number of scholarships for the Summer Online Workshops. Scholarships are given to students who demonstrate the most financial need.  * A link to the online scholarship application will be emailed to you once you submit a complete admissions application and supplemental profile form.  * More information about tuition and scholarships can be found [here](https://tisch.nyu.edu/special-programs/online-course/online-high-school-workshops-tuition-and-scholarships). |
| **Deadline** | * All applications must be completed and submitted by the application deadline.  * Please visit our [Admissions Calendar](https://tisch.nyu.edu/special-programs/admissions/admissions-calendar) to keep track of important dates and deadlines. |

*\*Need-based application fee waivers are available. Applicants can request a fee waiver by sending an email with an explanation of financial hardship to [tisch.special.admissions@nyu.edu](mailto:tisch.special.admissions@nyu.edu).*

-

### [STEP 4: REVIEW YOUR ADMISSIONS DECISION](#)

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Notification** | * Final status notifications will be sent on the dates listed in our [Admissions Calendar](https://tisch.nyu.edu/special-programs/admissions/admissions-calendar).  * Notifications are communicated via email to the primary email address used for the application. |
| **Confirmation & Cancellation** | * If accepted, you will be required to confirm or decline your participation.  * To confirm for Summer Online Workshops, you must submit a non-refundable and non-transferable deposit of $1,500 (USD). Deposits must be received by the confirmation deadline indicated in your acceptance letter.  * To confirm for Fall Online Workshops, you must pay the tuition in full (see below).  * If you do not confirm your participation by the deadline, your spot will be rescinded.  * It is important that you review and understand the Tisch Special Programs Cancellation Policy prior to submitting your confirmation form. Details can be found on the [Tisch Special Programs Cancellation Policy](/special-programs/admissions/cancellation-policy) page. |
| **Payment** | * Payments are made via **[NYU eSuite](https://www.nyu.edu/students/student-information-and-resources/bills-payments-and-refunds/billing-and-payment-due-dates/student-billing.html)**, NYU's eCheck system.  * International students must confirm your participation by paying via [Flywire](https://www.nyu.edu/students/student-information-and-resources/bills-payments-and-refunds/methods-of-payment/international-payments.html), NYU's wire transfer system. Instructions for third-party payments are available on the [NYU Bursar's website](http://www.nyu.edu/students/student-information-and-resources/bills-payments-and-refunds.html).  * For international students paying via Flywire, please send a copy of your receipt to [tisch.special.admissions@nyu.edu](/special-programs/admissions/tisch.special.admissions@nyu.edu) |

### APPLICATION POLICIES

###### 

###### **GUIDELINES**

* Please see our [Application FAQ](https://tisch.nyu.edu/special-programs/admissions/tisch-online-high-school-workshops-application-faqs) for more information about the Tisch Online High School Workshops and application process.

###### **AI USAGE**

* To make sure your application truly represents you, we strongly recommend against using AI tools.

* Your application gives us a chance to learn about you; we truly value hearing about your unique experiences, ideas, and goals.

* Please share your genuine self and let your personal story make your application stand out.

If you are having difficulty with the online application, please contact us at [tisch.special.admissions@nyu.edu](/special-programs/admissions/tisch.special.admissions@nyu.edu) or (212) 998-1512.
