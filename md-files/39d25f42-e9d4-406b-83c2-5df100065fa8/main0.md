Are you a high school student interested in learning more about high-tech opportunities in healthcare?

The Buffalo Healthcare Exploration learning experience — offered through the Roswell Park ATLAS program — is perfect for you!

We offer the chance to learn and innovate in a fast-paced medical environment, Roswell Park Comprehensive Cancer Center, learning from experts in their fields.

## Program highlights

The Buffalo Healthcare Exploration (BHE) program at Roswell Park Comprehensive Cancer Center through the [ATLAS Program](/education/professional-training/atlas-program) offers an exceptional opportunity for high school students to delve into the exciting world of healthcare. Through hands-on experiences and mentorship, BHE equips young minds with valuable insights into various medical disciplines, fostering their passion and interest in the healthcare field.

**The Buffalo Health Exploration Program has six core pillars:**

* Innovation
* Compassion for Community
* Empathy

* Visualization of Complex Concepts
* Education
* Career Path Understanding

Aspiring healthcare professionals can benefit greatly from the program's comprehensive curriculum and guidance from skilled mentors.

[Join us today!](https://redcapweb.roswellpark.org/redcap/surveys/?s=YJX49MJ8L7XNMMR7) Embrace this transformative journey, broaden your horizons, and pave the way for a successful and fulfilling career in the healthcare industry. Discover your potential and make a positive impact on your future and the lives of countless others.

## Frequently asked questions

#### Am I eligible for this program?

Current high school students grades 9-11 are eligible to apply for our BHE program. If you are graduating this year, please consider joining us as a TA!

#### When do I apply?

Applications for summer programs open in November 24, 2025 and close January 26, 2026*.*

#### When will the program take place?

The BHE Program will take place over three weeks:

**Week 1** - Monday, July 27 - Wednesday, July 29, 2026  
**Week 2** - Monday, August 3 - Wednesday, August 5, 2026  
**Week 3** - Monday, August 10 - Wednesday August 12, 2026

#### What is the cost of the program?

The program costs $400, but full scholarships are available for anyone who currently qualifies for the National School Lunch Program. Full scholarship information can be found in the online application.

#### Where can I get more information or updates on the program?

If you have any questions about the program, please do not hesitate to contact Nancy Preskop at [Nancy.Preskop@RoswellPark.org](mailto:Nancy.Preskop@RoswellPark.org)

[Apply here](https://redcapweb.roswellpark.org/redcap/surveys/?s=YJX49MJ8L7XNMMR7)
