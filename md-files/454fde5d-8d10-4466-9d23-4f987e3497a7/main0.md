### Competition

# The 14th Annual Art Competition on Extreme Weather

2018-2019 David Lustick Award Winner, Lucas. A 2nd grader from Winthrop, MA.

## Challenging youth to express their learning about science concepts through the visual arts!

* ## Elementary (K-5), Middle School (6-8), and High School (9-12) winners are selected.
* ## Winning artwork is displayed on public transit!
* ## Winners receive a $50 gift card.
* ## Submitted Artwork is displayed at Community Art Exhibition Celebrations!
* ## Online resources!
* ## Artwork can be submitted by mail or electronically!

  ## 

  ## The 2025-2026 Challenge Questions are:

  ## 1.What can we learn from nature about adapting to a changing climate?

  ## 2.How are Massachusetts and its cities and towns preparing for climate change?

  ## 3.How can we lower temperatures in urban heat islands?
