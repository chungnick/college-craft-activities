# How to Apply

### **Information for Graduate Students and Postdoctoral Researchers**

To submit an application for participating in the TRC, please email Christoph Schwingshackl (c.schwingshackl@imperial.ac.uk) a copy of your CV, a letter of recommendation by your advisor including a statement that your home institution covers your travel expenses, and a cover letter detailing your research interests and project preference. Applications are due by March 15, 2024. For more information, please contact a steering committee member.
