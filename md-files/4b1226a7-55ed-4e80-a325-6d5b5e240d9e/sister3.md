**June 1 - August 21, 2026**

## Tuition and Fees

---

Students who register for a course in Summer Session are assessed tuition, mandatory fees, and, if applicable, course-specific fees. Applies to course section registration:  01\*\*, 02\*\*, 03\*\*, 04\*\*, 05\*\*, 06\*\*, WB\*\*, GCC\*, GCW\* I1\*\*, I2\*\*.  Other section numbers may have program-specific tuition and/or fees. Students should contact the specific program for those rates.

### Course Tuition

Tuition is assessed at the per-credit rate according to student type and residency.

#### Summer Session Undergraduate and Graduate Tuition and Fees

| Student Type | Residency Classification | Rate for 1 credit | Rate for 3 credits | Rate for 4 credits | Rate for 6 credits |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Undergraduate | MD In-State | $437.00 | $1,311.00 | $1,748.00 | $2,622.00 |
| Undergraduate | Out-of-State | $1,678.00 | $5,034.00 | $6,712.00 | $10,068.00 |
| Graduate | MD In-State | $878.00 | $2,634.00 | $3,512.00 | $5,268.00 |
| Graduate | Out-of-State | $1,878.00 | $5,634.00 | $7,512.00 | $11,268.00 |

#### Summer Session Doctoral Candidacy (899\*) Tuition and Fees

| Student Type | Residency Classification | Rate for 1 Credit | Rate for 2 or more Credits |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Doctoral Candidacy (899\*) | MD In-State | $878.00 | $1,432.00 |
| Doctoral Candidacy (899\*) | Out-of-State | $1,878.00 | $2,732.00 |

### College Park Summer Mandatory Fees

College Park mandatory fees are assessed according to student type and delivery.

| Student Type | Sections\* | Category | Flat Rate |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Undergraduate and Graduate | 01\*\*, 02\*\*, etc. | Summer Campus Services Fee | $229.70 |
| Undergraduate and Graduate | WB\*\*, I\*\*\* | Summer Distance Learning Fee | $108.70 |
| Golden ID | 01\*\*, 02\*\*, etc. | Golden ID Summer Campus Services Fee | $229.70 |
| Golden ID | WB\*\*, I\*\*\* | Golden ID Summer Distance Learning Fee | $108.70 |

\***Note**: For sections not listed here, contact your program.

* **Summer Campus Services Fee, Mandatory**: Flat rate charged for any course registration that requires students to come to campus. Assessed for each six-week session: Session I, including I-A or I-B combined; Session II, including II-C or II-D combined. Supports university resources that include transportation, student union, recreation, and registration services.
* **Summer Distance Learning Fee, Mandatory**: Flat rate charged for any course registration that does not require students to come to campus (e.g., internships, courses offered completely online). Assessed for each six-week session: Session I, including I-A or I-B combined; Session II, including II-C or II-D combined. Supports university resources and registration services.
* **Golden ID Summer Campus Services Fee, Mandatory**: Flat rate charged for any course registration that requires students to come to campus. Assessed for each six-week session: Session I, including I-A or I-B combined; Session II, including II-C or II-D combined. Supports university resources that include transportation, student union, recreation, and registration services.
* **Golden ID Summer Distance Learning Fee, Mandatory**:  Flat rate charged for any course registration that does not require students to come to campus (e.g., internships, courses offered completely online). Assessed for each six-week session: Session I, including I-A or I-B combined; Session II, including II-C or II-D combined. Supports university resources and registration services.
* **Mandatory Fees Note**: Students who register for a mix of courses that meet on campus and online/off-site are assessed the summer campus services fee.

### Universities at Shady Grove Summer Mandatory Fee

Shady Grove mandatory fees are assessed at the per credit hour for courses held at the Universities at Shady Grove. Applies to course section registration: 01Zx or 02Zx. If a student is enrolled in College Park campus sections in addition to the Shady Grove sections, then both sets of mandatory fees will be assessed.

| Student Type | Sections | Category | Rate per Credit Hour |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| All | 01Z\*, 02Z\* | Shady Grove Student Services Fee | $15.75 |

### Course-specific Fees

Some courses may charge a lab, studio, or materials fees. These fees should be noted on Testudo. The fee posts to the student account as a separate charge.

Notes on Tuition and Fees

* Determination of residency status is made according to the [University System of Maryland Policy for Student Classification for Admission and Tuition Purposes](https://registrar.umd.edu/residency-reclassification).
* To determine the maximum number of credits permitted for Summer Session registration, see [Maximum Load Policy](/current-incoming-former-umd-students/summer-session/university-policies#ssmaxload).
* Although changes in tuition, fees, and other charges ordinarily are announced in advance, the University reserves the right to make changes when deemed necessary.
