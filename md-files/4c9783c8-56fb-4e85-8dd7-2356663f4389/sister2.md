##### **INFORMATION FOR STUDENTS AND PARENTS**

**Basic Requirements and Expectations:**

* Have a minimum 3.7 GPA and be a high school junior or senior.
* Submit a cover letter and resume to [jgeiser@uga.edu](mailto:jgeiser@uga.edu). (templates for these documents are below).
* Students who are accepted into the program will complete a minimum of 120 internship
  hours  for each semester they are in the program.
* Each student will deliver an oral presentation at the end of each semester.
* There is no tuition cost to participate in the program.
