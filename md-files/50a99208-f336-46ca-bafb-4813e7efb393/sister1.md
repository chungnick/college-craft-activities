# Undergraduate Summer Research Experience (REU)

#### The application for the ASTERIX 2026 Summer Undergraduate Research Experience for Undergraduates at Florida International University will open soon.

The selected students will be immersed in a 10-week intensive research experience in the field of advanced sensors and systems. Participants will gain experience under the guidance of an established research group by investigating a topic from a broad spectrum of science and engineering disciplines.

In addition to their research work, participants will be able to attend professional development seminars, and workshops.

REU Applications are reviewed and matched to faculty mentors on a rolling basis, so it’s very important that you apply as soon as possible.

### Eligibility

To be eligible for the 2026 REU ASTERIX program you:

* Must be a U.S Citizen or permanent resident of the U.S
* Must be enrolled in a two year or four-year Engineering degree program.

#### Deadline:

The applications will open soon. 

##### Application:

To apply send your resume and a short essay (1 page maximum) explaining why you are interested in participating in ASTERIX’s Summer REU to:
