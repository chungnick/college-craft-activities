The Davidson College Summer Promise Program is annual free, week-long college readiness program for rising ninth graders.  

##### Upcoming Program Dates: June 21—June 27, 2026

Through this residential program, selected students experience a snapshot of the holistic college experience to strengthen their confidence and enthusiasm for persisting in high school and pursuing higher education.  

Students experience courses co-taught by Davidson faculty and teachers from the Charlotte-Mecklenburg Public Schools. 

Outside of class hours, the students engage with the many co-curricular experiences and facilities at Davidson College.

## Program Highlights

* The program is designed to increase college readiness and empower students to unlock their promise and potential to attend college, and pursue their dreams.
* Rising 9th-12th grade students will spend seven days in June on the beautiful Davidson College campus, experiencing a taste of residential college life.
* Family representatives will receive valuable information on the college admissions process, financial aid requirements, and scholarship opportunities.

## Key Dates

##### Returning Summer Promise Scholars

* **January 16:** Returner Application Deadline – if this date has passed, and you are still interested in returning to the program, please reach out to [summerpromise@davidson.edu](mailto:summerpromise@davidson.edu) at your earliest convenience.
* **April 6:** Information Update about Summer Promise Week
* **May 20:** Course Preferences Due

##### New/Prospective Summer Promise Scholars

* **March 15:** Application Priority Deadline
* **April 12:** Application Final Deadline – apply by 11:59 p.m.
* **May 1:** Admission Decisions Shared
* **May 20:** Course Preferences Due

Application dates vary between returning scholars and new scholars, but all other key dates remain the same:

* **June 21:** Move into residence hall by 2:30 p.m.
* **June 27:** Nurturing the Promise Session focused on preparing parents for supporting their scholar's journey to higher education from 9-11:30 a.m.
* **June 27:** Check out of residence hall by 2:30 p.m.
