#### **Welcome to CNAS Transfer Connections!**

###### CNAS Transfer Connections is dedicated to supporting transfer students as they navigate the academic and social landscape of UC Riverside’s College of Natural and Agricultural Sciences. Our programs and resources are designed to ensure a smooth transition, foster meaningful connections, and build a strong sense of community among transfer students. Through mentorship, academic support, and professional development opportunities, we empower students to thrive in their studies and achieve their career aspirations. We also engage in outreach and recruitment efforts, collaborating with California Community Colleges to assist incoming transfer students.

###### Join us to connect with peers, access valuable resources, and make the most of your journey at UCR!
