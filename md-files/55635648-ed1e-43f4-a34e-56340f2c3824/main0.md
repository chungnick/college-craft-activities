# Registration

## Eligibility

Berkeley Summer Sessions is open to a wide variety of students, including UC Berkeley degree-seeking students, students from other UC campuses, visiting students from around the U.S. and the world as well as the general public.

UC Berkeley students are eligible to enroll in Summer Sessions courses.

Eligibility for non-UC Berkeley students:

* Must be 18 years or older by the start of your courses
* Non U.S. citizens must meet the minimum [English Language Proficiency Requirement](https://summer.berkeley.edu/international/english-proficiency-requirement)
* Must complete the Summer Sessions application for access to enroll in summer courses (see below)

Pre-College Scholars Program

High school students who are 16 and 17 years old are eligible to apply via our [Pre-College Scholars Program](https://precollege.berkeley.edu/).Â *Please do not complete the general Summer Sessions application.*

## How to Apply

### [UC Berkeley Students](/student-types/uc-berkeley-students)

UC Berkeley students can add summer classes in [CalCentral](http://calcentral.berkeley.edu).

*Applications are closed for Summer 2025. Check back in December for the Summer 2026 class schedule.*

### [UC Students from Other Campuses](/student-types/visiting-uc-students)

UC students from other campuses add summer classes through our application portal.

*Applications are closed for Summer 2025. Check back in December for the Summer 2026 class schedule.*

### [Visiting Students, All Other Students & General Public](/student-types)

*There is a $25 application fee for non-UC students.*

*Applications are closed for Summer 2025. Check back in December for the Summer 2026 class schedule.*
