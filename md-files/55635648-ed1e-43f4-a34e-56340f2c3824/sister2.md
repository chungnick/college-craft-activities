# Summer Financial Aid and Loan

## Summer Financial Aid

**Are you a Berkeley student wondering if you're eligible for summer financial aid?** UC Berkeley
offers a range of programs designed to keep a world-class Berkeley education affordable - including financing
your summer.

* Visit the [Financial Aid & Scholarships website](http://financialaid.berkeley.edu/summer-aid)
  to learn more.
* Get an estimate of how much financial aid you might receive by logging into [CalCentral](https://calcentral.berkeley.edu/) and navigating to the Financial Resources under My
  Finances to select Summer Cost and Aid Estimator.

## Summer Sessions Loan

**The Summer Sessions loan now offers expanded eligibility for students enrolled in at least 6 units**in any single UC Berkeley summer session or combination of sessions, Berkeley Summer Abroad program,
and summer UC Education Abroad Program (UCEAP). Students who received the Middle Class Scholarship (MCS) may
qualify for this low-interest, subsidized loan.

* [Learn More about Summer Loans](http://financialaid.berkeley.edu/summer-sessions-loan)

Visiting students are not eligible for aid from UC Berkeley. Please speak to the financial aid office at your home
institution for advisement on financial resources.
