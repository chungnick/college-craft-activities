# Schedule

## 2026 Academic Calendar

Choose from seven different sessions this summer.

|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
|  | Start Date | End Date |
| A - First Six-Week Session | May 26 | July 2 |
| B - Ten-Week Session | June 8 | August 14 |
| C - Eight-Week Session | June 22 | August 14 |
| D - Second Six-Week Session | July 6 | August 14 |
| E - Three-Week Session | July 27 | August 14 |
| F - Three-Week Session | July 6 | July 24 |
| 12W - Twelve-Week Session | May 26 | August 14 |

## 2026 University Holidays

**Memorial Day:** Monday, May 25  
**Juneteenth:** Friday, June 19  
**Independence Day:** Friday, July 3  
*No classes will be held on the above holidays.*

## 2026 Deadlines

All deadlines are **11:59 pm Pacific Standard Time (PT)**  
Deadlines for [special programs](/special-programs) may be different than the deadlines below.

* [Application Deadlines](/apply) (UC Berkeley students do not submit an application but enroll in courses in [CalCentral](http://calcentral.berkeley.edu))
* [Visa Document Submission Deadlines](/apply) (only applicable to [international students](https://summer.berkeley.edu/visa))
* [Pre-College Scholars Program Deadlines](http://precollege.berkeley.edu)

### Enrollment Change Deadlines

|  |  |  |  |  |  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
|  | Session A | Session B | Session C | Session D | Session E | Session F | Session 12W |
| [Deadline to Cancel](/enrollment-changes/dropping-all-your-courses) *\*tuition and fees refunded* | May 25 | June 7 | June 21 | July 5 | July 26 | July 5 | May 25 |
| Deadline to Add Courses  Deadline to Drop Courses *\*tuition refunded, but not fees* | June 5 | June 18 | July 2 | July 17 | August 2 | July 12 | June 5 |
| [Change Grading Option](/enrollment-changes/changing-course-options) | June 18 | July 31 | July 31 | July 31 | August 7 | July 17 | July 31 |

† Students who wish to drop all classes in a session must submit a [Term Cancellation/Withdrawal Request](https://ssall.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041883393-Cancelling-or-Withdrawing) in CalCentral

‡ After the deadline to drop courses listed above, students can continue to drop/withdraw until the course end date. However, all fees are non-refundable, and [Summer Tuition Credit](https://summer.berkeley.edu/registration/refund) is available instead of a tuition refund.

§ UC Berkeley matriculated students who wish to drop a class after the deadline to drop courses should consult with their College Advisor, as deadlines may be different. Visiting students should consult with Summer Sessions.
