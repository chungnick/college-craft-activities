**PennState  @KleinLife** Interest & Scholarship form **Fall 2025 and Spring 2026**

In partnership with KleinLife, Penn State University – Abington is offering Dual Enrollment (college level) Courses at a significantly reduced cost, MATH Enrichment classes and after-school workshops in Entrepreneurship and STEM skills.

**Location**

KleinLife (JCC), 10100 Jamison Ave, Philadelphia, PA 19116

**Course Schedule**

College Level (Dual Enrollment Classes)

**Fall 2025**

**STAT 200**: Introductory Statistics. Tuesdays and Thursdays,  09/16 to 12/18, 6-8pm

**CMPSC 131**: Python-based Intro Computer Science. Mondays and Wednesdays, 09/22-12/17, 6-8pm 

**Eligibility:**Students with solid mastery of **Algebra I**and**II**are eligible to apply. Students who successfully completed or are currently enrolled in Penn State @KleinLife's **MATH 1** program are also eligible.

**Spring 2026**

**MATH 140**: Calculus I. Mondays (in person) and Wednesdays (online),  2/2 to 5/6/2026, 6-8pm

**PHYS 211**:  Calculus-based Physics/Mechanics.

                              In person on Tuesdays and Thursdays, 1/27-5/7/2026, 6-8pm 

**Eligibility:** High school **GPA of 3.0+** is required**.**Students with solid mastery of **Algebra II**and**Trigonometry (pre-calculus)**are eligible to apply. Students who successfully completed or are currently enrolled in Penn State @KleinLife's **MATH 2** program are also eligible.

**MATH Enrichment:**

**prep-MATH:** pre-Algebra. The class is designed for students in Grades 6-8 and prepares them for MATH 1. Class will run later in the year if there is enough students. Tentatively on Mondays and Wednesdays at 6-8pm. Please sign-up below if interested!

**MATH 1**: Algebra I. Classes meet on Mondays and Wednesdays starting on 10/15 , 4-6pm

**MATH 2**: Algebra II and Trigonometry. Classes meet on Tuesdays and Thursdays starting on 10/14 , 4-6pm

**Eligibility**

STEM workshops are open to any student with strong interest in STEM in 6th grade or older

Students with strong interest in MATHare invited to apply to the MATH Enrichment classes. The MATH level will be determined by a diagnostic test.

Students who successfully completed MATH Prep automatically qualify for MATH 1.

Students who successfully completed MATH 1 automatically qualify for MATH 2.

Students who successfully completed MATH 2 automatically qualify for college level classes.
