#### [Stanford Middle School Scholars Program](/programs/stanford-middle-school-scholars-program)

The Stanford Middle School Scholars Program prepares students for a rigorous high school education with a three-week online summer course, and weekly online after-school meetings. The program serves low-income students in grades 6 or 7 who reside in and attend school in the United States.

[Learn more](/programs/stanford-middle-school-scholars-program)

#### [Stanford AI4ALL](/programs/stanford-ai4all)

Stanford AI4ALL aims to expand access across the field of Artificial Intelligence. During this two-week residential or online program, students are immersed in AI through a combination of lectures, hands-on research projects, and mentoring activities.

[Learn more](/programs/stanford-ai4all)

### Who We Are

Stanford Pre-Collegiate Studies advances the education of academically motivated, intellectually curious, pre-college students. We offer a variety of programs designed to meet the needs of students everywhere.
