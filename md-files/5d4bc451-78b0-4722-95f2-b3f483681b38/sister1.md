# Eve and Gene Black Summer Medical Career Program

|  |
| --- |
| 1969-2026: The Summer Program still going strong at 57! |

|  |
| --- |
|  |

The Los Angeles Pediatric Society established the Eve and Gene Black Summer Medical Career Program in 1969 and is proud to celebrate its 57th year in 2026. The purpose of the program is to encourage high school students to choose careers in the health professions.

#### **The 2026 Application is OPEN**

This program is best described as a medical mentor program. Participants will attend daily virtual sessions or in-person programs with presentations/interactive sessions from a variety of general pediatricians, specialists, including surgeons, nurses, pharmacists, dietitians, infectious disease, ophthalmologists, radiologists, child life, andÂ  therapists, (i.e., physical, speech, occupational, or cognitive).

Each participant will receive a certificate of completion at the end of the program and one $500 scholarship will be awarded. Funding is provided by contributions from individuals and groups. If you would to donate to this life changing program, please donate below.
