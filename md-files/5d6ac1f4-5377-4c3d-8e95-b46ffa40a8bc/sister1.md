# Commuter Course Schedule

# (July 7 - July 25, 2025)

## 

## Weekday Schedule - Commuter Program

Students are required to attend all classes on time each day. Students may register for an Online SAT prep course held over Zoom.

Students may not be checked out of the program at any point during unless an emergency is presented. No additional exceptions will be made. 

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| 9:00 - 11:30 am | Class/Lecture/Lab |
| 11:30 - 1:30 pm | Lunch |
| 1:30 - 4:00 pm | Class/Lecture/Lab |
