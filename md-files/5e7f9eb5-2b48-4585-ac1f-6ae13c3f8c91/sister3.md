# SJV PRIME+ | BS to MD Pathway

The **San Joaquin Valley (SJV) PRIME+ BS/MD** opportunity is an educational pathway partnership between UC Merced, [UCSF Fresno](https://fresno.ucsf.edu/), and [UCSF](http://meded.ucsf.edu/san-joaquin-valley-prime-plus). This critical new pathway will educate and support the SJV's future physicians in advancing health equity and the health of communities in the SJV.

[SJV PRIME+](https://admissions.ucmerced.edu/SJVP-BStoMD) is a workforce development model for newly-incoming, first-time-freshman to become a doctor for the [San Joaquin Valley of California](https://sjvpremed.ucsf.edu/). An SJV PRIME+ scholar completing this medical education pathway receives their medical degree (MD) from [UCSF School of Medicine](https://meded.ucsf.edu/ucsf-san-joaquin-valley-prime-sjv-prime), after obtaining a [Bachelor of Science from UC Merced](https://admissions.ucmerced.edu/SJVP-BStoMD) and finishing medical training at [UCSF Fresno](https://fresno.ucsf.edu/sjv-prime).

[REQUEST INFO](https://ucmerced.my.salesforce-sites.com/admission/targetX_eventsb__formpage?formid=218243)/

### This is an eight-year pathway in which you will:

1. Earn a four-year baccalaureate degree in one of the four majors and options listed below at UC Merced

2. Complete first 1.5 years of [UCSF Bridges Curriculum](https://meded.ucsf.edu/bridges-curriculum) pre-clinical medical training at the UCSF School of Medicine Regional Pre-Clerkship Campus planned for UC Merced

3. Complete the remaining 2.5 years of [UCSF Bridges Curriculum](https://meded.ucsf.edu/bridges-curriculum) clinical training at the UCSF School of Medicine Regional Clinical Campus at UCSF Fresno and receive an MD degree from UCSF School of Medicine

### When enrolled in the SJV PRIME+ BS/MD pathway, you will earn a BS in one of four qualifying major options:

[Biological Sciences](https://www.ucmerced.edu/majors-and-minors/biological-sciences-bs)  
(Tracks: [Human Biology](https://catalog.ucmerced.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=11&poid=1243), [Molecular and Cell Biology](https://catalog.ucmerced.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=16&poid=1862&returnto=1562), and [Developmental Biology](https://catalog.ucmerced.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=17&poid=2028))

[Bioengineering](https://www.ucmerced.edu/majors-and-minors/bioengineering-bs)  
(Tracks: [Biotechnology](https://catalog.ucmerced.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=21&poid=2582&hl=Biotechnology&returnto=search), [Biomedical Imaging and Bioinstrumentation](https://catalog.ucmerced.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=21&poid=2581&hl=bioinstrumentation&returnto=search))

[Chemistry](https://www.ucmerced.edu/majors-and-minors/chemical-sciences-bs)  
(Track: [Biological Chemistry](https://catalog.ucmerced.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=16&poid=1885))

[Public Health](https://www.ucmerced.edu/majors-and-minors/public-health-ba)  
(Track: Pre-Med)

## Applying to SJV Prime+

Welcome to the SJV Prime+ application process! Designed to foster the next generation of healthcare professionals committed to serving the San Joaquin Valley, our pathway offers a unique opportunity for aspiring medical students. By joining SJV Prime+, you'll receive specialized training, mentorship, and support tailored to address the specific health needs of our vibrant community. Explore the following sections to learn more about the eligibility criteria, application requirements, and important deadlines.
