Interested in spending part of your summer at Stevens? Check out our one- and two-week residential non-credit programs that combine a fun and challenging academic experience with the excitement of campus living. There is no cost to apply — simply complete the online application.

**Cost**  
One Week Programs: $3,300  
Two Week Programs: $4,900

## Courses for College Credit

This program allows students to earn college credits while still in high school. Participants enroll in actual Stevens courses and work closely with professors, gaining valuable academic experience and a head start on their college education.
