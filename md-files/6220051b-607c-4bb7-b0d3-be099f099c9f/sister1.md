### Application Notes:

1. Applicants who submit early have the best selection of available courses. You will have priority access to enroll when registration opens.
2. Students who require an F-1 visa or who have international transcripts are strongly advised to apply by the Suggested International Deadlines. This date is suggested to ensure you have enough time for student visa processing. You may refer to [this website](https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/us-visas/visa-information-resources/wait-times.html) to review the current visa appointment wait times.
3. Completed applications are reviewed on a rolling basis.
4. If it is past the deadline and you have questions about the term, please contact summersession [at] columbia [dot] edu (summersession[at]columbia[dot]edu).

***Please note** the University is closed on Monday, May 25th in observance of the Memorial Day holiday. Friday, May 29th is the scheduled makeup date for Monday's classes.*
