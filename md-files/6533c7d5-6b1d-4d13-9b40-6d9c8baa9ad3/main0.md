## Fire Protection Discovery

Students going into 9th through 12th grades can take advantage of a great opportunity
and apply engineering principles to design systems for safe evacuations and to detect
and suppress a fire while using hands-on learning for “Burn to Learn - The Engineering
Systems to Keep it Safe”. Registration includes all accommodations, meals and supplies
for design projects, and a shirt. Seats are limited so be sure to register soon. Starts Monday at 1pm and Ends Friday at 1pm, 8am - 5pm each day with evening functions
and activities.
