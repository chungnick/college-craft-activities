**June 24  –  August 7**

**Requirements for Summer 2026 High School applicants:**

* All students must be, **at least 16 years old on, or****before July 4, 2026, no exceptions.**
* Have an unweighted GPA of 95/100 (3.8/4.0)
* **Must have at least 3 of the following subjects (Honors or AP/IB a plus but not required):**
* English
* Chemistry
* Mathematics or Calculus
* Physics
* Biology (optional)
* Standardized test scores (PSAT/SAT/ACT and/or Regents) must be 60th percentile or
  above.

+ Students who show evidence of leadership, special talents or interests, and other
  personal qualities through extracurricular activities, volunteer work, and other non-academic
  pursuits will receive special consideration.

**Along with the application form (Parts 1 and 2) you MUST  have submitted:**

* Transcript of grades (sent by your school)
* Three letters of recommendation, with one being from your Science teacher.
* Non-Refundable $50 Registration fee - Payable by check or wire transfer
  + Please make checks payable to "SUNY Stony Brook",  or include wire transfer receipt
    in your application submission. (Details for wire transfers and check mailing can
    be found in Part 2 of the application.)

[Application Link (Part 1)](https://stonybrookuniversity.co1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_aVmhwN1MAlE8DBA "Apply")

**Part 2, as well as submission of letters of recommendation and transcripts will be
available at personalized links emailed to you immediately after completing Part 1.**

**We recommend completing Part 1 as soon as possible. Part 2 may be completed in a separate
sitting.**

**Application Deadline: February 23rd, 11:59PM EST**

[Research Experience for Undergraduates (REU)](/commcms/garcia/summer_program/reu.php "REU")

* Research Experience for High School Teachers (RET)

  The Garcia Center for Polymers, funded by the National Science Foundation for research
  and education on Polymer Engineering has four openings in the Research Experience
  for High School Teachers (RET) summer program. The summer program lasts 7 weeks, starting
  the last week of June and pays a stipend. Candidates can work independently or join
  existing research groups doing research on polymer blends, composite materials, polymer/inorganic
  surfaces and interfaces, protein and DNA surface interactions, polymer recycling,
  polymer materials engineering, theoretical modeling, x-ray and neutron scattering
  at Brookhaven National Laboratory, electron, x-ray and scanning force microscopy,
  Infrared spectroscopy, and secondary ion mass spectrometry. Candidates will publish
  in refereed research journals and attend professional conferences.

  Interested candidates should email the program directly with any questions at [GarciaResearch@stonybrook.edu](mailto:GarciaResearch@stonybrook.edu).

  **[*Application Link*](/commcms/garcia/_documents/applications/Garcia%20RET%202025%20Application.docx)**

  Application Deadline: February 28th 2026
