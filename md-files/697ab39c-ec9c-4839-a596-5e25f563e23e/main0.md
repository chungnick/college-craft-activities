# Pre-CollegeA Taste of College Life

---

> The Emory Pre-College Program is a summer academic program for high school students. It gives current sophomores and juniors an exciting glimpse of academic and/or residential life at a top-ranked liberal arts university.

## Program Announcements

##### applications are open for summer 2026!

We have rolling admissions. Complete applications are reviewed once submitted. Apply now!

##### join us for an info session - registration required

Get to know us and the difference between all three opportunities we offer. **[Register now!](about/info-sessions.html)**

## Get Started

### Step 1

### Choose a Program

Every summer we offer a wide range of 100+ courses all taught by Emory faculty. These courses are divided between two programs -- Pre-College (noncredit) and Summer College (credit bearing).

[Compare Programs](programs/index.html)

### Step 2

### Browse & Find your Course

Visit our course listings to search by your favorite subject, instruction method, date ranges, etc.

[Browse Courses](courses/index.html)

### Step 3

### Review Costs & Scholarship Information

Determine costs based on your chosen program and course. Explore additional funding options and review our payment and refund policies.

[Research Costs & Scholarships](cost-and-scholarships/index.html)

### Step 4

### Verify Eligibility & Start your Application

Ensure that you are eligible to apply to our programs. Review the application checklist for any additional requirements. Start a new application, or complete an open application.

[Apply Now](apply/index.html)

### Step 5

### Complete Pre-Arrival Checklist

Once accepted, complete next steps in the Pre-Arrival Checklist.

[Admitted Student Information](info-for/admitted-students/index.html)
