# Eligibility & Application Process

## Program Criteria & Eligibility

#### Apply if [you meet all criteria]:

* **Minimum 15 years** of age by session start date
* Current 10th or 11th graders in high school **when completing the application**
* Highly motivated and committed to engaging in college-level work during the summer
* Minimum GPA for admission is 3.0

#### do Not Apply if:

* Currently in 9th or 12th grade
* Younger than 15 years of age
* Graduated from high school

### International Applicants

Any applicant who is **not a US citizen or US Permanent Resident** must apply as an international applicant. This also applies to students currently attending a US high school.

Applicants whose primary language is not English must demonstrate fluency in written and spoken English. This can be shown by: 

* A 700+ on the SAT Evidence-based Reading and Writing
* A 30+ on the ACT English subset

 If the initial criteria are not met, one additional exam must be submitted, with the following acceptable scores: 

 *If you do need meet the above SAT or ACT test score, you must submit one of the below test score.* 

* TOEFL: 104 (iBT), C1 (TOEFL ITP), or 7.5 (IELTS)
* For students in mainland China, a C1-level score on TOEFL ITP Plus is acceptable.

The highest score from multiple test sittings will be considered. Students may also submit SAT or ACT writing sections for proficiency. 

Additionally, the Duolingo English Test (DET) can be submitted. Competitive applicants typically score above 130 out of 160. The video interview portion is not scored.

## Application Checklist

Here is a brief description of what is required to complete your application. Please read all instructions and information in the online application.

* $70 non-refundable application fee
* Online application questionnaires (demographic, parent/guardian, educational information, course selection)
* Unofficial or official high school transcript
  + We do **not** accept report cards and progress notes in lieu of a transcript
* Test scores: PSAT, PreACT, SAT or ACT scores are all acceptable standardized tests
  + PreCollege Program (noncredit) Course: **optional**
  + Summer College Program (credit) Course: **required**
* Two recommendations from teachers and/or a guidance counselor

**Note:** Applicants will enter information for primary parent/guardian in the online application. The primary parent/guardian is required to complete the parental consent.

### International Applicants

International Applicants must complete the application checklist in addition to:

* English writing sample
* English language test score
  + Refer to *Program Criteria and Eligibility for International* *Student* listed above

## How To Start An Application

##### **Things to know:**

* Complete applications are evaluated ***upon submission*** until classes are filled or [deadline](../programs/index.html) approaches.
* Decision notifications are made (via email) on a rolling basis.
* Apply early as classes fill quickly.

### Pre-College Program Applications

To start an application you must:

1. **Create an account.***You will be leaving the Emory Pre-College website to an application portal. Take your time to follow all of the required prompts.*
2. **Already created an account?** Return to the application portal using the **Account Login** navigation at the top-right or footer of our website.

Need [**LOGIN HELP**](../help.html)? You can always find it in the top-right corner and footer of our website.
