# Tuition and Financial Aid

A smart investment yields a good return. That's what our students get when they choose Penn State Abington. The financial investment students and their families make helps students receive the education they need to find a career they'll love.  
  
Employers hire our graduates because they trust a Penn State education. Penn State ranks in the top five for preparing students for careers after graduation, according to a survey of the nation’s top corporate college recruiters.

Please [contact Financial Aid](https://www.abington.psu.edu/tuition-financial-aid/contact) if you need assistance.

## 2026-27 Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA®)
