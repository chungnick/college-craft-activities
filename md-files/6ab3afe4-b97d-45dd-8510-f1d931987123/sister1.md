# Summer Camps and Youth Programs

Explore a diverse range of youth programs and summer opportunities at The University of Texas at Arlington. We offer programs designed to cater to various ages, interests, and skill sets from stimulating academic challenges and athletic pursuits to immersive experiences in the arts and cutting-edge technology. Our programs encourage learning, personal growth, and community engagement. Whether you are a child, teen, or adult seeking to acquire new skills, connect with peers, or stay active during the summer, our programs are tailored to help you reach your objectives and create unforgettable moments.

Choose from short workshops to multi-week camps in fields of science, technology, engineering, math, college preparation, sports, art, and music. Discover the perfect program to ignite your passion, forge lasting friendships, and embark on a journey of discovery and fun.
