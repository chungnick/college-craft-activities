# Summer Camp for High School Students

Two camps are offered that are each two weeks long. Each camp can accommodate up to
30 students and takes place on the Texas Tech Health El Paso campus. The curriculum
consists of interactive presentations with health care professionals, field trips,
a mock crime scene investigation, financial aid information, and a reality store exercise
(simulation of life expenses). Students are informed through visits with advisors
at high school campuses in the El Paso region.

Please see [Summer Medical Camp flyer](/som/admissions/outreach/_images/Summer_Camp_Illustration_Flyer.png) for a printable version.

[2025 Summer Camp Registration](https://connect.elpaso.ttuhsc.edu/register/?id=3cb0b075-982e-478d-9564-47aeef93a79a)

We are encouraging eligible participants and families of participants to get vaccinated
before participating in any summer activities with increased risk exposure.

Must be available to attend in person, from 8 a.m.- 4 p.m. on camp days

**Camp Dates:**

* Camp 1: June 16 - 27, 2025
* Camp 2: June 30 - July 11, 2025

For any questions please email [outreach-elpaso@ttuhsc.edu](mailto:outreach-elpaso@ttuhsc.edu)
