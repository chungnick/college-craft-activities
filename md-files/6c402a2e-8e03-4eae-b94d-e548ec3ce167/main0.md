# Overview

The University of Texas Engineering & Computer Science Camp is **for students who will have graduated from 9th or 10th grade by Summer 2026**. In this five-week camp, students explore concepts related to real-world engineering, computational thinking, computer programming in Scratch and object-oriented languages like Python, and technology development. 

All students receive support from an instructor, UT student interns, and other UT faculty and staff. The camp also includes a mixture of lab tours, career speakers, and college/career exploration opportunities. This camp is NOT for high school credit. 

**Registration Requirements:** Seats fill first come, first served. A complete registration contains the following components:

* **UT Ideal Logic PREP Application**.
  + Submit the application using the link above.
* **UT EID**. Create a UT EID and enter it in the application. [**You can create one here**](https://idmanager.its.utexas.edu/eid_self_help/?geid=), for step by step instructions, [**check out a how-to video here**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EePa27mGuGE)**.**
* **Student Survey**.A student’s google email (separate from parent/guardian) is required to receive the student survey link. Student’s will receive the link once the application is submitted.
* **Copy of your Student's Grade Report**
  + This can be uploaded to the online application or via email [summercamps@uteach.utexas.edu](mailto:summercamps@uteach.utexas.edu).
  + Must have minimum 2 six-weeks or 1 nine-weeks of grades.
  + **Grade Requirements:**
    - Must have an 85 minimum in Math, Science & English. 80 allowed for advanced courses.
    - All other courses must have a 70 minimum.
