## UTeach Outreach Summer Camps

UTeach Outreach offers a variety of programs that inspire and engage students with inquiry-based, hands-on science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM) content. Come learn with us this summer!

# Early STEM Engagement Camps

Early STEM Engagement Camps give younger students an exciting introduction to the wonderful world of STEM.

### [Chemistry Camp](/chemistry-camp)

Chemistry Camp is open to students who will have graduated from 3rd, 4th, or 5th grade by summer 2026.

[Chemistry Camp](/chemistry-camp)

### [Research in Life Sciences Camp](/life-science-summer-camps)

Research in Life Sciences Camp is open to students who will have graduated from 4th, 5th, or 6th grade by summer 2026.

[Research in Life Sciences Camp](/life-science-summer-camps)

## UT PREP Summer STEM Pathway

The UT PREP Summer STEM Pathway provides advanced content, career exploration opportunities, and mentorship to middle and high school students for four summers. UT PREP camps must be completed consecutively.

## STEM Academy Programs

STEM Academy Programs are designed to supplement traditional, in-school curriculum with inquiry-based methods.

### [Physics & Math Camp](/physics-and-math-camp)

Physics & Math Camp is open to students who will have graduated from 7th or 8th grade by Summer 2026.

[Physics & Math Camp](/physics-and-math-camp)

### [Research & Statistics Camp](/research-statistics-camp)

Research & Statistics Camp is open to students who will have graduated from 8th or 9th grade by Summer 2026.

[Research & Statistics Camp](/research-statistics-camp)

### [Engineering & CS Camp](/engineering-and-computer-science-camp)

Engineering & CS Camp is open to students who will have graduated from 9th or 10th grade by Summer 2026.

[Engineering & CS Camp](/engineering-and-computer-science-camp)
