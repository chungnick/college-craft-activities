## Why Summer Session?

* Accelerate progress towards your degree
* Enroll in courses that are difficult to enroll in during the fall, winter or spring quarters
* Enhance your career prospects
* Enrich your cultural and intellectual knowledge

**FINANCIAL AID NOTE**: UC San Diego Students, please review the [UC San Diego Summer Session Financial Aid](https://fas.ucsd.edu/summer-session/index.html) site for more info. Non-UC San Diego Students should contact their home campus for Financial Aid assistance.

**ACADEMIC RIGOR NOTE**: The majority of classes in the summer are the same you will find during the 10 week academic year terms (Fall, Winter, Spring Quarters), and are taught in 5 week sessions.

**IMPORTANT**: If you drop a course, be sure to do the following: check the [refund schedule](tuition/refunds.html), [contact housing](https://hdhughousing.ucsd.edu/living-on-campus/summer/index.html), and [contact financial aid](tuition/financial-aid.html).

## Summer Steps

1. Determine your [eligibility](enroll/eligibility.html) to be a Summer Session student
   * [Current UC San Diego Students](https://summersession.ucsd.edu/enroll/application.html#Current-UC-San-Diego-Students)
   * [Incoming First Year and Transfer Students to UC San Diego](https://summersession.ucsd.edu/enroll/application.html#Incoming-First-Year-and-Transfe)
   * [Visiting UC Students](https://summersession.ucsd.edu/enroll/application.html#Visiting-UC-Students)
   * [High School Students](https://summersession.ucsd.edu/enroll/application.html#High-School-Students)
   * [International Students](https://summersession.ucsd.edu/enroll/application.html#International-Students)
   * [Other College or University Students and the General Public](https://summersession.ucsd.edu/enroll/application.html#Other-College-or-University-Stu)
   * [Alumni of UC San Diego](https://summersession.ucsd.edu/enroll/application.html#Alumni-of-UC-San-Diego)
2. Campus health and safety regulations can be found on the [Return to Learn website](https://returntolearn.ucsd.edu/).
3. Search the [Schedule of Classes](https://act.ucsd.edu/cgi-bin/tritonlink.pl/3/students/academic/classes/schedule_of_classes.pl) to find the [course(s](courses/index.html)) you need
   * In-Person: course will have an on-campus building and room
   * Fully remote: course will have the building name RCLAS
   * Hybrid: combination of an on-campus building and room assignment with building name RCLAS
4. Visit [Application and Enrollment](enroll/application.html)to complete the summer session application
5. Enroll in your course(s) via [WebReg](https://act.ucsd.edu/webreg2)
6. Pay [summer fees](tuition/index.html)
7. Review Course Syllabus on [Canvas](https://canvas.ucsd.edu/).
8. Complete Course Assignments
9. Review [transcript](enroll/grading.html) record
