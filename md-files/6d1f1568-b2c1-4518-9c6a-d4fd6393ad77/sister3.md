# Financial Aid

Financial aid is available from UC San Diego for eligible UC San Diego students only. Please visit the [Financial Aid Summer Session website](https://fas.ucsd.edu/summer-session/index.html) for more information.

If you are visiting student for the Summer, you may be eligible for aid from your home campus. Please contact the Financial Aid Office on your home campus for more information.

For more information regarding billing and other deadlines, please refer to the [Summer Session Calendar.](../calendar/index.html)

Please visit the [Financial Aid Office Summer Website](https://fas.ucsd.edu/summer-session/) for more information.
