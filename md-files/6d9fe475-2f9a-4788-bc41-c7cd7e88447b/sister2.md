# How to Apply

### **Access the Summer Scholars**[**registration website**](https://apps.ideal-logic.com/cuparticipant?key=S2QM-6QBSB_WPG5-2H2G_3502a470)**here**

#### **FOR NEW CAMPERS:**

1. Select "Ideal-Logic Login".
2. Select "Create a New Account".
3. Enter Parent/Guardian name and a valid, frequently-accessed email address.  The information provided will be the primary method of contact prior to program start.

  

#### **FOR RETURNING CAMPERS:**

1. Select "Ideal-Logic Login".
2. Use the credentials you previously created to log in to your account

  

**Once logged into the registration site, complete all forms fully and accurately. Follow along with this video to walk you through the application process:**

#### **DEPOSIT:**

A $200 initial deposit is required to submit the registration (This only applies to summer camps). See fees and payments below.

\*This fee must be paid online at the time of registration to secure a spot in the summer program. Mailed checks are discouraged and may not be received in time to secure a spot if the class fills. 

#### **TRANSCRIPTS:**

All students must submit an unofficial copy of their High School transcript before acceptance into the program.

* Applicants who have a gifted GPA of 3.3 or higher will be accepted upon receipt of transcript.
* Applicants with a weighted GPA lower than a 3.3 must also submit a letter of recommendation from a teacher, coach, counselor, tutor, etc.

**Transcripts may be mailed or sent electronically from the camper's school to [cusummerscholars@clemson.edu](mailto:cusummerscholars@clemson.edu).**

#### **WAITLISTS:**

Waitlists are available for all courses. To be added to a waitlist, please complete the registration as normal; however, on the session and course page, you will select "+waitlist" instead of "+course."

* If a spot becomes available, we will reach out to you to let you know.
* No deposit is required for those who are on the waitlist unless a spot becomes available.

  

**Registration is on a first-come, first-serve basis. Your registration will not be approved until a transcript has been received.**

Thank you for choosing Summer Scholars!  Stay tuned for additional information as your session approaches.  Feel free to email [cusummerscholars@clemson.edu](mailto:cusummerscholars@clemson.edu) any questions as they arise.  Go Tigers!
