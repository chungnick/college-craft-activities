# Catalyst: Creating Opportunities in STEM For Students with Disabilities

*High School* | *Yearlong* | *Commuter*

Catalyst is a free, yearlong, international, national and local award winning program that serves high school Students with disabilities who are interested in STEM Careers.

We provide:

* STEM Content and Skills
* Workforce and College Readiness Skills
* Paid STEM Internships
* National Field Studies Trips
* and some STEM Certifications

Attend our Open House on January 17, 2026 at 10 a.m. on NC State’s campus or our Virtual Open House on January 15, 2026 at 7 p.m. Sign up at [go.ncsu.edu/catalyst-open-house-2026](https://go.ncsu.edu/catalyst-open-house-2026).

**Students must attend if they want to enroll for June 2026-June 2027.**

| Program Dates | June 2026 – June 2027 |
| --- | --- |
| **Application Deadline** | January 15 and January 17, 2026 |

## Cost of Attendance

No Cost

## Delivery Mode

In-Person

## Eligibility

Must be a 9-12th grade student in North Carolina with an IEP, 504 Plan or a documented disability.
