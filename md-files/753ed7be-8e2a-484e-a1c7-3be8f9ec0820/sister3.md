# Summer 2024 Scholar Info Session (EASTERN TIME)

An overview of Kode With Klossy and the application!

Registration is closed

[See other events](https://www.kodewithklossy.com)

## Time & Location

Feb 29, 2024, 7:00 PM – 8:00 PM EST

## About the event

Get your questions answered about Kode With Klossy! Join members of the Kode With Klossy team as they walk you through what to expect from KWK, from the application and more. 

Please note that this event is set to Eastern Standard time
