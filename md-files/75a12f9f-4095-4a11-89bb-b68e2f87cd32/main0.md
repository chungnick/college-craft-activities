# About

2019 DukeREP Cohort

**The 2026 DukeREP student application is open!**

Duke Research in Engineering Program (DukeREP) is a 7-week summer program hosted by the [Biomedical Engineering Department](https://bme.duke.edu/) (BME) for high school students with an interest in science and engineering.

DukeREP’s mission is to promote STEM education and hands-on training for all high school students. Our program introduces students from all backgrounds to scientific research in hopes of inspiring them to pursue academic and professional careers in STEM.

**DukeREP eligibility criteria:**

* Be a rising junior or rising senior in high school in Summer 2026
* Be 16 years of age or older

While priority is given to Durham Public School students, we accept applications from all eligible high school students ***in North Carolina***.

***Students from first-generation families or from economically disadvantaged backgrounds are especially encouraged to apply. We are unfortunately not able to provide accommodations for students outside of NC.***

**Program Specifics:**

* Students work on research projects in Duke BME labs\*\*
* Students participate in weekly social activities and seminars that include college advising, professional skills development, and talks by professors and professionals in engineering
* No cost to apply or attend
* Students receive a stipend
* A GoPass is provided for transportation
* Lunch is provided daily

\*\*DukeREP Summer 2025 will be held in person. This is subject to change. For the latest updates, visit our [news](https://sites.duke.edu/dukerep/news/) page.

**Impo****rtant Dates:**

* Application release date: November 2025
* Student Application deadline: February 15th 2026
* Teacher Recommendation Form deadline: February 20th 2026
* Decision notification: Spring 2026
* Program Dates: June 15th, 2026 – July 31st, 2026

**Visit the [Apply](https://sites.duke.edu/dukerep/apply/) page for application instructions.**

For more information, visit our [FAQ](https://sites.duke.edu/dukerep/faq/) page or email us questions at dukerep@duke.edu.
