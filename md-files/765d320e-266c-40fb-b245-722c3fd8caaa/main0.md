# Summer Scholars Leadership Program Application

### The Leadership program is for rising 10th-12th graders.

### [Leadership Program Application](/live/forms/41-summer-scholars-leadership-program-application)

---

How do you become a leader? Find out during the Summer Scholars Leadership Program.The Leadership program is for rising 10th-12th graders. This program provides skills and tools to help you become better prepared for leadership roles in high school as well as in college.

During the program, your student will experience a unique mix of learning activities designed to broaden your child’s interests and expose him or her to a college-like setting.  If you choose to send your child to Summer Scholars camp, he or she will attend classes in E&H academic buildings, live in a campus residence hall, and be assigned an adult counselor who will be present at all times throughout the entire week.

During the week, leadership students attend a leadership class in the morning to learn about topics related to leadership. This accelerated program will provide skills and tools to help students become better prepared for leadership roles in high school as well as college. Students will have the opportunity to select from several different modules in the Leadership Program to include Servant Leadership, Leadership in Action, Faith Based Leadership, and the Outdoor Adventure Program. Topics may include: what is leadership, leadership assessments and challenge activities. Afternoon and evening class topics may include leadership tower, community service, exploring values and participation in outdoor adventures.
