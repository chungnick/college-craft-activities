# Admissions

Excellent Education Within Reach: Tuition Reduced to $19,990 beginning in the Fall of 2026 with scholarships/aid for all

(new & returning students)](/financial-aid/tuition-fees/)

With an Emory & Henry education, you will be rooted in a tradition of academic excellence, community and values, and equipped with the skills and support to go anywhere.

## Join a Community that Supports Your Uniqueness

You belong on our campus and in our community — where we celebrate learning, performing, connecting, playing, designing, practicing, embracing our differences, having fun, building skills, and getting hands-on experiences in and out of the classroom.

This is your time to explore you, the world and find your place. *You can* and *you will* succeed at Emory & Henry. We’re here for you – Let’s do this!

## Start Your Journey at Emory & Henry

Whether you are [beginning your journey in higher education](/admission/how-to-apply/first-year/), [transferring](/admission/how-to-apply/transfers/), [pursuing a graduate education](/admission/how-to-apply/graduate/), [an international student studying in the United States](/admission/how-to-apply/international/), or [an adult learner wanting to continue their education](/eh-online/admissions/), we encourage you to take the first step to an affordable, rewarding education at Emory & Henry University. Our [qualified admissions counselors](/admission/meet-our-team/) can help you with the application process and answer your questions.

[Apply Now](/admission/apply-now/) [Request Info](/live/forms/89-request-information)

* #### First-Year Students

  We’re glad you’re considering attending Emory & Henry University for the first time. Find out what is required and recommended when you apply for enrollment. Also, learn about exciting program options for first-year students.

  [Apply as a First-Year Student](https://www.emoryhenry.edu/admission/how-to-apply/first-year/)
* #### Graduate Students

  Elevate your career to new heights by continuing your studies with an advanced degree through Emory & Henry. Learn about admissions requirements for each graduate program, application deadlines, and available financial aid.

  [Apply as a Graduate Student](https://www.emoryhenry.edu/admission/how-to-apply/graduate/)
* #### Transfer Students

  Now is the time to Transfer & Transform when attending the university of choice for students transferring from community college or four-year private or public institutions. Learn about the requirements for transferring.

  [Apply as a Transfer Student](https://www.emoryhenry.edu/admission/how-to-apply/transfers/)
* #### International Students

  Emory & Henry welcomes applications from international students interested in pursuing an education in the United States. Learn about information related to admission, scholarships and requirements for student visas.

  [Apply as an International Student](https://www.emoryhenry.edu/admission/how-to-apply/international/)
* #### Concurrent Admission

  Emory & Henry has Concurrent Admission Agreements with several Virginia community colleges to provide a transfer pathway for students to earn four-year degrees. Learn about the different pathways and benefits of concurrent admission.

  [Apply for Concurrent Admission](https://www.emoryhenry.edu/admission/apply-now/concurrent-admission/)
* #### Re-Admit & Non-Degree Seeking Students

  Do you want to return to Emory & Henry University or enroll in classes without earning a degree? Learn how you can apply for readmission or apply with a special student application.

  [About Readmission & Other Applications](https://www.emoryhenry.edu/admission/apply-now/re-admit-other/)
* #### Online Student & Adult Learner

  Emory & Henry University offers online degrees for adult learners wanting to complete their degree on their own time. Learn about tuition requirements for online programs.

  [Learn More about Online and Degree-Completion Programs](https://www.emoryhenry.edu/eh-online/admissions/)

## Explore Tuition and Aid

We have lowered our undergraduate tuition price to $19,990 beginning in the Fall of 2026. Emory & Henry is committed to making an excellent education accessible to every student and offers generous academic merit, performance and affinity scholarships, as well as need-based financial aid that makes the cost to attend even lower. We want to partner with you to make your education as affordable as possible. Reach out!

Our qualified [admissions counselors](/admission/meet-our-team/) and [financial aid staff](/financial-aid/) are available to discuss what scholarship and aid options are available to you.

[View Tuition & Aid](/admission/cost-financial-aid/)

### Frequently Asked Questions

* Do I have to submit all documents at the same time as my application?

  No. Application materials can be submitted with or after submission of your admission application. If you do not submit all materials upon submission of your application you will be notified of missing requirements.
* Where do I mail my transcript(s)?

  You will need to request official transcript(s) be sent to Emory & Henry. Transcripts can be mailed to Emory & Henry’s address or requested electronically from their high school guidance office to be emailed to [admission@emoryhenry.edu](mailto:admission@emoryhenry.edu).   
    
  Mail your transcript(s) to:  
  Emory & Henry University  
  Office of Admissions  
  P.O. Box 947  
  Emory, Va. 24327
* Is SAT or ACT required for admission?

  We are test-score optional at this time. For admission to Emory & Henry University, a student should complete the application for admission and submit their official transcripts from high school and/or for any college or university coursework.
* What is your average GPA for admission?

  The average GPA for new incoming students: First-Year 3.5 or Transfer 3.3
* Do you accept application fee waivers?

  Applications for undergraduates are free to complete with either the [E&H application](/admission/apply-now/) or the [Common App](http://www.commonapp.org/). Therefore, Emory & Henry University does not accept application fee waivers.
* Do you give credit for AP (Advanced Placement) Test?

  Yes. Emory & Henry College offers credit for AP Test based on specific scores related to specific subjects. See a list of Courses and Test Scores in our Academic Catalog.
* What is the deadline to apply?

  Mid-July
* When will I hear back about an admissions decision?

  We offer rolling admissions and typically once all documents are received you will receive an admissions decision within 24-48 hours.
* What type of Financial Aid does Emory & Henry offer?

  At Emory & Henry, we’re committed to partnering with you to make your education at Emory & Henry University affordable. Visit the [Office of Financial Aid](/financial-aid/) web page to learn more.
* Does E&H accept the College Scholarship Service (CSS) Financial Aid Profile?

  Emory & Henry University does not accept the CSS Profile, we administer our financial aid process through the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA).
* Does E&H accept the Yellow Ribbon?

  Yes. The Yellow Ribbon GI Education Enhancement Program (Yellow Ribbon Program) is a provision of the Post-9/11 GI Bill that allows veterans to attend private schools and graduate programs that cost more than the state tuition cap. [Learn more about Veteran Benefits](/financial-aid/resources/information-for-veterans-benefits/).
