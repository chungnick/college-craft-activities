## Is Summer Springboard for Me?

Summer Springboard is designed for teens, ages 12-18 (must have completed 6th grade for our middle school programs, and open to rising 9th graders for our high school programs) who want to get a first hand taste of campus living, learn more about their academic and career interests from real world experts, improve their personal leadership skills and create their own unique pathway to happiness and success.

Our program can be your springboard to investigate career pathways and learning to be a leader at school and in your community.

Our students are bright and motivated teens from all over the world, who are eager to meet new people and experience new things. This makes for a diverse and exciting community of new friends.

## What Does It Mean if I am Waitlisted?

There are two reasons you would be placed on a waitlist.

* There is no more space available in the course you selected
* There is no more space in the session dates you selected

Once on a waitlist you will stay on the waitlist and will not be charged the deposit or application fee until a spot opens up.

If an enrolled student decides to cancel or switch sessions we will call the next person up on the waitlist to take their spot.

Typically between March-May there is not a lot of movement on the waitlist. We recommend applying to a different campus/session that has [availability](https://summerspringboard.com/availability/) to ensure you are able to reserve a spot.
