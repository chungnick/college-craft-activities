### All day camps are held in Jordan Hall on Clemson University’s campus

[Registration Closed](https://bit.ly/3cbTJwN "https://bit.ly/3cbTJwN")

***Note:*** **If your child attends a morning and afternoon camp in the same week, please bring 2 snacks and a lunch they can eat with our counselors — lunch and snacks are not provided.**

If you have signed up for only one day camp in a given week (AM or PM), drop-off starts 15 minutes before class start time (9:15 am or 12:45 pm respectively), and students must be picked up within 15 minutes of the class end time (12:15 pm or 3:45 pm respectively).

**Crime Scene Investigation (**CSI)

**Age:** 8-12 years old

**Dates:**

**Cost**: $170

* Investigate a crime scene to determine what happened and how
* Learn how your senses detect clues — or fool you!
* Identify criminals and victims using DNA, fingerprints, blood, and other forensic science

**Electricity, Magnets, Robots, and Rockets (Physics)**

**Age:** 8-12 years old

**Dates:**

**Cost**: $170

* Explore basic principles of electricity by building and manipulating electric circuits
* Investigate how magnetic and electric fields interact
* Build and test conductivity meters, electromagnets, and battle bots
* Experiment with Newton’s 3 laws of motion by designing and building compressed air rockets

**Engineering Design**

**Age:** 8-12 years old

**Dates:**

**Cost**: $170

* Learn the engineering design cycle by engaging in hands-on design challenges
* Build, test, evaluate, and improve your prototypes based on scientific principles
* Design and build crash cars, pop rockets, lunar landers, and more!

**Botanical Wonders**

**Age:** 8-12 years old

**Date:**

**Cost:** $170

* Examine plants using compound and dissecting microscopes
* Create art using plants and construct your own terrarium
* Explore the “superpower” of plants – photosynthesis
* Solve the mystery of the dead hemlock tree
* Thanks to a partnership with Clemson’s Herbarium, campers will be able to examine plants and seeds and learn all about their structure!

**Weird & Wacky Food Science**

**Age:** 8-12 years old

**Dates**:

**Cost**: $170

* Discover what makes pop rocks pop
* Experiment with coke and mentos to create eruptions
* Explore enzymes and sugars in our food
* Investigate how our senses impact taste

**Elemental Adventures: Exploring Physical Properties**

**Age:** 8-12 years old

**Dates:**

**Cost**: $170

* Experiment with air pressure using bottles, balloons, balls, paper airplanes and Cartesian divers
* Dig into density and surface tension by constructing your own density column and making a compass using surface tension
* Design and build some boats based on buoyancy.
* Discover why a gummy bear grows or shrinks by exploring the concepts of diffusion and osmosis

**Chemistry Quest**

**Age:** 8-12 years old

**Dates**:

**Cost**: $170

* Investigate the pH of different items using indicators
* Harness chemical and physical reactions to create art
* Create polymer models, and design a delayed release pill using polymers
* Discover the science behind chromatography

***Cancellation Policy:*** Withdrawals prior to May 15th will be assessed a $25 processing fee; these withdrawals must be received in writing (an email is sufficient). There will be no refunds for withdrawals after May 16th.

## How to Register for Summer Camp

Near the top of this page is a button that says Register HERE. (Circled in purple on the screenshot below.)

Click to go to our registration page.

**Accessing the registration system:**

* If it is your first time accessing the site:
  + Select “Community Login”.
  + Select “Create a New Account”.
  + Under “About You”, Select “I am the parent or guardian”. Continue. Enter Parent/Guardian name and a valid, frequently accessed email address.  The information provided will be the primary method of contact prior to program start. Continue and follow the prompts.

* If you have accessed the site previously:
  + Select “Community Login”.
  + Use the credentials you previously created to log in to your account.

Once logged into the registration site, select your week, and course, and complete all forms fully and accurately.
