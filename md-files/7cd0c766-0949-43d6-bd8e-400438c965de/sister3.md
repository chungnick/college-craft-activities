Visit Opportunities

Explore the stunning beauty of our historic campus and discover our welcoming Flyer community. Virtual and in-person campus tours are available.

### Undergraduate Students

We invite you to visit our 423-acre campus and experience why UD students are consistently ranked among the happiest students in the nation by The Princeton Review. Our convenient options include:

* [**Information session and campus tour**](https://events.udayton.edu/en/Hu1Iu7R7/g/B1sf7jvTVd?date=TODAY&groupUi=Calendar) to learn about the admission process, discover the value of a UD education and tour campus
* [**Academic visits**](/apply/undergraduate/visit/academic-visit.php) to meet with faculty and learn more about your major
* [**Admission events**](/apply/undergraduate/events/index.php)
* [**Virtual visit**](/apply/undergraduate/visit/virtual-visit.php)

### Transfer Students

As a transfer student, you have specific questions. Visiting campus and meeting with an admission counselor is a great way to determine if the University of Dayton is the right fit. [Schedule a visit](/apply/undergraduate/transfer/visit.php) and try us on for size.

### International Students

Students from around the world have chosen the University of Dayton for their undergraduate education. To see if our top-tier university is the right place for your undergraduate studies, learn more during [visits and events for international students](/apply/international/undergraduate/visit-events.php).

Students interested in pursuing graduate studies at the University of Dayton should [contact their program director](/apply/graduate/visit.php) to learn more.

### Graduate Students

When immersed in the rigors and challenges of graduate studies, it helps to be surrounded by an inclusive and supportive community on a picturesque campus just minutes from downtown Dayton.

To schedule a personalized visit, contact the director for your desired [academic program](/academics/programs/index.php).

### Law Students

Is the UD School of Law right for you? [Visit campus](/law/admissions/index.php) and allow us to argue our case. Information sessions and individual visits are available, and we also attend recruitment events across the country.
