Get a jump on college credits Stony Brook University offers an exciting variety of opportunities for high school students, ranging from genuine hands-on research to credit bearing academic classes that can jump start your college career.   * **Stony Brook is one of the most affordable, top-ranked universities in the nation** * **Enhance your time management and study skills to be better prepared for college** * **Experience what it’s like to attend a major university**   If you are a high school student who will complete your junior year in June and your grade point average is 85 or higher, you may enroll in selected introductory (100-level) summer courses as a visiting Summer Sessions student. High School Summer Programs  * [Academy of Civic Life](https://www.stonybrook.edu/academyofciviclife/) * [Engineering Summer Camp](https://www.stonybrook.edu/commcms/istem/students/grades_5-12/summer/engineering.php) * [Explorations in Forensics Camp](https://www.stonybrook.edu/commcms/istem/students/grades_5-12/summer/explorations_forensics_camp.php) * [IACS COMPUTES!](https://calendar.stonybrook.edu/site/iacs/event/iacs-computes-2025/) * [Machine Learning & Robotics Summer Program](https://sunriseaitech.com/camp-page/) * [Mathematics Summer Program](https://www.math.stonybrook.edu/mathcamp) * [Middle School Math Camp](https://www.stonybrook.edu/commcms/istem/students/grades_5-12/ms_summer/middle_school_math.php) * [Middle School Science Exploration Camp](https://www.stonybrook.edu/commcms/istem/students/grades_5-12/ms_summer/science_exploration.php) * [Stony Brook Southampton Marine Science Courses](https://www.stonybrook.edu/commcms/southampton/somas_info.php#summer-courses) * [Physics Summer Camp](https://www.stonybrook.edu/commcms/istem/students/grades_5-12/summer/physics.php) * [Pre-College Summer Residential Program](https://stonybrook.edu/precollege-summer/) * [Science And Research Awareness Series (SARAS)](https://renaissance.stonybrookmedicine.edu/anesthes/SARAS) * [Simons Summer Research Fellowship](https://www.stonybrook.edu/simons/) * [Summer Youth Camp for Computational Linguistics (SYCCL)](https://iacs.stonybrook.edu/opportunities/K-12/Summer%20Youth%20Camp%20for%20Computational%20Linguistics.php) * [Youth Entrepreneurship Summer Camp](https://www.stonybrook.edu/commcms/cef/Program/TEASummerCamp)    Steps to Enroll - Summer 2025  1. Make your **course selection** and credits. 2. Complete our [**Summer Session Application**](https://enroll.stonybrook.edu/apply/). If appling for a specific program please check with the program's coordinator to    see if they have a separate application process. 3. Allow (3-5) business days for processing. You will be sent a **Stony Brook ID Number and registration instructions**. 4. If you are under 18 years of age, submit the [Financial Responsibility and Parental Consent Form](/commcms/summer/_pdf/minor.pdf) to Stony Brook's Office of Student Accounts by mail, fax, e-mail. This form must    be submitted before the first day of classes. 5. When you have completed your course work, you can order an [official transcript](/commcms/registrar/transcripts/ordertranscript). Please wait until grades have been posted to your SOLAR Account before placing the    order. | | | | | |
