## Pre-College Summer at Stony Brook

## Ready to see what college is really like?

Spend a week (or more!) experiencing our campus. You'll try out a subject you're curious
about, make new friends from far away, and get a genuine feel for college life. Whether
you're already set on a major or still figuring it out, you'll leave better prepared
for your next steps.

##### What you'll gain:

* A real sense of what college is like — the independence, the academics, and the community
* Hands-on experience in a subject area before committing to a major
* Practical skills that will help you succeed freshman year
* Connections with faculty who make their subjects come alive
* Clarity about your path forward
* Friends across the country who share your curiosity

## Choose Your Experience

Whether you stay in the residence halls or commute from home, you'll get the same
courses, build the same skills, and make lasting connections.

### Residential

* On-campus housing
* All meals included
* Evening programming
* Optional weekend stays

[Learn More](/commcms/precollege-summer/residential-experience.php)

### Commuter

* Two schedule options: 8am–4pm or 8am–6pm
* Add dinner (6pm option)
* Add college readiness workshops (6pm option)
* Return home each evening

[Learn More](/commcms/precollege-summer/commuter-experience.php)

### Scholarship Opportunity for Future Seawolves!

If you attend the SBU Pre-College Summer Program and subsequently apply, get accepted,
and enroll at Stony Brook University, you will be eligible for one of two scholarships:

* **New York State residents** will receive a $1,000 scholarship over 4 years ($250 per year)
* **Out-of-state residents** will receive a $2,000 scholarship over 4 years ($500 per year)
