# Deadlines

Listed below are relevant dates of registration and financial aid for the Season 29 tournaments.
You can add these dates to your calendar [here](//link.hmmt.org/reg-calendar-ics) (iCal)
or [here](//link.hmmt.org/reg-calendar-web) (web URL).

Please note if you are accepted for financial aid, you must confirm 1 week earlier
than the confirmation deadline listed below to receive financial aid. For more information,
please read [Financial Aid](/rg/finaid).
