# AMRI Summer Outreach Research Program

[**Research Program for High School Students**](http://new.uno.edu/amri/summer-outreach/research-program-high-school-students)

High school students and teachers participate in research projects within the areas of synthesis, characterization and application of nanoparticles and their composites and assemblies. Participants also attend scientific and professional development seminars, a session on research ethics, and participate in field trips to local industrial and related sites and in organized social activities. This program will begin June 1 and ends on July 24, 2026.

[**Research Program for Undergraduates**](http://new.uno.edu/amri/summer-outreach/research-program-undergraduates)

Undergraduate students participate in research projects within the areas of synthesis, characterization and application of nanoparticles and their composites and assemblies. The student participants also attend scientific and professional development seminars, a session on research ethics, and participate in field trips to local industrial and related sites and in organized social activities. The REU program begins June 2 and ends on July 25, 2025.
