## **Robertson Resident Life and Intercultural Development Office**

**The purpose** of the **Robertson Resident Life and Intercultural Development Office (RIO)** is to support the mission of I-House to foster respect, understanding, lifelong friendships, and leadership skills for a more just and peaceful world.

**Our mission** is to promote **intercultural curiosity and exchange and to advance inclusive global leadership**. We **respect** and **honor diversity** in all aspects and we aim to cultivate a **safe community** of **inclusion** and **authenticity**.

## Weekly Newsletters

The I-House Weekly Newsletter provides residents with updates on upcoming events, programs, recent developments, and news from International House at UC Berkeley. The newsletter serves as a communication hub for the diverse and vibrant community at International House, highlighting the rich cultural exchange and intellectual exploration that takes place on a daily basis.

I-House residents are automatically signed up for weekly newsletters when they sign up for StarRez resident portal. Make sure to updare your email in Starrez Resident portal with your preferred email address.
