## UHealth High School Student Research Trainee Program

UHealth’s high school student summer program begins **6/16/2026** and ends
**8/7/2026**, with limited spaces available.

**Application deadline:** 4/30/2026

The ideal student for this role has excellent interpersonal skills, and strong written and verbal communication skills. They should demonstrate compassion for patient needs and be detail-oriented, adaptable, and flexible. They should be able to follow directions, complete the application process, onboarding, and work independently.

## Student Training Activities May Include

* Data collection and analysis
* Experimentation in labs
* Helping PIs with various assignments
* Learning new software
* Participation in research meetings

## Requirements and Commitment

* Students must be at least 16 years of age to apply
* Maximum 20 hours a week training
* Students must train in pairs of two or more
* Identify and secure the department and supervisor for your desired research trainee position.
  **Prior sponsorship confirmation is required before applying**.
* Apply: [Volunteer Services Application (High School Student)](https://umiami.vsyslive.com/pages/app/HSVOLAPP)
* Meet all occupational health requirements, including proof of required immunizations, vaccinations, and recent TB Test (within 12 months)
* Complete a security background check and drug screening
* Complete and pass online training modules
* Attend mandatory virtual UHealth Care Orientation held on the first Wednesday of each month.
