### Application Fee Waiver

Fee Waiver Applications received after this date will not be considered. Approval of an application fee waiver **does not** constitute approval for a tuition scholarship or acceptance to the program.

**If your Application Fee Waiver is approved, you must return to your application portal and proceed to submit your application.**

[APPLICATION FEE WAIVER FORM](../include/documents/2026feewaiver.pdf)

### Tuition Scholarship Application

Deadline: February 16, 2026

**Qualifications To Apply for Scholarship:**

* Domestic U.S. student (international students are not eligible to apply)
* Completion of junior year of high school (rising senior)
* Demonstration of financial need
* Strong academic and extracurricular achievement

Scholarship Applications received after this date will not be considered. All scholarship applicants will be notified via email of the scholarship evaluation committee’s decision approximately four weeks after the deadline. **In order to be fully considered for a chance to receive a tuition scholarship the applicant must complete CPP's online application in its entirety, including having transcripts turned in before February 16, 2026**.

[TUITION SCHOLARSHIP APPLICATION](../include/documents/2026scholarship.pdf)

**Other Resources for Financial Assistance**  
 You may wish to search for other financial assistance opportunities by talking with your guidance or career counselor at your school, local PTA, civic (Rotary, Lions, Kiwanis, Alumni Associations, etc.) and religious organizations.
