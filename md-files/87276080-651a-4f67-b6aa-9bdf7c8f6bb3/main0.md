# High School Law Camp

  

## LSPI High School Law Camp

## June 22, 2026 to July 3, 2026

### The High School Law Camp is back for Summer 2026.

UTEP's Patti and Paul Yetter Center for Law offers a High School Law Camp each year.  The Law Camp is a two-week, all-day summer program that gives high school students an opportunity to explore a career in law and prepare for college.  Camp participants engage in a variety of activities, including case law instruction, fields trips to legal community organizations, professional skills development, and a mock trial exercise.  The Law Camp takes place on the UTEP campus.

The High School Law Camp is:

* Held Monday through Thursday (8:30am to 3:30pm) and Friday (8:30am to 12:30pm)
* FREE, with lunch and snacks provided Monday through Thursday
* Open to rising high school sophomores, juniors, and seniors, and recently graduated students

The High School Law Camp:  

* Introduces students to the legal profession
* Develops analytical and critical reading and writing skills
* Shows students that they can go to law school
