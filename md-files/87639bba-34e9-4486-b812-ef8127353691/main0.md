# Register for CMM 2026

Caltech Math Meet 2026 will take place on January 24th, 2026.

Registration for CMM26 is now closed.

Registration for CMM 2026 is now closed.

​

​​

​

​

CMM's in-person competition consists of the following:

​

* Power Round (to be done virtually before the competition)
* Team Round (in-person)
* Individual Round (in-person)
* All other optional in-person events (Integration Bee, Admissions event, Math talk)
* Breakfast and Lunch will be provided, as well as t-shirts and other goods from our sponsors.

​

We ask that, if possible, a coach, that is a legal guardian, fill out the sign up for each team, and accompany the students of the team to the competition if possible.

​

If your team is selected, then payment will be due by December 1st. Each team will cost $120 and each individual costs $20. Payment instructions are [here](https://www.caltechmathmeet.org/payment-instructions).

​

The top 10 teams from CMM 2025 will be guaranteed spots, but they still must register before the deadline. They are:

​

AlphaStar: 2 Teams  
Youth Euclid Association: 1 Team  
University High School: 1 Team  
Gunn Math Circle: 1 Team  
Random Math: 1 Team  
ZEFR: 1 Team  
Austin Math Circle: 1 Team  
Davidson Academy: 1 Team  
Canyon Crest Academy: 1 Team
