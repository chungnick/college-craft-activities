# Payment Instructions

Below are instructions to pay for this year's CMM 2026. Please only send payment if you have received an email on November 15th, 2025 that you were selected for the in-person competition. No refunds will be granted, unless the competition is cancelled. Payment is due by December 1st 2025, 11:59 PM PST.

## Payment by Credit Card

We prefer that you pay by credit card, which you can do by clicking below. Please ensure the information you enter on the form is correct to avoid any delays.

[Payment Form](https://sfe.jotform.com/252045629529967)

## Payment by Check

You may also pay by check if that's preferrable, but please ensure that the check and envelope details are correct or we will either not receive your payment, or be unable to process it. Below is a diagram with how both should be formatted. Please fill out the spaces as appropriate for your registration. The amount is $20 for an individual and $120 for a team. If you are paying by check, please also email [cmm-help@caltech.edu](mailto:cmm-help@caltech.edu) that you are doing so, so we know to expect your payment.
