## VOLUME DISCOUNT DETAILS

* **To qualify for volume discounts, all Team Numbers must be purchased on the same line item in the same order. Discount will be automatically applied during checkout.**
* Volume discounts may not be applied after an order is placed and paid for (including purchase orders).
* Orders may not be combined after they have been placed and paid for in order to qualify for volume discounts.
* Discounts do not apply to **[Affiliate Fees\*](https://www.destinationimagination.org/challenge-experience/pricing/affiliate-fees/)** added to each Team Number.
* **If you need any assistance with placing an order for volume discounts, please email [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#c1a0b2aaa5a881a5a8a9b0efaeb3a6).**

## \*Additional Fees & CostS

* Destination Imagination is administered through Affiliate organizations worldwide. These organizations are made up of volunteers and staff that dedicate countless hours to our mission.
* **AFFILIATE FEES:** Some Affiliates have directed us to collect fees with the purchase of Team Numbers. Affiliates may also charge additional tournament registration fees. **[Click here](https://www.destinationimagination.org/challenge-experience/pricing/affiliate-fees/)** to find your Affiliate and view posted fees.
* Teams are responsible for all material costs associated with their solution presentation. While each Challenge solution has a budget, what a team spends as they prototype, prepare and practice can vary greatly.
* At this time the registration fee for [Global Finals](https://www.destinationimagination.org/challenge-experience/global-finals/) 2026 is $5500 and $5000 per team for U.S. and non-U.S. teams respectively. Destination Imagination is working hard to ensure that the event is accessible to as many teams as possible.6

## CANCELLATION & REFUND POLICY

Team Number(s) are not eligible for refunds or cancellations. Purchase of a Team Number gives the purchaser digital access to exclusive content and intellectual property of DII, which cannot be undone or returned once accessed.
