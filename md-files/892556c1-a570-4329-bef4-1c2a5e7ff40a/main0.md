# NYU Dentistry Saturday Academy

[Skip Sidebar](#main-article)

The mission of the Saturday Academy is to cultivate a representative oral health workforce, especially in dentistry, by introducing high school students from all backgrounds to the profession of dentistry, while simultaneously preparing them for the college application process.

Established in 2012 by [Dr. Lorel Burns](http://dental.nyu.edu/faculty/ft/leb409.html) and [Dr. Cheryline Pezzullo](http://dental.nyu.edu/faculty/ft/cp1379.html) — at the time, both second-year students at the College — this preparatory program meets biweekly on Saturdays, 7 sessions per Fall semester, and has introduced 457 high school students to the field of dentistry as a career option. Today, some of the earliest Saturday Academy alumni are now dentists.

## How to Apply

The online application portal for the 2026 cycle will become available in the program website. We encourage applications from students of all backgrounds. Ideal candidates will:

* Be currently enrolled in high school. Sophomores and juniors are strongly encouraged to apply; freshmen and seniors are welcome
* Be able to commute to [NYU College of Dentistry](https://www.google.com/maps/place/NYU+College+of+Dentistry/@40.7362438,-73.9792319,17z) for all program sessions.
* Demonstrate interest in healthcare, dentistry, or STEM
* be committed to fully engaging in all program components

## Who You'll Study With

Previous program professionals and guests have included dentists and dental students.

## Areas of Study

Dentistry and College Application Preparation

This tuition-free program includes:

* Hands-on introduction to clinical dentistry and dental specialties
* Mentorship from NYU dental students and faculty
* Professional development and college readiness workshops
* Exposure to public health and health equity topics
* Career exploration in healthcare and STEM fields
* Academic enrichment activities and group discussions
