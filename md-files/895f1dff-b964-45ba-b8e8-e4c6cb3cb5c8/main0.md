# Botball Educational Robotics

The Botball Educational Robotics Program engages middle and high school students in a hands-on, team-based robotics competition that brings STEM to life. Students design, build, and program autonomous robots ‚Äî no remote controls here! ‚Äî using real engineering and coding skills aligned with today‚Äôs academic standards.

Botball inspires students through inquiry-based, learn-by-doing experiences that build creativity, critical thinking, and teamwork. By combining technical skill with collaboration, students develop the confidence and leadership needed to thrive in STEM careers.

Each season begins with Botball Educator Workshops in January through March, where teachers and mentors receive training and learn the details of the year‚Äôs game challenge. After a seven-week build period, teams head to regional tournaments to showcase their robots and compete for top honors.
