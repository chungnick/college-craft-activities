# High School Programs

The Hk Maker Lab is a free 5-week summer program, in collaboration with Columbia Engineering, that provides rising high school juniors and seniors with intensive engineering design training.

[LEARN MORE](https://www.hypothekids.org/hkmakerlab)

New York Bioforce is a free comprehensive science research training and internship program that prepares older high school students for higher education in STEAM fields and sets them on a path towards career success.

[LEARN MORE](https://www.hypothekids.org/nybioforce)

The STEAM EDU program trains high school students to become confident, hands-on STEAM educators through a combination of comprehensive training and paid summer internships, during which participants teach K–5 students using the HYPOTHEkids STEAM curriculum.

[LEARN MORE](https://www.hypothekids.org/steamedu)
