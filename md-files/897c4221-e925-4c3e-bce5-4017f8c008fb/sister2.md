Summer Pre-College

Applications for Summer 2025 are closed.

**Please note:** The first prompt on entering the application system is to create a username and password. The email address associated with this login MUST belong to the STUDENT who is applying. A parent email address will be entered at a later step. Unique email addresses belonging to the student and the parent are REQUIRED in order for your application to be processed.

We accept applications from rising sophomores, juniors and seniors in high school. High school seniors who will graduate in Spring 2025 are also welcome. Please note, the Pre-Veterinary Medicine and Veterinary Technology programs are open only to rising juniors and seniors.

Admissions decisions are made on a rolling basis. Applications will be accepted until programs have filled, or until the deadlines outlined below for each session (subject to availability). We recommend applying early as programs may fill up.

* Programs starting June 29: May 1st, 2025  *Extended to May 15th*
* Programs starting July 13: May 15th, 2025
* Programs starting July 27: May 31st, 2025

To apply for Summer Pre-College Programs, we require:

* A written statement of interest: a brief, one-page statement describing why you want to attend Summer Pre-College at UMass Amherst and specifically the program you have chosen.
* An unofficial high school transcript (PDF), including fall grades from the current school year. Rising sophomores should include a middle school transcript as well.
* A letter of recommendation written by a teacher or guidance counselor who can speak to your skills, abilities, maturity level and academic accomplishments that make you a suitable candidate for the program you are applying to. Letters of recommendation should be obtained by the applicant and uploaded into the online application system. If your reference prefers to submit their letter of recommendation directly, please upload a brief statement, in PDF format, indicating that your letter of recommendation is being sent directly, and instruct your reference to email the letter to: [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection).
* A non-refundable $45 application fee

UMass Amherst Pre-College Programs does not permit the use of generative AI tools for creating application materials such as statements of interest.

International students will be asked to provide additional information regarding English language proficiency. We accept TOEFL, IELTS or Duolingo scores. Students currently attending an English language or bilingual high school are exempt from test scores.

Internet Explorer browser is not recommended for the application.

Research Intensives

**Applications for Summer 2025 Research Intensives are closed.**

**Please note:** The first prompt on entering the application system is to create a username and password. The email address associated with this login MUST belong to the STUDENT who is applying. A parent email address will be entered at a later step. Unique email addresses belonging to the student and the parent are REQUIRED in order for your application to be processed.

We accept applications from rising juniors and seniors in high school. High school seniors who will graduate in Spring 2025 are also welcome.

* A letter of recommendation written by a teacher or guidance counselor who can speak to your skills, abilities, maturity level and academic accomplishments that make you a suitable candidate for the program you are applying to. Letters of recommendation should be obtained by the applicant and uploaded into the online application system. If your reference prefers to submit their letter of recommendation directly, please upload a brief statement, in PDF format, indicating that your letter of recommendation is being sent directly, and instruct your reference to email the letter to: [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection).
* A non-refundable $45 application fee

International students will be asked to provide additional information regarding English language proficiency. We accept TOEFL, IELTS or Duolingo scores. Students currently attending an English language or bilingual high school are exempt from test scores.

College Prep Workshops

The college prep workshops are open to rising sophomores, juniors and seniors in high school. High school seniors who will graduate in Spring 2025 are also welcome. 

**Online Workshops**

No application is required for the online college prep workshops. Registrations will be taken on a first come first served basis, and will remain open until workshops have filled.

1. From the [workshops webpage](https://www.umass.edu/uww/programs/pre-college/workshops), click the Register Now button to fill out and submit a registration form.
2. You will receive a follow-up email within 24-48 hours containing instructions to complete your registration.
3. Follow instructions to activate your Spire account, if you don't already have one.
4. Follow instructions to pay your workshop fees.

**In Person Workshops**

To apply for Summer Pre-College Programs, we require:

* A written statement of interest: a brief, one-page statement describing why you want to attend the workshop you have chosen.
* An unofficial high school transcript (PDF), including fall grades from the current school year. Rising sophomores should include a middle school transcript as well.

*No letter of recommendation is necessary (a placeholder PDF may be uploaded in this space).*

UMass Amherst Pre-College Programs does not permit the use of generative AI tools for creating application materials such as statements of interest.

**Please note:** The first prompt on entering the application system is to create a username and password. The email address associated with this login MUST belong to the STUDENT who is applying. A parent email address will be entered at a later step. Unique email addresses belonging to the student and the parent are REQUIRED in order for your application to be processed.

Applications for Summer 2025 are closed.
