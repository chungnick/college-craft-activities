# Volunteer

As a DOROT volunteer, you’ll make a difference in your life and the lives of others. Connect across generations, share your skills and knowledge, and build meaningful friendships. Volunteer in person, online, or by phone.

**Ready to learn more?** Scroll through our full list of volunteer opportunities below or use the filters to find the ones that interest you the most. Or sign up for an upcoming [Volunteer Information & Orientation Session](https://www.dorotusa.org/volunteer/volunteer-information-orientation-session/) to learn more.

### [Anita K. Hersh Jewish Summer Teen Internship](https://www.dorotusa.org/volunteer/jewish-summer-teen-internship/)
