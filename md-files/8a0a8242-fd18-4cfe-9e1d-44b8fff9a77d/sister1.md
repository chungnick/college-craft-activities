* [Estimated Cost of Attendance, 2026-27](/admission-aid/tuition-fees/estimated-cost-attendance-2026-27)

The figures below show the full, annual costs for a student attending Colgate without financial aid. To learn more about what your personal costs might be, try Colgate's MyinTuition or Net Price Calculators

[Estimate your aid](/admission-aid/financial-aid/estimate-your-aid "Estimate Your Aid ")

## The Colgate Commitment

A financial aid initiative for access and affordability that makes a Colgate education within reach for the best and brightest students from all economic backgrounds.

[More about the Colgate Commitment](/admission-aid/financial-aid/colgate-commitment "The Colgate Commitment")

## Figures for the 2025-26 Academic Year

### Billed Charges

First-Years & Sophomores (On-Campus)

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Tuition | $73,206 |
| Housing | $8,908 |
| Food | $9,538 (first-year students, sophomores, and anyone living in traditional residence halls are required to enroll in the [Premier meal plan](/about/campus-services-and-resources/meal-plans "Meal Plans")) |
| Student Activity Fee | $440 |
| Total Billed Charges | $92,092 |

Juniors & Seniors (On-Campus)

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Tuition | $73,206 |
| Housing | $9,654 (rate is the same for Broad Street housing, apartments and townhouses) |
| Food | $9,538\* |
| Student Activity Fee | $440 |
| Total Billed Charges | $92,838 |

*\*Cost of Premier meal plan. For juniors/seniors who choose a lower cost meal plan or do not enroll in a meal plan, the same amount will be allotted in the cost of attendance to determine financial aid eligibility.*

Seniors (Off-Campus)

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Tuition | $73,206 |
| Food | $9,538\* |
| Student Activity Fee | $440 |
| Total Billed Charges | $83,184 |

*\*Cost of Premier meal plan. For seniors who choose a lower cost meal plan or do not enroll in a meal plan, the same amount will be allotted in the cost of attendance to determine financial aid eligibility.*

Graduate Students (Off-Campus)

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Tuition | $48,804\* |
| Food | $9,538\*\* |
| Student Activity Fee | $440 |
| Total Billed Charges | $58,782 |

*\*Based on 3 course credit enrollment per semester. The graduate tuition charge per course credit is $8,134.00.*

*\*\*Cost of Premier meal plan. For graduate students who choose a lower cost meal plan or do not enroll in a meal plan, the same amount will be allotted in the cost of attendance to determine financial aid eligibility.*

## Additional Student Charges

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| One-time transcript fee (charged only in the first semester of attendance) | $50 |
| Student health insurance (may be waived with proof of sufficient coverage) | $1,913 Student Health Insurance Plan (SHIP) premium   * +$77 service fees and taxes.   $1,990 total (charged only for students not covered through family) |

Colgate also requires students to have health and accident insurance coverage. Students will be enrolled in and charged for the [student health insurance](/student-life/health-and-wellness/student-health-services/required-student-health-insurance "Required Student Health Insurance") plan automatically unless proof of sufficient coverage is provided annually by August 1.
