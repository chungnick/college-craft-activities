# A (summer) that changes everything

TKS Summer

## The ultimate summer experience at UCLA.

Embark on a transformative 3‑week pre‑college summer program at one of the world’s top universities, crafted exclusively for high school students. Experience innovative workshops, dynamic lectures, and real-world challenges all while living on campus, building global friendships, and exploring your future in tech, science, and entrepreneurship.

Hands-on workshops, discussions, and collaborative projects designed to spark creativity, build leadership skills, and help students thrive in a fast-changing world.

[Student Life](#Student-life)[Summer Courses](#Courses)

A summer you'll never forget

## "It's a life-changing experience in just one summer."

##### Life At UCLA

Experience college life firsthand. Stay in UCLA dorms, dine on campus, and make the city your playground. Spend your afternoons exploring Los Angeles, from Universal Studios and Disneyland to Santa Monica Beach, Malibu, pro sports games, Hollywood hikes, and introspective sunsets.

##### Summer Overview

Spend three weeks experiencing college life at UCLA. Mornings focus on hands-on learning, while afternoons take you beyond the classroom with industry visits, excursions, and time to explore LA. Choose your courses, build new skills, and develop mindsets for the future.

##### Why You’ll Love It

This isn’t just another summer program - it’s a chance to push your limits, gain real-world experience, and connect with like-minded people who share your drive. Develop employable skills, work on industry projects that set you apart, and showcase them in a personal portfolio.

##### Adventure In LA

Make lifelong memories with an incredible community, whether you're exploring Disneyland, cheering at a Dodgers game, or discovering the city with new friends. From thrilling excursions to late-night conversations, these experiences will stay with you long after the summer ends.

##### Speaker Series

Gain exclusive access to a speaker series featuring founders, scientists, and innovators who are shaping the future. Learn from world-class experts through personalized mentorship and hands-on coaching.

##### Official Certificate

Immerse yourself in three weeks of hands-on learning and earn a *Certificate of Completion* and a *Letter of Recognition* to showcase your achievements. Strengthen your academic portfolio and stand out in college applications with real-world experience that sets you apart.

## Frequently Asked Questions

01

What is included in the cost of the program?

This all-inclusive 3-week program covers tuition, housing, transportation, meals, and activities. **The total cost is $9300 USD.**

**Campus Life & Activities**

* **Residence at UCLA** – Stay in university residence halls and experience life as a college student.
* **All-Inclusive Dining** – Enjoy fully covered meals, with lunch and dinner provided daily.
* **Explore L.A.** – From Disneyland and Dodgers games to city adventures, make unforgettable memories.
* **Seamless Experience** – All transportation for program activities is covered, so you can focus on learning and fun.
* **Built-in Community** – Connect with ambitious students and form lifelong friendships while growing together.
* **24/7 Support & Safety** – Dedicated program directors and residential advisors ensure a safe, supportive environment.

‍

**Academic Experience**

* **University-Level Learning** – Dive into an intensive course led by industry experts, startup leaders, and innovation strategists.
* **Hands-On Projects** – Work on real-world challenges, build a portfolio, and sharpen problem-solving skills.
* **College & Career Readiness** – Develop leadership, personal branding, networking, and insights into college admissions.

[I'm Interested](https://airtable.com/appfIgL5sxCfy0mkI/pagetDvfMfqWsSmwQ/form)

02

What are the dates for these summer courses?

You can choose to attend one 3-week session or stay for the full 6-week summer experience.

* **Summer Session 1:** Sunday, June 29 – Friday, July 18, 2027
* **Summer Session 2:** Sunday, July 20 – Friday, August 8, 2027

All courses will be offered in both sessions, so you can choose the dates that work best for you. If you want to maximize your experience, you can attend both sessions, spending six weeks immersed in learning, leadership, and networking while exploring Los Angeles.

[I'm Interested](https://airtable.com/appfIgL5sxCfy0mkI/pagetDvfMfqWsSmwQ/form)

03

Will I get a certificate from the program?

You will receive a Certificate of Completion and a Letter of Recognition, which highlight your achievements and can be valuable for college applications, internships, and future career opportunities.

‍

Additionally, you’ll leave with a professional portfolio showcasing real-world projects, demonstrating your skills in leadership, problem-solving, and innovation - making you stand out to universities, employers, and scholarship opportunities.

04

How many students will be in each batch?

Each batch is limited to 50 students, ensuring an interactive, collaborative, and high-engagement learning experience with direct mentorship.

05

Can I participate if I'm a current student or an alumni of TKS?

Yes! This program is designed for students ages 15-18 from around the world. The TKS experience will enhance your experience in the summer program, while getting immersed in college life at UCLA.
