## 2025 Pre-College Summer Academies

---

Through these unique and exciting program opportunities, you'll participate in interactive discussions, hear from guest speakers, and engage in hands-on activities with our faculty and student ambassadors...all while earning **1 college credit**. Enjoy socializing and networking with your fellow classmates during fun-filled campus events.

*Our Academies are open to high school students entering their junior or senior year, as well as any accepted Freshman for the Fall of 2025 at Molloy University.*

DEADLINE TO REGISTER IS JULY 1st, 2025.  ALL ACADEMIES ARE NON-REFUNDABLE AFTER JULY 1st, 2025.

## JOIN US THIS SUMMER!!!

---

This year our program takes place **Monday, July 14th through Thursday, July 17th.**

Students will have the option to stay in one of our residence halls or commute to and from campus each day.

**Residence Hall Program** - Drop off Monday, July 14th at 9:00am and pick up Thursday, July 17th at 1:00pm.  Cost is $900. Meals and evening activities included.

**Commuter Program** - Drop off each day at 9:00am, pick up Monday, Tuesday and Wednesday at 4:00pm, Thursday at 1:00pm.  Cost is $400. Lunch is included.

Check out each of our exciting programs below and select the one that is most interesting to YOU!

* [School of Business - Business Boot Camp](#accordion1)

  Experience a week of challenging, informative, and fun activities at Molloy University's Business Boot Camp!

  Students will be exposed to various business disciplines with professors who have experience in *Accounting, Entertainment and Sports Management, Finance, Marketing Analytics, Artificial Intelligence, and much more*!

  You will learn how to swim with the sharks at our renowned Shark Tank competition.  You will learn how to make financial decisions during our Buy, Sell, Hold Trading competition with candy recommendations being the name of the game!  You'll need to put your thinking caps on for the Quiz Bowl competition, where students team up to make sure that they are not the weakest link!  These are just some of the interactive activities that will help you choose your own career path.

  Get ready for the countdown to reserve your spot at Molloy University's Business Boot Camp!

  [Register and Pay HERE!](https://admissions.molloy.edu/register/businessbootcamp2025)
* [The Barbara H. Hagan School of Nursing and Health Sciences - Compassionate Healthcare Academy](#accordion2)

  Engage in exciting hands-on learning inside and outside the university classroom to understand the science of healthcare in nursing, cardiovascular technology, nuclear medicine technology, respiratory care, speech language pathology (speech therapy) and audiology.  Learn how clinicians are making a difference supporting diverse patients in each of these disciplines and experience the opportunity to self-identify a future career path.

  [Register and Pay HERE!](https://admissions.molloy.edu/register/compassionatehealthcare2025)
* [School of Education and Human Services - Change Makers Who Care (An Academy for Aspiring Teachers and Social Workers)](#accordion3)

  If you want to teach or work with youth in any capacity, this program is for you!  This hands-on, interactive week of activities will allow you to explore various youth-serving careers, including social work and teaching.  You will create learning materials that foster cultural responsiveness and social awareness.  Join us to develop an appreciation for fulfilling and impactful careers that transform individuals and communities.

  [Register and Pay HERE!](https://admissions.molloy.edu/register/changemakers2025)
