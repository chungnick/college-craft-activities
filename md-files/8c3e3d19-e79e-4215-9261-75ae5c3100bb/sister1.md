## Unleash Your Creative Power with Our World-Class Training

Are you excited about acting for the camera, passionate about musical theatre, looking to audition with confidence, or wanting to bring your creative vision to life on screen?

The Academy offers a variety of world-class summer training programs to ignite your potential, refine your craft, and advance your career. With in-person, online, and evening options, you can choose the format that best fits your interests and your schedule.

#### **Classes include:**

* Camera Acting for Film & Television
* Acting & Scene Study
* Musical Theatre
* Voice & Speech
* Auditioning for The Camera

* Social Media Content Creation
* Improvisation
* Movement
* Singing
* The Business of Acting

## Develop Your Craft with Our Passionate and Dedicated Faculty

Gain firsthand experience by working with The Academy’s legendary instructors, who will guide you to grow your skill set and get inspired. Our small class sizes ensure personalized feedback from these passionate mentors, who will share valuable insights and techniques direct from today’s entertainment industry.

## Find Inspiration In Our Supportive Community

Thrive in a safe and welcoming environment where you are free to break through personal barriers and take the creative risks needed for artistic growth. Whether you are a lifelong performer, or just getting started, you are welcome here.

On-campus housing is available for an additional fee for all programs, except the Evening Intensive and Online Intensive.

Our student housing is located next to campus in the trendy Flatiron/NoMad district of Midtown Manhattan, offering community connection, convenience, and an invaluable immersion experience for both training and living. Limited spaces are available. Find more details on the New York Academy House residence [here](https://www.aada.edu/programs/summer-program/ny-campus-program-details//#nyc-housing).

## Join The Academy Family

Follow in the footsteps of The Academy’s legendary alumni and grow your own professional network with rising talent from around the globe. Share and learn from a tradition of excellence in acting known throughout the world for 141 years.

## Frequently Asked Questions

## On-Campus Program

Is on-campus housing included?

On-campus housing is available for an additional fee for all programs, except the Evening Intensive and Online Intensive. Our student housing is located next to campus in the trendy NoMad district of Midtown Manhattan, offering community connection, convenience, and an invaluable immersion experience for both training and living. Limited spaces are available. Find more details on the New York Academy House residence [here](https://www.aada.edu/campuses/new-york-student-housing/).

Are there scholarships or financial aid available?

Scholarships and financial aid are reserved for those attending our full-time program. At the time you are accepted into our Summer Program, you will receive a tuition payment deadline. However, if necessary, we would be happy to work with you to the best of our ability to extend this deadline pending space in the program.

While we are unable to provide scholarships or financial aid for summer students, we do have several [special offers](#) you may be eligible for.

Do you offer payment plans?

We are unable to provide payment plans for our Summer Program.

Who can take classes?

For the two-week Focused Intensives (on-campus and online), students must be at least 16 years of age by May 1st in order to be eligible. For all other programs, students must be at least 18 years of age by May 1st in order to be eligible to attend. There is no upper age limit and students will be sectioned by age whenever possible.

What is the time commitment?

The amount of time spent in class varies by program; please see the details below.

Additionally, students are expected to complete work outside of the classroom—such as research, character development, and memorization. However, each student is responsible for managing their own time to meet these expectations.

**Two-Week Focused Intensive:**

* Monday - Friday
* 24 hours per week
* 4-6 hours in class per day

**Four-Week Content Creation Lab:**

* Monday - Friday
* 24 hours per week
* 4-6 hours in class per day

**Five-Week Comprehensive Foundation:**

* Monday - Friday
* 20 hours per week minimum (total number of hours may vary depending on additional electives being taken)
* 4-6 hours in class per day

**Four-Week Evening Intensive:**

* Tuesday & Thursday Evenings
* 6 hours per week
* 3 hours in class per day

**Two-Week Online Intensive:**

* Monday - Friday
* 24 hours per week
* 4-6 hours of class time

Can I take more than one program?

Yes, depending on your selections, it may be possible to enroll in multiple programs over the summer. Please contact the Summer Team to discuss your options.

Do I get a certificate?

For all on-campus programs, you will be awarded an official Certificate of Completion on your final day of classes. Those enrolled in the two-week online intensive will receive an electronic Letter of Completion upon the successful completion of the program.

Do you require an audition for the Summer Program?

No, an audition is not required. Instead, a decision will be made based off your completed application. Please be sure to include as much detail and information as possible so the committee can make the most informed decision.

## Online Program

How will classes be taught?

Classes will be taught via Zoom. Students will receive complete setup and technical information prior to the start of their program. All Zoom links will be emailed with your class schedule two weeks prior to the first day of class.

Are there scholarships or financial aid available?

Scholarships and financial aid are reserved for those attending our full-time program. At the time you are accepted into our Summer Program, you will receive a tuition payment deadline. However, if necessary, we would be happy to work with you to the best of our ability to extend this deadline pending space in the program.

While we are unable to provide scholarships or financial aid for summer students, we do have several [special offers](#) you may be eligible for.

Do you offer payment plans?

We are unable to provide payment plans for our Summer Program.

Who can take classes?

We encourage those of all levels of training and experience to apply. We pride ourselves on meeting students of all levels, exactly where they are, tailoring our work in the classroom so each student gets the maximum benefit from their experience. Students must be at least 16 years of age to participate in the Summer Program.

What is the time commitment?

You can expect to be in class between 4 to 6 hours per day, Monday through Friday. Work will be expected outside of the classroom (research, character development, memorization, etc.), however, it is up to each student to self-manage their time and what is expected of them.

Can I take more than one program?

Yes. Students may simultaneously enroll in multiple on-campus or online programs as long as their dates do not overlap.

Will I receive a certificate?

You will receive a formal letter confirming your completion via email at the end of the program.

Do you require an audition for the Summer Program?

No, an audition is not required. Instead, a decision will be made based off your completed application. Please be sure to include as much detail and information as possible so the committee can make the most informed decision.

## Special Summer Offers

These limited-time offers are designed to help you make the most of your Summer experience at The Academy.

### Early-Bird Housing Offer: Reserve Now and Save

Upon acceptance, students will receive 10% savings off the cost of living on-campus if both Tuition and Housing are paid in full by **Sunday, March 15, 2026**. No additional steps are necessary.

(In the event of cancellation before the start of program, students taking advantage of this offer should note that their housing fee will be non-refundable, while tuition will be covered under the normal program refund policy.)

### Welcome Back Offer

Are you an alumnus of the Summer Program, a current Two-Year Conservatory student, or a graduate of The Academy? We’d like to welcome you back for Summer 2026 with 10% off the cost of your program tuition.

  
(Please note: only students who have successfully completed a previous Summer Program or Two-Year Conservatory Program - or are currently enrolled in the Two-Year Conservatory Program - are eligible.)

### Full-Time Conservatory Tuition Credit

Many students who start their training with The Academy's Summer Program have such a rewarding experience that they choose to continue their studies with our full-time Two-Year Conservatory Program.

  

To support this transition, students who successfully complete the Summer Program and are later accepted into the Two-Year Conservatory Program will receive a credit for their Summer Program tuition, which will be applied to their Conservatory training costs. This tuition credit is available alongside any other scholarships or financial aid the student may be eligible for through the Two-Year Conservatory admissions and audition process.

### Questions?

Reach out to our Summer Admissions department via our [Admissions Department contact form](https://www.aada.edu/contact-us/admissions-department).
