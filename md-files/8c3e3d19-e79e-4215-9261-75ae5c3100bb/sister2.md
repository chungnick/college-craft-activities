# Application Management

## Welcome to AADA's *NEW* online application platform!

We recently upgraded our application system. If you have not logged into your AADA account since **September 2025**, you will need to create a new username and password by selecting **“Create an account.”**

If you have already created an account in our new system, simply click **“Log in”** to access your portal.  
  
Questions? Please email admissions@aada.edu.
