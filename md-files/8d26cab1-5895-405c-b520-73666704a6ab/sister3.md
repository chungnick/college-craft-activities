# Scholarships

Scholarships: Take Advantage of Them

The good news about being a Grizzly is that Grizzlies help each other. And we’re humbled
by the amount of support from alumni, donors and partners who are committed to helping
Grizzlies make their way through college. If you don’t think there is a scholarship
for you, think again.

## Automatic Scholarship Consideration

Great news for new students pursuing their first bachelor's degree: Your application
for admission to UM also serves as your application for the [UM Academic Achievement Scholarship](https://www.umt.edu/finaid/scholarships/mt-resident-entering-student-scholarships/default.php "MT Resident Scholarships For Entering Students") , the [Western Undergraduate Exchange](https://www.umt.edu/finaid/scholarships/non-resident-scholarships-for-entering-students/default.php#wue) program, or [Non-Resident UM Academic Achievement Scholarship](https://www.umt.edu/finaid/scholarships/non-resident-scholarships-for-entering-students/default.php).

TUITION DISCOUNT

## Western Undergraduate Exchange

Students with a 3.0+ GPA who live in WUE-eligible states are automatically considered
for the Western Undergraduate Exchange tuition award if they apply by Jan. 15, 2026.

* [Learn More About the WUE](/how-to-apply/wue.php)

#### Play Video

## Scholarships for first-year students and transfers

New students should complete the General Application in the UM Scholarship Portal
to be considered for departmental and renewable scholarships.

* [Learn More About the UM Scholarship Portal](https://www.umt.edu/finaid/scholarships/um-scholarship-portal/default.php)

UM student Siena explains UM's Scholarship Portal

## Davidson Honors College Scholarships

Our premier academic scholarships are offered through the Davidson Honors College, and include the Presidential Leadership Scholarship, Provost’s Honors Scholarship, and the Dean’s
Leadership Scholarship.

* [Learn About Honors Scholarships](https://www.umt.edu/honors-college/prospective-students/incoming-student-scholarships.php)

## Affordable for All

If you’re an in-state resident from a family with an income of $50,000 or less, UM
is dedicated to making sure your tuition is covered so you can attend tuition-free.

* [Learn more about the Grizzly Promise](https://www.umt.edu/finaid/scholarships/grizzly-promise/default.php)

## Scholarships by Department

Thousands of students receive significant scholarship awards directly from their academic
departments. The best part? One application gets you in the running.

* [Search for Academic Department Scholarships](https://www.umt.edu/finaid/scholarships/academic-department-scholarships/default.php)

## Payne Family Impact Scholarship

A $7.5 million scholarship fund, this scholarship is designed for students with a lot of need who aren’t able to get Federal Pell Grant eligibility.

* [Learn more about the Payne Family Impact Scholarship.](https://www.umt.edu/finaid/scholarships/impact-scholarship/default.php)

## Athletic Scholarships

For Grizzlies with an athletic side, UM offers athletic scholarships and degree-completion scholarships — totaling more than $4.2 million for over 300 student-athletes.

* [Intercollegiate Athletics at UM](http://www.gogriz.com/)

## National Merit Scholarship

UM participates in the National Merit Scholarship Program and offers awards of $2,000
a year to finalists who have indicated UM as their first choice for attending college.
Semi-finalists who enroll at UM receive an award of $1,000 a year.

* [Learn About the National Merit Scholarship](https://www.umt.edu/finaid/scholarships/miscellaneous-um-scholarships/default.php#merit)

## Grizzly Battalion ROTC

The University of Montana participates in the Army ROTC Scholarship program. To receive
an application for a four-year scholarship, contact your high school guidance office.
Applications for two and three-year scholarship are available directly from the UM
ROTC department.

* [Explore UM's ROTC program.](https://www.umt.edu/military-science/default.php)

## Horatio Alger Scholarships

The Horatio Alger Montana Scholarship Program provides financial assistance to eligible
high school seniors in the state of Montana who aspire to pursue higher education
at the University of Montana. For detailed information, visit the Dennis and Phyllis Washington Foundation website.

* [Dennis and Phyllis Washington Foundation](http://www.dpwfoundation.org/)
