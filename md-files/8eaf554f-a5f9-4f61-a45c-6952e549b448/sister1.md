# SUMMER YOUTH EMPLOYMENT PROGRAM

[Registration](https://form.jotform.com/250064068948160)for the 2025 City of Albany Summer Youth Employment Program (LIGHT) will be open from Monday, February 23rd at 8am through Tuesday, March 31st at 12pm (Noon) for city of Albany youth residents ages 14- 18. All eligible youth are encouraged to apply by completing the online application to begin.

## Eligibility

1. Be a City of Albany resident.
2. You must be between the ages of 14-18 by March 31st.
3. Complete the entire registration process.

## Registration Process / Phases of Registration

1. Complete the  online application between Monday, February 23rd at 8 AM – Tuesday, March 31st at 12 PM (Noon).
2. Print out and complete the payroll packet
3. Bring the complete payroll packet and additional [required documents](/DocumentCenter/View/8126) during the drop-off periods.
4. Attend and complete a mandatory orientation session.

## Things to consider

Applications will follow a first-come, first-served process, and registration does not guarantee paid work experience. **Youth applicants are required to be present during document drop-off**.

Please note, as part of the SYEP Application process, attendance at orientation is mandatory. Please check your email for more information regarding orientation. If you have any questions or need additional information, please contact our office at 518-447-5977.
