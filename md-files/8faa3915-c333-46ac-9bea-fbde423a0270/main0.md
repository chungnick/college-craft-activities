# Junior Doctors of Tomorrow Summer Program

Join our Junior Doctors of Tomorrow (JDOT) Program for an immersive journey into the world of medicine. This week-long summer enrichment program offers high school students the opportunity to explore a career in health care.

Featuring hands-on clinical workshops, interactive learning sessions, and mentorship from experienced medical professionals, JDOT equips students with the knowledge, skills, and attributes needed to succeed in medicine. **The program is limited to 30 students per session.**

This program is made possible through the generous support of the Haitian-American Chamber of Commerce of Florida, Health Foundation of South Florida, and Primary Medical Care Center.

Please note that participants are responsible for arranging their own transportation to and from campus.

Dates   
June 15-19, 2026  
 Applications will be accepted from Jan. 1 to May 1.

Location   
FIU Modesto Maidique Campus

Cost   
Free

Eligibility   
High school juniors and seniors with a minimum 3.0+ GPA

[Apply Here](https://jdot.medicine.fiu.edu/)

## Application Requirements

* 500-word essay (prompt provided in application)
* Letter of recommendation from teacher (preferably math or science) or guidance counselor who can verify student is in good academic standing with no history of disciplinary issues
* High school transcript with most current grades and GPA

\*\*Please note that incomplete applications will not be reviewed.

## Have a question?

For more information or if you have questions about our application, please contact the Academic Community Program and Partnership office at [acpp@fiu.edu](mailto:acpp@fiu.edu).
