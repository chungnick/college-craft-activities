# Summer@CSU: High School

Cleveland State University offers a wide range of summer programs that provide high school students the opportunity to get a jump start on their college education. Links to the direct program registration will be available one month prior to the program date below, if you need to sign up early please use our [Summer@CSU interest form](https://www.csuohio.edu/summercsu/summercsu-program-interest-form).

The Viking CHAMPS program is no longer offered. Please peruse our running programs below.

---

### CSU Recreation Center Summer Swimming

**Private swim lessons tailored to any level of swimmer (ages 3+)**

Our private swim sessions are for people ages 3 years and older. Instructors are ready to meet with you individually and help you reach your swimming goals. Lessons are scheduled based on pool, instructor, and participant availability. Prior to purchasing a private lesson package, please connect with an Aquatic Staff member to make sure we can properly meet your needs.

###### Learn more and register online [HERE](https://csurec.com/programs/private-swim-lessons/ "(opens in a new window)")!

**Aquatic adaptive personal training (any age)**

Water provides a low impact environment for exercise that can be accessible to anyone regardless of needs. Adaptive Aquatic personal training offers a safe environment for individualized instruction, with a qualified instructor, adapted to personal needs. Tailored for individuals with mental and physical special needs wanting to further their aquatic fitness.

###### Learn more and register online [HERE](https://csurec.com/services/personal-training/ "(opens in a new window)")!

***NEW!*** **Adaptive open swim Wednesday evening (any age)**

Our new adaptive open swim program is designed for a time to allow individuals with intellectual and physical special needs to enjoy time in the water. Under the supervision of a qualified instructor, this will allow for a safe and fun experience! Parents are welcome to get in the water and join in on the fun with no additional charge!

###### Learn more and register online [HERE](https://csurec.com/programs/learn-to-swim/adaptive-swim/ "(opens in a new window)")!

Contact information: Spencer Tussing | [s.tussing@csuohio.edu](mailto:s.tussing@csuohio.edu) | 216-802-3252

### Legacy ++ Program

This week-long program is designed for Black young women currently attending high school in Ohio who will be taking the AP Computer Science Principles (CSP) exam for college credit.

The Legacy ++ Program is supported by the National Science Foundation and aims to boost success on the AP CSP exam. Your week on campus at Cleveland State University will include mentoring by Black women in computer science, study sessions, meeting peers and learning more about the computer science industry. All participants will stay on campus and meals will be provided to help foster a peer-to-peer learning community.

**Date:** June 22—27, 2025  
**Time:** Sunday—Friday  
**Price:** Free to apply and free for those accepted into the program. Spots are limited to 50 participants.  
**Program Contact:** Dr. Becky Odom-Bartel | [r.odombartel@csuohio.edu](mailto:r.odombartel@csuohio.edu)

###### Learn more and register online [HERE](https://academic.csuohio.edu/legacy-plus/ "Legacy Program")!

### Math Corps

On Hiatus for Summer 2025. Come back next year!! High school students earn a stipend as Teaching Assistants (TAs) in our program. In the morning they serve as tutors, role models and big brothers/sisters to middle school students in a two-to-one ratio on teams led by college students. In the afternoon they enrich their own math knowledge from algebra to calculus, and also take a college-level class on either Logic or Proof.

***Details coming soon!***

### Odyssey Film Camps

The CSU School of Film & Media Arts offers four different residential or day camps for teens (age 13-18) interested in filmmaking. Participants learn screenwriting, acting, directing, cinematography and editing, and complete their own individual film projects.Learn more about the Odyssey Program at [csufilmodyssey.com](https://csufilmodyssey.com "Odyssey Film Program").

### Sport Management Institute

The Summer Sport Management Institute is a blend of classroom lectures from industry professionals, visits to a number of local sports and entertainment venues, team building projects, case studies and group discussions, all focused on the Sports and Entertainment industries. Students will network with industry professionals, learn about the variety of careers available in these industries and more! Students will also attend a Cleveland Guardians Game.

**Dates:** Session 1 July 7–8, Session 2 July 15–16

***Registration is now closed! Try again next year!***
