## 2026 WIE Aspire Recommendation Form

Thank you for agreeing to submit a recommendation form for a student who is applying to the [WIE Aspire](https://eng.umd.edu/wie/future-students/6-12-programs/aspire) summer program at the University of Maryland. The Women in Engineering Summer Program Admissions Committee will use the information that you provide to help inform our admission decision.   
  
WIE Aspire! Explore Engineering at the University of Maryland is a week-long, commuter summer program designed for rising 9th and 10th grade students who are considering engineering as a possible college major and potential career. Students will spend each day learning about the various engineering disciplines through hands-on activities, informative workshops, and conversations with faculty, staff, and students in the Clark School of Engineering.  
  
Please contact Jen Kuntz at jakuntz@umd.edu with any questions. You will receive a copy of your form responses immediately after submission to confirm your recommendation form has been received.
