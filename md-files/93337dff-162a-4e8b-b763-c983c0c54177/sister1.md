### Stipends and Subsidies

The stipend for the 2026 program is $880 per week, which is comparable to other astronomical summer internships in the U.S. Travel and housing assistance will be provided for the 2026 SASP.

### Applications

The application website will open on January 1, 2026.

[Application Website](https://stsci.slideroom.com/#/permalink/program/87644/0XGJAXVGWl)

### Visas

Students who are non-U.S. citizens will need a visa to work in the U.S. regardless of where their college or university is located. We provide assistance to qualified international applicants in the acquisition of appropriate visas and other work documents required to work at STScI. If you are accepted, our Human Resources department will contact you to provide assistance in obtaining a work visa.
