# Music Theatre (Advanced/Pre-College)

### July 17 - 28

### Arts Campus

The Summer Academies in the Arts are college and career preparatory intensives in Art, Dance, Drama, and Music focused on practical and technical skill development for experienced high school and college arts students on campus at UC Irvine.

Visit <https://www.arts.uci.edu/summer-academies> to learn more and to apply.

## WHAT is this about?

Students will advance their triple-threat skills, learning from one of the top college music theatre programs in the nation. Modeled on our undergraduate program, students experience life as a UCI Drama major.

---

## WHO is this for?

For advanced high school or college students seriously considering pursuing an education or a career in music theatre.

**We are are committed to the safety of our participants, instructors, and staff and are actively following federal, local, and University mandates related to the ongoing global COVID-19 pandemic.**
