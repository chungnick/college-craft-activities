# Apply

## Interested in TRIO Programs? Apply!

Notre Dame’sTRIO Upward Bound and Talent Search programs work in collaboration with personnel from target high schools and other community agencies in South Bend to identify, recruit and select young people who are eligible to participate in the program. Eligible participants are students from low-income families and students from families in which neither parent holds a bachelor's degree.

To explore the possibility of joining either of our programs, please complete the respective application by clicking on the appropriate button below. Applications in Spanish are available upon request. Completing the application will provide our staff with all the necessary information needed to determine program eligibility, as outlined in §645.3 of the Code of Federal Regulations (CFR). Once completed, please submit to our office. For additional questions or assistance, please feel free to contact the TRIO Upward Bound office at 574-631-5669 or the TRIO Talent Search office at 574-631-5670.

Please note: Based on grant regulations, students can only participate in one TRIO Program.
