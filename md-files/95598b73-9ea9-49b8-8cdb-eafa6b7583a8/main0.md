## Summer Seminar

### Naval Academy Summer Seminar

*For Students Entering the 12th Grade*

Summer Seminar kick-starts your Naval Academy application by giving you a taste of life at the Academy. For one week in June, you will experience first-class academic, athletic and professional training while building lifelong friendships with campers from all over the world.

**2026 Session Dates**

Summer Seminar *(rising 12th graders)*

**Session 1 (Saturday - Thursday):** May 30 - June 4, 2026

**Session 2 (Saturday - Thursday):** June 6 - June 11, 2026

**Session 3 (Saturday - Thursday):** June 13 - June 18, 2026

[More about Summer Seminar](NASS.php)

## Summer STEM

### Science Technology Engineering and Math

*For Students Entering 9th – 11th Grade*

Summer STEM at the Naval Academy is a vibrant week of problem solving, exploratory learning, critical thinking, and team building for rising 9th-11th graders interested in a future in **S**cience,**T**echnology, **E**ngineering, and **M**ath.

**2026 Session Dates**

Summer STEM *(rising  9th-11th graders)*

**Session 1 (Monday - Saturday):**June 1 - 6, 2026

**Session 2 (Monday - Saturday):**June 8 - 13, 2026

**Session 3 (Monday - Friday):**  June 15 -19, 2026 *(please note this session runs during a Federal holiday)*

[More about Summer STEM](STEM.php)
