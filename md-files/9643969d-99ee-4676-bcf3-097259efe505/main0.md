# UTSA Prefreshman Engineering Program

## Welcome to the Dr. Manuel P. Berriozábal Prefreshman Engineering Program

The mission of Dr. Manuel P. Berriozábal's Prefreshman Engineering Program (PREP) is to prepare middle and high school students for advanced studies and careers in STEM fields.

[Learn more about PREP](https://klesse.utsa.edu/prep/about.html)

## A Foundation for Bright Futures

At PREP, students gain essential skills in science, technology, engineering, and math through hands-on learning and collaboration.

[Learn more about PREP](https://klesse.utsa.edu/prep/about.html)

## Innovate, Create, Inspire

PREP challenges students to think critically and work creatively, preparing them to tackle real-world problems.

[Learn more about PREP](https://klesse.utsa.edu/prep/about.html)

## Building Connections, Shaping Leaders

PREP students are empowered to lead with access to UTSA resources, industry experts, and inspiring mentors.

[Learn more about PREP](https://klesse.utsa.edu/prep/about.html)

## Celebrating Achievement, Inspiring Growth

PREP students showcase their talents through STEM challenges, projects, and internships, gaining confidence and experience.

[Learn more about PREP](https://klesse.utsa.edu/prep/about.html)

#### Important Reminder:

The PREP program has a two-step enrollment process:

1. **Application** – Students must first submit an application to be considered for the program.
2. **Registration** – If accepted, students will receive an acceptance email with instructions on how to register.

**Please note:** Applying to the program is not the same as registering. Registration can only be completed after acceptance.

#### Recordatorio Importante:

El programa PREP tiene un proceso de inscripción en dos pasos:

1. **Solicitud** – Los estudiantes deben enviar primero una solicitud para ser considerados en el programa.
2. **Inscripción** – Si son aceptados, los estudiantes recibirán un correo electrónico con las instrucciones para completar la inscripción.

**Importante:** Enviar una solicitud no es lo mismo que inscribirse. La inscripción solo puede completarse después de ser aceptado en el programa.

## What is PREP?

PREP is the Prefreshman Engineering Program (PREP) at UT San Antonio. It is a pre-college summer program committed to delivering science, technology, engineering and mathematics (STEM) enrichment opportunities for middle and high school students.

[Learn more about PREP](https://klesse.utsa.edu/prep/about.html)
