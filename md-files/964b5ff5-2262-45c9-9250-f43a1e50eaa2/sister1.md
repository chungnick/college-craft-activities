# Schedule

The 2026 schedule will be updated in spring 2026. Below is the schedule from 2025.

#### Sunday: Welcome and Arrivals

* Campers arrive on campus: check in between 1 - 3pm.
* Check-in, find dorms, explore campus, meet your roommates.
* Welcome dinner & icebreaker games
* Lesson on Introduction to Circularity
* Evening orientation and RA group meetings in dorms
* Nighttime activity

#### Monday: Class Introduction and Columbia River Gorge Field Trip

* Introduction to camp and the circular economy
* Guest speaker: [Jim Fitzpatrick](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jim-fitzpatrick/) with [Conservation International](https://www.conservation.org/)
* Depart for a field trip in the Gorge!
* Pack lunches, dinner on way home
* Evening activities

#### Tuesday: Class, Speakers and sunset at Council Crest

* Guest Speakers: Lauren Zimmerman Onstad with [Portland BPS](https://www.portland.gov/bps), Scott Welch with [Loopt Foundation](https://www.loopt.org/), Pallavi Pande with [Dtocs](https://dtocs.com/),
* Lesson on ideation & prototyping
* Team time
* Evening visit to Powell’s Books and sunset viewing at Council Crest

#### Wednesday: Site Visits & Learning

* Site visits:
  + [Looptworks](https://looptworks.com/)
  + [Repair PDX](https://www.repairpdx.org/)
* Picnic lunch
* Team time
* Dinner with young alumni

#### Thursday: Mentors & Work Time

* Speaker: [Admissions](/offices/admissions/), [Jessica Kleiss](/live/profiles/125-jessica-kleiss), Environmental Studies Professor at  LC.
* Lessons on Lean Canvas business models
* Team time with mentors help to prepare for poster session
* Evening activities

#### Friday: Presentation Day!

* Poster presentations
* Q&A and Reflection time
* Ice cream social & departures
* End of Camp 12pm
