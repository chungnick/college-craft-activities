## Welcome to the NSS spUN Debate Program

**The National Space Society (NSS) spUN Debate Program** is an exciting vehicle for high school and university students to become informed of the space industry, an economy that is projected to reach $3 trillion dollars by 2050, You may have a place in this industry!

Interested in an international competition to hone speech, debate, and leadership skills, and learn about the quickly evolving space industry and your place in it ?

The spUN Debate Program may be the answer!

Involvement in the program is a great enhancement for a resume and career development.

## The NSS spUN Debate Program strives for:

### Education

NSS fosters worldwide debate to encourage and advance young adult critical thinking and communication skills. Debate spurs learning and conversation on the challenges ahead in the use of space!

### Diversity, Inclusion, Collaboration

NSS is dedicated to involving a diverse range of students and ideas, speaking from all backgrounds. We applaud differences in language orientation, cultures, and world lenses!

### Awareness

NSS is raising awareness among young adults about space issues and global challenges, empowering them to develop and implement solutions to allow beneficial progress on and off world!

We are people from around the world dedicated to the creation of a spacefaring civilization. We are an independent nonprofit nonpartisan educational membership organization.
