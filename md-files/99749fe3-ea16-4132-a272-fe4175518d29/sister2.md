# National Youth Science Camp Application (US and International Applicants)

* [Home](https://www.nysacademy.org)
* [Programs](https://www.nysacademy.org/programs/)
* [National Youth Science Camp (NYSCamp)](https://www.nysacademy.org/programs/nyscamp/)
* National Youth Science Camp Application (US and International Applicants)

**The deadline to submit the application for the 2025 National Youth Science Camp is February 28, 2025, for US Applicants.**

**International Applicants’ deadline dates vary by country and this information can be found on this [page](https://www.nysacademy.org/nyscamp-information-for-international-applicants/).**

Two key points:

* You should submit your application ***days or weeks* BEFORE** the deadline. This will ensure that your recommender has sufficient time to upload their recommendation letter. Please be sure to also notify your recommender in advance.
* The form does **NOT** save automatically. We recommend preparing your responses in a separate document first, and then copying and pasting them into this form when you are ready to submit. To see requirements for responses and upload, please visit this [page](https://www.nysacademy.org/programs/nyscamp/2024-nyscamp/).
