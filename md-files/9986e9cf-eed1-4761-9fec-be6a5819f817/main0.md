## WHAT IS NASH U?

**Nash U** is a virtual college and career readiness workshop series open high school seniors. Participants gain confidence navigating their college application process through 13 workshops on topics ranging from college lists, application support, and financial aid. Nash U supports each participant in developing the tools they need to thrive on their own unique journey and prepares them to lead with purpose in their communities and beyond.

**The Nash U community** is all about giving and receiving support as you and your peers go through the college application journey together. This program aims to build a network of care and shared learning.

## WHO SHOULD APPLY?

Must identify as a woman or gender-expansive person of color and be a high school senior.

Applicants must be available **virtually once a week** on Saturdays.

* Saturdays, November 15th - January 30th

There are no academic or skill criteria for admission into our programs

## HOW DO YOU APPLY?

The application for this cycle for Nash U is currently **closed.** The next cycle of applications will go live in the Fall of 2026.

For more questions, reach out to Charlie Ambrose at charlie@sadienash.org

## MORE DETAILS

* Nash U **runs from October to January**
* Participants are eligible to receive an **end-of-year stipend up to $260** based on how many workshops they attend
