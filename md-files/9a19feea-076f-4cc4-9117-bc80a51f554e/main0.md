## Summer Programs - July 14-18, 2025

FREE weeklong day programs for rising high school juniors and rising seniors, hosted on the University of Oregon Eugene campus, where high school students learn about various subjects from distinguished University of Oregon faculty members through fun, social and interactive activities.

[**REGISTER HERE**](https://apps.ideal-logic.com/UOSAIL?key=V1C9-239VG_K9KH-5PTF_6a8990382d3b)

[Learn how to get high school credit by participating in SAIL programming!](https://fyp.uoregon.edu/sail/summer/high-school-credit)

## Summer Programs

July 14 - 18, 2025.

Our programs run Monday through Friday, 8:30 am to 4:30 pm. We are working on 2025 program options and will post updates soon!

**Step 1:** [**Review Eligibility**](https://fyp.uoregon.edu/sail/summer/eligibility)  
**Step 2:** [**Review Program Options**](https://fyp.uoregon.edu/sail/summer/program-options)  
**Step 3: Register! (Link to come)**

Programs are open until filled.

[Program FAQs](https://fyp.uoregon.edu/sail/summer/day-faqs)

*If you need accessibility assistance to apply, contact [sailstaff@uoregon.edu](mailto:sailstaff@uoregon.edu).*
