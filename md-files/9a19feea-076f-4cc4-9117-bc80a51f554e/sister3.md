# SAIL Scholarships

## SAIL Scholarships

**Applications Close March 1, 2025 at 5 p.m.**

SAIL participants may apply for SAIL scholarship opportunities if they plan to attend the University of Oregon. See the information below for eligibility requirements and how to apply. If you require any accessibility assistance, contact [sailstaff@uoregon.edu](mailto:sailstaff@uoregon.edu).

[**SAIL General Scholarship**](#general)

[**SAIL Housing Scholarship**](#housing)

## SAIL General Scholarship

***Applications Close on March 1 at 5 p.m.***

The SAIL program awards scholarships to high school SAIL students who are pursuing higher education at the University of Oregon. The SAIL scholarship amounts will be based on level of participation in the SAIL program, financial need, and available funding.

Scholarships range from $1,000 to $2,000 for a one-time award and will be disbursed at the time of entry into UO.

[Qualifications & Instructions](https://fyp.uoregon.edu/sail/scholarships/general)

[Apply Now](https://fyp.uoregon.edu/sites/default/files/2025-01/2025-sail-general-scholarship-application-new.docx)

## SAIL Housing Scholarship

***Applications Close on March 1 at 5 p.m.***

The SAIL program is honored to award a scholarship for UO on-campus housing for one year. This housing scholarship will be for high school SAIL students who are pursuing higher education at the University of Oregon and are Pathway Oregon Scholarship recipient. The SAIL Housing Scholarship amount is based on the average cost of a common room type and Carson limited meal plan. Eligibility for housing award will be based on level of participation in the SAIL program, financial need, and available funding.

The SAIL Housing Scholarship will provide $15,000 toward room and meal charges for students beginning fall term who will be living in UO on-campus residence halls.

[Qualifications & Instructions](https://fyp.uoregon.edu/sail/scholarships/housing)

[Apply Now](https://fyp.uoregon.edu/2025-sail-housing-scholarship-application-0)
