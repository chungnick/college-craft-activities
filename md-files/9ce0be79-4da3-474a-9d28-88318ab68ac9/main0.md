## 6-Week Program

Rising high school juniors and seniors also have the option to participate in our signature 6-week program. Students can complete up to two courses during this program. In addition, students are supported with weekly faculty roundtable discussions, student success coaching, tutoring and regular check-ins on course progress by first-year professional advisors.

### Program Dates:

* Summer Session I - May 18 - June 29, 2026
* Summer Session II - July 6 - August 15, 2026

### Program Cost:

* Virtual Coursework - $1,155 plus $30 registration fee
* On Campus Coursework - $385 per contact hour plus $30 registration fee

## On Campus Courses

On campus coursework is available to pre-college students who are interested in completing pre-calculus and calculus coursework. A placement exam is required for all students in order to enroll in math coursework at UMass Lowell.

[Apply and Register for On-Campus Summer Coursework](https://go.uml.edu/register/precollege6week)
