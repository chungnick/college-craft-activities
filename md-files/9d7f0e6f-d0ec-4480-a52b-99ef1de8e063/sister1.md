# 2026 Child Health Research Internship

**PROGRAM DEADLINE: February 01, 2026 at 11:59 PM(Midnight)**

#### Description

Welcome to the 2026 Child Health Research Internship application page!

The Department of Pediatrics at the University of Colorado School of Medicine and the Colorado Child Health Research Institute at Children's Hospital Colorado host a summer research program for high school seniors over the age of 18, college students, graduate students and first-year medical students. The internship will run from Monday, June 1, 2026 - Friday, July 31, 2026.

Eligible students will work in the laboratory with members of the Department of Pediatrics faculty at Children's Hospital Colorado and the University of Colorado on the Anschutz Medical Campus. This competitive program offers motivated students the opportunity to gain research experience related to child health. The program is structured for students to:

* participate in a weekly research lecture series, presented by faculty members
* collaborate with laboratory experts and/or clinical science experience experts under the guidance of faculty mentors
* present their research at the completion of the summer internship
* connect with fellow interns throughout the program

Each intern will be provided a stipend of $4,400 to assist with travel and housing costs. The stipend will be paid out over two payments at the end of June and end of July.

Housing is not included as part of the internship, and interns are responsible for arranging housing while in Colorado. Please visit the CU Anschutz Campus Parking & Transportation webiste for more details: https://ucdenver.edu/parking

#### Requirements

* Students must be a \*high school senior over the age of 18, an undergraduate student, graduate student or a student completing the first year of medical school.
* Students must be available for the entire nine-week internship (start dates may be delayed for school schedules).
* \*High school seniors must be Colorado residents. Students who are under the age of 18 before the internship starts on June 1, 2026 will not be eligible.

Applications will be open between December 1, 2025 - February 1, 2026.

Letters of recommendation will be accepted through Monday, February 2, 2026 (11:59 PM - MST Midnight).

All applicants will be notified of their status no later than Friday, March 13, 2026 (11:59 PM - MST Midnight).
