# English Proficiency Requirements

In order to be admissible, applicants are required to demonstrate English language proficiency by meeting one of the following criteria:

| Test | Score |
| --- | --- |
| **TOEFL iBT (taken before Jan. 2026)** | 80 (no part score under 17) Send via [ETS](https://www.ets.org/toefl/test-takers/ibt/scores/sending)using institution code 6681 and leave department code blank |
| **TOEFL iBT (taken after Jan. 2026)** | 4.0 (no part score under 3.0) Send via [ETS](https://www.ets.org/toefl/test-takers/ibt/scores/sending)using institution code 6681 and leave department code blank |
| **TOEFL iBT Home Edition** | 80 (no part score under 17) Send via [ETS](https://www.ets.org/toefl/test-takers/ibt/scores/sending)using institution code 6681 and leave department code blank |
| **TOEFL Essentials** | 9 Send via [ETS](https://www.ets.org/toefl/test-takers/ibt/scores/sending) using institution code 6681 and leave department code blank |
| **IELTS** | 6.5 (no part score under 6.0)  Send through your [IELTS Testing Center](https://www.ielts.org/en-us/for-test-takers/results) |
| **DuoLingo (DET)** | 110 or higher Send via [DuoLingo English Test](https://englishtest.duolingo.com/edu) |
| **ACT** | English section score of 21 Send via [ACT](https://www.act.org/content/act/en.html)using institution code is 1356 |
| **SAT** | Reading + Writing score of 540 Send via [College Board](https://www.collegeboard.org/) using institution code is 6681 |
| **Advanced Placement Exams: English and Composition** | Mark of 4 or higher  Send via [College Board](https://www.collegeboard.org/) using institution code is 6681 |
| **Advanced Placement Exams: Literature and Composition** | Mark of 4 or higher  Send via [College Board](https://www.collegeboard.org/) using institution code is 6681 |
| **Cambridge International Exams: English Literature A Level** | Grade C or higher Upload a copy of your GCE Exam Certificate in your [Admissions Profile in MyUI](https://myui.uiowa.edu/) after you apply |
| **Cambridge International Exams: English Literature AS Level** | Grade C or higher Upload a copy of your GCE Exam Certificate in your [Admissions Profile in MyUI](https://myui.uiowa.edu/) after you apply |
| **Cambridge GCSE and IGCSE Exams: English or English First Language** | Grade C or higher Upload a copy of your GCSE/IGCSE Exam Certificate in your [Admissions Profile in MyUI](https://myui.uiowa.edu/) after you apply |
| **International Baccalaureate: HL English A: Literature** | Mark of 5 or higher Send an IB Certificate or Diploma via[International Baccalaureate](https://www.ibo.org/) |
| **International Baccalaureate: HL English A: Language and Literature** | Mark of 5 or higher  Send an IB Certificate or Diploma via[International Baccalaureate](https://www.ibo.org/) |

## Other Ways to Show English Proficiency

**Citizen of a Country in which English is the Official Language****reflected on your application.**  
(Antigua and Barbuda, Australia, Bahamas, Barbados, Belize, Bermuda, Botswana, Cameroon, Canada, Cayman Islands, Dominica, Gambia, Ghana, Grenada, Guyana, Ireland, Jamaica, Kenya, Lesotho, Liberia, Malawi, Namibia, New Zealand, Nigeria, Sierra Leone, South Africa, St. Christopher & Nevis, St. Lucia, St. Vincent & the Grenadines, Tanzania, Trinidad & Tobago, Uganda, United Kingdom, Zambia, Zimbabwe)

**Attend a U.S. high school (located within the U.S.) for a minimum of 3 years and will graduate from a U.S. high school.**  
Upload a copy of your transcript from your U.S. high school in your [Admission Profile in MyUI](https://myui.uiowa.edu/) after you apply.

**Transfer students who complete 30 s.h. of transferable credit with a minimum 2.50 GPA from a U.S. institution.**  
Have official transcripts sent from every U.S. college and university you have attended.

**American Degree Transfer Program (ADTP) transfer students who complete 30 s.h. of transferable credit with a minimum 2.50 GPA.**  
Upload a copy of your transcript for every college and university you have attended outside of the U.S. in your [Admissions Profile in MyUI](https://myui.uiowa.edu/) after you apply.

**Transfer students who complete the equivalent of the University of Iowa's Composition I and Composition II while physically attending a U.S. institution.**

Have official transcripts sent from every U.S. college and university you have attended. Online courses are not accepted.

*Please be advised, an applicant may still be held to the on campus* [*English Placement Exam*](https://lllc.uiowa.edu/academic-areas/english-second-language/credit-program) *(EPE) depending on which criteria is used to meet the English Proficiency Requirement. More information regarding criteria to waive the EPE is listed in the following section.*

## English Placement Evaluation (EPE)

The on-campus [English Placement Evaluation (EPE)](https://lllc.uiowa.edu/academic-areas/english-second-language/credit-program) is an exam taken prior to course enrollment. All students will be held to this EPE unless an applicant meets one of the following criteria:

* Citizenship in a Country in which English is the Official Language and your first language is English.
* Attend a U.S. high school (located within the U.S.) for a minimum of 3 years and will graduate from a U.S. high school.
* Transfer students who complete 30 s.h. of transferable credit with a minimum 2.50 GPA from a U.S. institution.
* American Degree Transfer Program (ADTP) transfer students who complete 30 s.h. of transferable credit with a minimum 2.50 GPA.
* Transfer students who complete the equivalent of the University of Iowa’s Composition I and Composition II while physically attending a U.S. institution. Online courses are not accepted.
