# Summer Camps & Community Programming

* [Home](https://www.lvc.edu/)
* [About LVC](https://www.lvc.edu/about/)
* Summer Camps & Community Programming

From expert music instruction to educational summer camp opportunities and Colloquium lectures to our expansive Vernon and Doris Bishop Library, Lebanon Valley College provides community resources to inspire creative and intellectual thinking.

## Community Music Institute

Built on the tradition of music at Lebanon Valley College, the Community Music Institute is a member of the National Guild for Community Arts Education and has a long-standing reputation for providing quality music instruction to student of all ages.

[Community Music Institute](/about/camps-community-programming/community-music-institute)

## Actuarial Science Camp

LVC has had a successful Actuarial Science Program for more than 40 years, and we’ve run this summer camp for more than three decades.

[Actuarial Science Camp](https://www.lvc.edu/about/camps-community-programming/actuarial-science-camp/)

## Computer Programming with AI Camp

Our week-long, immersive program is designed for rising high school juniors and seniors who want to explore the future of technology and discover whether a career in computer programming or data science is right for them.

[Computer Programming with AI Camp](https://www.lvc.edu/about/camps-community-programming/computer-data-science-summer-camp/)

## Health & Biomedical Sciences Camp

The Health & Biomedical Sciences Camp provides high school students with a window into the scientific basis of human health.

[Health & Biomedical Sciences Camp](/about/camps-community-programming/health-biomedical-sciences-camp)

## NextGen Healthcare Camp

The NextGen Healthcare Summer Camp is designed to introduce participants to a variety of health science programs currently offered at LVC. Rising high school sophomores, juniors, and seniors will have an opportunity to explore exercise science, athletic training, clinical mental health counseling, nursing, and physical therapy.

[NextGen Healthcare Camp](https://www.lvc.edu/about/camps-community-programming/nextgen-healthcare-camp/)

## Speech-language Pathology Camp

This three-day, overnight summer camp is for rising high school juniors and seniors to explore speech-language pathology at LVC.

[Speech-Language Pathology Summer Camp](https://www.lvc.edu/about/camps-community-programming/speech-language-pathology-summer-camp/)

## Summer Music Camp

Lebanon Valley College’s Music Department offers a summer music camp for students entering grades 9 through 12.

[Summer Music Camps](/about/camps-community-programming/summer-music-camp)

## Athletic Camps

Lebanon Valley College offers a variety of youth-development camps and clinics throughout the year.

[Check Out Athletic Camps & Clinics](https://godutchmen.com/sports/2017/10/18/camps.aspx)
