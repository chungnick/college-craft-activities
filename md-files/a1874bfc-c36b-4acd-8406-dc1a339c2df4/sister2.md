## Application Requirements

### **THE ONLINE APPLICATION IS NOW OPEN!**

* The university no longer requires the COVID-19 immunization for non-healthcare students.
* If you have questions or concerns regarding ESAP, don’t hesitate to get in touch with us via email at [pennesap@boldsummers.com](mailto:pennesap@boldsummers.com).

December 1, 2025

January 31, 2026

January 31, 2026

**Final Application Deadline:**

February 28, 2026

## **The following application materials are required:**

* **Online application**

* **Essay** (two page limit):

* + *Prompt:* Discuss an experience or a concept that has led you  
    to apply to the Engineering Summer Academy at Penn. What excites you about the field of engineering? What sort of impact on your community, the world, society, etc. would you like to make with your skills gained from this program? Lastly, describe any skills, software and programming proficiencies, or talents that will make you successful in the ESAP program.

* **OFFICIAL high school transcript**: sent directly from your school via Parchment  

  #### Instructions for Official Transcript Submission

  + Have your school counselor/administrator send your official transcripts to “University of Pennsylvania – School of Engineering and Applied Science – Graduate Admissions & ESAP” through the Parchment online platform.
  + If your school does not use an electronic transcript service, please have your school counselor/administrator send your official transcripts to [pennesap@boldsummers.com](mailto:pennesap@boldsummers.com)

* **Limit to 1 Letter of Recommendation**: Contact information for your recommenders must be submitted on the online application (name, phone number, and email address). They will then be emailed instructions for submitting their letter online. Your letter of recommendation must be written by a STEM teacher. Any educator of Math, Science, Engineering, and or Technology  in elective or core subjects is acceptable.

* **$90 non-refundable application fee**

* \*\***Standardized Test scores**: PSAT, SAT I/ II, ACT, AP, TOEFL, DET, IELTS

\*\***Standardized test scores are optional**.  If you have taken any of the above tests and feel that your scores will strengthen your application, please submit them.  Upload a copy of your scores in the “Standardized Tests” section of the online application.  International students: please refer to our [International Student page](https://esap.seas.upenn.edu/admissions/international-students/) regarding TOEFL scores and English language requirements.

Official high school transcripts can be sent via parchment.
