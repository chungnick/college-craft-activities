# Teen Wellness Summit on Mental Health

#### Mark your calendars for the 2026 Teen Wellness Summit!

[Get notified when registration opens](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfEv6tqVq-uto6LH89vKNbJZbxkSoSUilt5Lsez2ZMUes0qwA/viewform?usp=dialog)

[2025 Summit Print Program](https://sep.ucsf.edu/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/25TWC_summit_program-PRINT-VERSION.pdf)

##### **When**: Saturday, March 21, 2026

##### **Time**: 9:00am – 3:00pm

##### **Location**: UCSF Mission Bay Campus: Genentech Hall

##### **Schedule**

* FREE Breakfast
* Morning Breakouts
* FREE Catered Lunch and Resource Fair
* Afternoon Breakouts
* More details to come!

## ORGANIZED BY TEENS FOR TEENS!

## Educational

FREE educational event open to all Bay Area teens in 9th-12th grade

## Lunch

Raffle, goodie Bags, FREE catered lunch from SF Kebab

## Experts

Presentations from UCSF researchers, faculty, and local experts on teen mental health

## Community

Meet and interact with teens from across San Francisco

*The Teen Wellness Summit welcomes everyone to our events. If you need a reasonable accommodation to participate in this event because of a disability, please contact* [*rachel.harris@ucsf.edu*](rachel.harris@ucsf.edu)
