2026 Teen Wellness Summit Interest Form

Thank you for your interest in participating in the UCSF Teen Wellness Summit on Mental Health! The summit date will be on **March 21st from 9AM-3PM**, and it will be taking place at **UCSF Mission Bay Campus: Mission Hall and Genentech Hall.**  
  
At the Summit, you will:  

* Learn from top professionals and groundbreaking researchers at UCSF.
* Gain tips and resources to address and improve your mental health.
* Join us for a day of learning and fun along with a free catered lunch and goodie bags!

**Sign up here to get notified when registration opens in January!**

Hope to see you there!
