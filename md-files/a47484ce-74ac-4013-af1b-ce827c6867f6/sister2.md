## Prize tiers

500 schools will receive prize packages of Samsung technology and classroom resources.

## Promising STEM Innovation

400 schools will win $1,000 in prizes for demonstrating outstanding potential with their STEM innovation.

## Semi-Finalists

100 Semi-Finalists will be named, with 90 schools earning $10,000 in prizes and 10 moving to the next round.

## National Finalists

10 National Finalists will be invited to an in-person pitch event, with seven teams earning $50,000 in prizes for their schools.

## National Winners

Three National Winners will win $100,000 in prizes for their schools.

## Employee Choice Award

Samsung employees will select one school to win an additional $10,000 in prizes.

## Community Choice Award

One school selected by online public voting will earn an additional $10,000 in prizes.
