# CURE Research Internship Frequently Asked Questions

1. **Can I still apply if I do not meet the eligibility requirements?**  
   Yes, you can apply, we will consider all applicants but priority will be given to students who meet eligibility requirements.
2. **Does the CURE Program provide housing and/or transportation for interns?**  
   No. Students are responsible for their own housing accomodations and transportation to and from their internship site.
3. **How many recommendations are required?**  
   Each applicant is required to submit at least one recommendation form. Recommendation letters will not be accepted, please have your recommender complete the [online recommendation form](https://ucsf.co1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_2bKq3zHHP0MlDue). Two recommendation forms MAX per student applicant.
4. **Who can be a recommender?**  
   One recommendation form must be completed by a current or previous **science teacher**. We will accept additional recommendation forms from a counselor, mentor, or other teacher.
5. **Is this a paid internship?**  
   Yes, students will receive a stipend for the successful completion of the internship.
6. **Can this internship be done remotely?**  
   No. All programming will be in-person.
7. **Can I take summer school or simultaneously participate in another summer program?**  
   No. Students are required to participate fully during the entire 8 week program (40 hrs/week, Monday- Friday). Participants will need to attend their internship during normal business hours and attend Friday College Seminar. Please note: Absences will be cause for removal from the program.
8. **My family is going on a vacation, can I still apply?**  
   Yes, you can still apply. Please note that priority will be given to students who are available for the entire 8 week program commitment. Absences will be cause for removal from the program.
9. **What are the important dates to remember?**
   * Parent/Guardian orientation: Thursday, May 21, 2026 (in person, mandatory)
   * Student orientation: Thursday, June 11, 2026 (in person, mandatory)
   * Program dates: Monday, June 15- Friday, August 7, 2026 (in person, mandatory)
10. **Where will the internship program take place?**  
    Internship sites are located across multiple UCSF campuses in San Francisco, CA and Oakland, CA.
11. **When should I be hearing back after I submit my application?**  
    We will try our best to notify applicants of their status by the mid to late February. Please note as we receive hundreds of applications each cycle, we will not be able to notify applicants who **do not** move on to the interview portion of the admissions process.
