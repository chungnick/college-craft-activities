* Pharmacy Scholars Summer Program

**The Summer Scholar program will return June 22-23! Registration information coming soon.**

Mercer University’s Pharmacy Scholars Summer Program (PSSP) is a great opportunity for high school students in metro Atlanta to gain exposure to the pharmacy profession. The two-day, daytime program engages students with hands-on experience and learning about pharmacy careers.

You’ll be led through fun and interactive activities by our current pharmacy students and meet faculty, staff, and alumni.

### Eligibility Requirements and Criteria for Participation

* Must be a rising high school sophomore, junior, or senior.
* Must have an interest in pharmacy or pharmaceutical sciences.

### Registration Process

A completed registration consists of the following items:

* Completed [registration form](https://connect.mercer.edu/register/PharmacyScholarsSummerProgram)
* $125 participation fee (submitted through the registration form)

**The deadline to submit a registration form is May 2, 2025.** Successful registrants will be notified by email of their registration into the Pharmacy Scholars Summer Program within a week of submitting their complete registration form.

### Participation Fee

Program participation fee is $125.00, which includes lunch each day, instruction materials, laboratory supplies, t-shirt, and field trip transportation expenses.

### Additional Requirements

All participants are required to submit proof of current health insurance and signed permission forms prior to program participation *(required documents will be provided after registration and program participation fee are received).*
