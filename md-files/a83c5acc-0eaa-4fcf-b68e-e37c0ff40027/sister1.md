# PharmD Degree Program

## PharmD Degree Program

Consistently ranked among the top pharmacy programs nationwide, we train learners to think critically and scientifically to advance the safest, most effective treatments.  
  
[Apply now](/admissions/steps)  
[Ask a question](/ask)  
[Take a video tour](https://ucsfpharmacy.university-tour.com/)

* ### Integrated 3-year program

  Our innovative year-round curriculum organized by organ systems and disease states launches your career in just three years.

  [Learn about our 3-year program](/curriculum?utm_campaign=sop-internal&utm_medium=web&utm_source=pharmd-cards&utm_content=3-year)
* ### Top-ranked pharmacy school

  Our PharmD program consistently ranks among the top three programs in the country (U.S. News and World Report).

  [Read the report](https://pharmacy.ucsf.edu/news/2024/04/ucsf-school-pharmacy-ranked-1st-california-2nd-nation?utm_campaign=sop-internal&utm_medium=web&utm_source=pharmd-cards&utm_content=top-ranked)
* ### Excellence at our core

  Professionalism, respect, integrity, diversity, and excellence (PRIDE) are at the core of UCSF’s values.

  [Learn about our values](https://pharmacy.ucsf.edu/about/dei?utm_campaign=sop-internal&utm_medium=web&utm_source=pharmd-cards&utm_content=diversity)
* ### Residency and fellowship success

  Nearly 70% of our graduates go on to advanced training. Annually, we rank among the top schools in the nation for number of students matched to residencies.

  [Discover our performance](/about/grad-rate-performance?utm_campaign=sop-internal&utm_medium=web&utm_source=pharmd-cards&utm_content=residency-success#post-grad-plans)
* ### #1 in research funding

  Our school leads the nation in NIH research funding, giving our students the unique opportunity to learn from the best of biomedical scientists.

  [See our success in research](https://pharmacy.ucsf.edu/news/2024/03/school-pharmacy-leads-nih-funding-44th-straight-year?utm_campaign=sop-internal&utm_medium=web&utm_source=pharmd-cards&utm_content=nih-1st)
* ### Dual degrees

  Unlock boundless opportunities​—​our curriculum is a spring­board for additional training. Combine your PharmD with a PhD or the MS in artificial intelligence and compu­ta­tional drug discovery and development.

  [Learn more about dual degrees](/about/advanced-training?utm_campaign=sop-internal&utm_medium=web&utm_source=pharmd-cards&utm_content=dual#other)
