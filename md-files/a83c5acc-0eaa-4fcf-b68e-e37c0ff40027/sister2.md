# Home

## Coding the Future of Medicine

Administered by the UCSF School of Pharmacy, our innovative Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence and Computational Drug Discovery and Development program trains the next generation of scientists in advanced computational tools and methods.

[Learn how to apply](https://pharm.ucsf.edu/aicd3/admissions)

## Discover Our Program

* ### [Admissions](/aicd3/admissions)

  The School of Pharmacy's MS in AICD3 degree program provides a welcoming environment for a wide range of diversity in its student population, including students with disabilities.
* ### [Curriculum](/aicd3/curriculum)

  Our program is a full-time, in-person commitment at UCSF's Mission Bay Campus, consisting of five full-time quarters.
* ### [Fees](/aicd3/fees)

  UCSF Student Financial Services external site (opens in a new window)  offers opportunities for students to apply for federal loans or federal work-study jobs through the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) external site (opens in a new window) .

* ### [Executive Committee](/aicd3/executive-committee)

  The Executive Committee determines and implements policy for the good of the program, determines program membership, and represents the interests of the program to universities and agencies.
* ### [Industry Advisory Board](/aicd3/industry-advisory-board)

  The AICD3 program is guided by an industry advisory board that includes partnerships with marquee companies like Genentech, Gilead Sciences, Pfizer, and Amgen. The partners teach, help with financial support, and secure internships and mentorships for students.
* ### [Students](/aicd3/students)

  Meet the talented learners in our program who make up the next generation of scientists to apply data-driven methods to the discovery and development of new drugs and therapies.
