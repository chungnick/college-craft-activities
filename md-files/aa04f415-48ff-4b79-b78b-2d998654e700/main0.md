## Experience the coolest profession around

Looking to explore the world of business, professional services, or accounting and gain hands-on experience with real projects and clients? Discover your passions and pave the way for your future career with CLA’s high school internship program.

Designed for students aged 16 to 18, this summer internship provides the opportunity to develop new skills, understand your strengths, and get paid for your hard work. Internship opportunities are available in select locations nationwide starting in June.

LAUNCH YOUR INSPIRED CAREER

Gain experience and skills

CLA's internship program helps high school students get a head start in considering career options, understand the opportunities available in professional services, and work with real projects and clients before pursuing additional education.
