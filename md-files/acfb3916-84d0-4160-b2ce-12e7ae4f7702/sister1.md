## Eligibility

The Theatre Arts Division of the NHSI is a competitive program. Application decisions, made by the Admissions Committee, are based on ability and merit. Enrollment is limited.

* Applicants must be **sophomores** or **juniors** in high school (10th or 11th grade) who will be rising into their junior or senior years of high school during their summer at Northwestern. *Juniors receive priority consideration.*
* Applicants should rank academically near the top 30% of your high school class.
* Meet a high standard of character, responsibility, and intellect
* Able to provide evidence of experience and/or substantial interest in the theatre arts.

## How to Apply

In this video, **George Keating**, Director of the Theatre Arts Division, outlines some tips and pitfalls of the application process. We highly encourage you to watch this video **before** completing step 3 below!

1. Submit the [National High School Institute Application](https://nhsi.northwestern.edu/apply-now/) including $60 application fee.
2. **Receive a confirmation email from NHSI with instructions on how to submit supporting materials,** completing your application. *You should receive this confirmation email within 2-3 business days of submitting the online app.*
3. **Submit the supporting materials as outlined to complete your application.** These include a resume, two essay responses, a letter of recommendation, current high school transcript, and standardized test scores (optional). Students applying for the MT track are required to submit a third essay.*Further details will be included in your confirmation email.*
