Below are the important dates for the 2026 summer session.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Sunday, June 21 | Students move in (morning) |
| Saturday, June 27 | All students remain on campus; no overnights away. |
| Friday, July 3 | Family Day *Families participate in classes, and then students leave with their families. The campus is closed for the weekend.* |
| Sunday, July 5 | Students return from the long weekend by 7 p.m. |
| Saturday, July 11 | Alumni Day *All students remain on campus; no overnights away.* |
| Saturday, July 18 | College Day *Families participate in programming, and then students can leave for an overnight with their families. The campus remains open.* |
| Saturday, July 25 | All students remain on campus; no overnights away. |
| Sunday, July 26 | ASP Graduation at 11 a.m. *Students depart campus by 1 p.m.* |
