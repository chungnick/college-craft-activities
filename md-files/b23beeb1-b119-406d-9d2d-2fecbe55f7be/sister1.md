# Tuition and Financial Aid

Tuition, Fees, Financial Aid, Scholarships, Withdrawals and Refunds

## Tuition and Fees

Tuition and fees vary by program and course length. Specific costs are listed on [each course card in the Explore Courses page](https://my.ctd.northwestern.edu/myctd/s/course-offerings). Program and event pages show tuition ranges or related fees. For policies and an overview, visit the Tuition & Fees page.

[Learn More About Tuition and Fees](/tuition-and-fees)

## Financial Aid

Center for Talent Development (CTD) offers need-based financial aid for the majority of its programs.

[Learn More](/financial-aid)

## Scholarship Opportunities

In addition to financial aid, CTD has a several scholarship opportunities for students. In addition, CTD works with the Jack Kent Cooke Foundation to inform students about the Young Scholars program.

[Learn More](/scholarships)

## Withdrawals and Refunds

Please read about the requirements for requesting a withdrawal and determining eligibility for a refund. Policies vary by program.

[Learn More](/withdrawals-and-refunds)
