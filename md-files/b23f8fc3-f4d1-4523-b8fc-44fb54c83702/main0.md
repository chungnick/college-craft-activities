## Information for Prospective Applicants

Hello, prospective student, and welcome! We invite applications from mathematically talented students aged 13 to 18. Each summer, we enroll approximately 65 new students and 55 returning alumni. New students are admitted via a thorough, selective [application process](/admission/how_to_apply/). If you're looking for information about admissions & financial aid, you're in the right place.

### Stay Updated

If you'd like to receive emails about important dates and deadlines during the Mathcamp application process,
please [sign up for updates](https://appsys.mathcamp.org/get_updates/).

### Questions & Answers

Do you have questions about the program or the application process? Come to our next live online Q&A session. [Learn more about Math Jams.](/about_mathcamp/mathjams)

### Interested in applying to Mathcamp?

Ready to get started? Applications for Mathcamp 2026 are now open! The application deadline is February 23, and all applications received by the deadline will receive equal consideration. We look forward to reading your application!

[Start your application now.](https://appsys.mathcamp.org/)
