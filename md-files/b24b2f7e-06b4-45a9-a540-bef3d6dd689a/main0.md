## ***NURPH applications are now closed as of March 1st, 2025.***

### **Currently, NURPH only accepts applications from students of the [Chicago Math and Science Academy](https://www.cmsaonline.net/), [Northwestern Academy](https://academy.northwestern.edu/), and [Holy Trinity High School](https://holytrinity-hs.org/).**

High school students from sophomore to senior levels are eligible to apply, with priority given to juniors.
