# Cost & Registration

When you register for a *FIRST*®program, you're investing in student's futures and giving them the skills they'll need to succeed today and tomorrow.

Investment costs vary by program and offering with additional cost considerations varying by region and event participation.

[Choose a Program](https://www.firstinspires.org/programs/choose-a-program?hsLang=en)

[View Available Grants](https://www.firstinspires.org/programs/team-grant-opportunities?hsLang=en)

## *FIRST* Registration Options

Please use the tabs below to view the registration costs, associated materials, and available training options.

*All prices are listed in USD. Applicable sales tax and shipping not included.*

* *FIRST* Robotics Competition

  Ages 14-18 | Grades 9-12

  ### *FIRST* Robotics Competition

  Starting with a Kit of Parts, teams of high school students design, program, and build industrial-sized robots to play an action-packed game, released in January. They compete on a themed field as part of a three-team alliance in the spirit of *Coopertition*®. In 360-degree learning guided by adult mentors, each *FIRST*® Robotics Competition team also creates a team identity, raises funds to meet its goals, and advances appreciation for STEM in its community.

  [About *FIRST* Robotics Competition](https://www.firstinspires.org/programs/frc/?hsLang=en)

  [Getting Started Info](https://www.firstinspires.org/programs/frc/get-started?hsLang=en)

  What's included:

  + Official *FIRST* Robotics Competition team number for new teams.
  + Kit of Parts including the Kickoff Kit, Virtual Kit, and *FIRST* Choice credits.

  + Participant/alum access to career discovery opportunities, connections to scholarships/employers, and a place in the *FIRST* community for life.
  + Access to team roster to register team members including mentors and participants.
  + Participation at one (1) Regional Event for Regional Teams and participation at two (2) District Events for District Teams.

What payment methods do you accept?

### You can pay for *FIRST*registration and materials through a few different payment methods.

1. **Purchase Order (PO):** You must upload your purchase order through the payment screen on the *FIRST* Dashboard.

***FIRST* cannot accept a commitment letter from a School or School District.**

2. **Credit Card:** *FIRST* accepts Visa, MasterCard, American Express and Discover.  Credit Card Payments can be processed through the payment screen on the *FIRST* Dashboard.

3. **Check:**(USD funds only drawn on a U.S. bank).  Please make sure that all checks remitted state your program and team or account number to enable swift & proper crediting.

Learn more about [*FIRST* payment options and terms](/payment-terms-refund-policy?hsLang=en).
