## Do You Want to Become Upward Bound?

Upward Bound seeks students that are committed to their education and are highly motivated in their pursuit of attaining a college education and achieving their career and professional goals. The Academic Year and Summer Programs are rigorous and designed to motivate and prepare students for post-secondary education.

##

### How to Join

Submit the online application form & requested materials through the link below. If you have questions regarding the application procedures, contact Ms. Thomasina Coley at [upwardbound@pobox.upenn.edu](mailto:upwardbound@pobox.upenn.edu) or (215)-898-3185.

[Get Started >](https://upenn.co1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_1ZIqvLSXmQxqrky)

### Qualifications

Qualifications to join the program include:

* Applicants must attend one of Upward Bound’s participating high schools:
* **Overbrook | Parkway – West | Sayre | West Philadelphia**
* Must be a US citizen or permanent resident
* Preferably in 9th or 10th grade when they apply
* Low – income **and/or** first-generation-to-college
* Maintain a 2.5 GPA or higher
* Demonstrated motivation to pursue a college degree after high school

### Application Process

1. Complete the Interest Form
2. Student & Guardian Interview
3. Complete application & submit required documents, e.g. Proof of Income
4. Review & Acceptance
