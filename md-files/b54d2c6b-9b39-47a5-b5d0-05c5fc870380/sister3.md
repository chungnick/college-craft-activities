# Handbooks & Manuals for Academic Contests

## CONTEST HANDBOOKS & MANUALS

The UIL publishes manuals and handbooks as free downloads to provide guidelines for each UIL academic contest. Handbooks are intended for contestants, coaches and contest directors. All participants should be familiar with the contents. Most are updated every year or substantially revised every few years as needed.

[Accounting Handbook 2025-26](https://www.uiltexas.org/files/academics/Accounting_Handbook_2025-2026_draft.pdf)

[Literary Criticism Handbook 2025-26](https://www.uiltexas.org/files/academics/Literary_Criticism_Handbook_2025-26.pdf)  
[Ready Writing Handbook 2025-26](https://www.uiltexas.org/files/academics/Ready_Writing_Handbook__2025-2026_draft.pdf) (updates being made to reflect scoring guide implemented 8/26)  
[Social Studies Handbook 2025-26](https://www.uiltexas.org/files/academics/Social_Studies_Handbook__2025-2026_draft.pdf)  
[Spelling & Vocabulary Handbook 2025-26](https://www.uiltexas.org/files/academics/Spelling_Handbook_2025-2026_draft.pdf)
