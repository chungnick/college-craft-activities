[## CSSEC Summer Internship for high school students Learn more](https://med.stanford.edu/cssec/about-our-internship.html)

Welcome to the Cardiothoracic Surgical Skills and Education Center. The center provides classroom and hands-on basic-to-advanced cardiac surgical training through our Cardiothoracic Resident Training Program and our [High School Student Stanford Summer Internship](https://med.stanford.edu/cssec/about-our-internship.html) that provides education to those interested in the field of medicine and surgery.

## Our Goals

Founded by the [Department of Cardiothoracic Surgery](http://ctsurgery.stanford.edu/) at Stanford, the goals of the Center is to provide:

- education of and insights in cardiothoracic surgery to **high school level students** considering a career in medicine and surgery through the [Cardiothoracic Surgical Skills and Education Center Summer Internship](https://med.stanford.edu/cssec/about-our-internship.html)

## [Cardiothoracic Surgical Skills and Education Center Summer Internship](https://med.stanford.edu/cssec/about-our-internship.html)

for high school students

The [Cardiothoracic Surgical Skills and Education Center Summer Internship](https://med.stanford.edu/cssec/about-our-internship.html) is designed to educate high school students considering careers in science, medicine, and public health in cardiovascular anatomy and physiology as well as surgical techniques that are used in medical training.

## Instructors

Members of the Faculty in the Department of Cardiothoracic Surgery

Residents in training in the Department of Cardiothoracic Surgery

## Make a Donation

Please consider partnering with us to further our [High School Student Stanford Summer Internship](https://med.stanford.edu/cssec/about-our-internship.html). We strive to make this program more readily accessible to all individuals, including those with less opportunity, through a robust scholarship and assistance program. We also wish to expand the scope and impact of the program by adding several new educational modules. See our [Donations webpage](https://med.stanford.edu/cssec/donations.html) for more information.
