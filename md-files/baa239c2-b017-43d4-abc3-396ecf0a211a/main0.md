# High School National Championship Tournament

The HSNCT is NAQT’s premier high school event. The tournament is held at the end of each school year and features the top quiz bowl teams from across the United States and, increasingly, the world.

Teams [qualify for the HSNCT](qualification.jsp) by their performance at [regular-season tournaments using questions supplied by NAQT](/schedule/?hs=true).

The [2026 HSNCT](/hsnct/2026/) will be May 22–24 (Memorial Day weekend) at the Atlanta Marriott Marquis and Hilton Atlanta.

The [2025 HSNCT](/hsnct/2025/) was held at the Atlanta Marriott Marquis and Hilton Atlanta on May 23–25. Livingston High School’s A team (Livingston, New Jersey) was the champion out of 336 teams.

For the 2018 HSNCT, the 20th edition of the tournament, NAQT released [20for20](/hsnct/20for20/), a series of 20 interviews with people with significant past and present roles in the HSNCT, in the 20 weeks leading up to the tournament.

If you have any questions about the HSNCT, please write to [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#543c273a3720143a3525207a373b39).
