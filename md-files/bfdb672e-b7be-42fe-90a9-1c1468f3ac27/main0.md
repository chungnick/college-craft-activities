## Physics Summer Program

Discover the wonders of physics at our one-week, hands-on program. Designed for high
school students who have not yet taken a physics course, this engaging program includes
exciting topics like Newtonian Mechanics, Light, Electricity, and Quantum Mechanics.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Dates:** | July 13 - 17, 2026 |
| **Application Deadline:** | May 1, 2026 |
| **Application Instructions:** | Student fills out Applicaton Form (see below).  Teachers fill out Recommendation Forms (see below).  Students need to share the link to this page with the teachers who need to fill out their recommendation forms. |
| **Cost:** | $650 |
| **Eligibility:** | Students entering 10th, 11th or 12th grade in the fall 2026.  Students with a strong interest and background in math and science are encouraged to apply. This program is not for students who have taken physics. |
| **Lab Hours:** | |  |  | | --- | --- | | Monday - Thursday | 9:00am - 3:00pm | | Friday | 9:00am - 2:00pm | |
| **Instructors:** | Rich Gearns Joseph Coffey |
| **Housing:** | Housing will not be offered for Summer 2026 |

Each day, students will:  
Explore fascinating physical phenomena;  
Design, build, and test projects related to the day's theme;  
Take measurements and analyze results.

Participants will experience hands-on interactive activities, indoors in our physics
building and outdoors on our beautiful campus, as well as enjoy guided tours of cutting-edge
campus facilities and research labs. 

The week culminates in a Family Showcase, where students will share their projects
and discoveries!

Space is limited, so apply now to secure your spot in this dynamic adventure into
the world of physics!

 

For additional information or questions, please call (631-632-9750) or e-mail [(istem@stonybrook.edu](mailto:istem@stonybrook.edu)).
