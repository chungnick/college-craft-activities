# Costs and Aid

### [Program Dates and Costs](/costs-aid/program-dates-and-costs)

Your cost is determined by the program you enroll in, and for Summer@Brown students, the number and length of courses to be taken.

### [Application Fee Waivers](/costs-aid/application-fee-waivers)

You can request a waiver of the application fee if you meet certain criteria. Waiver requests are reviewed by the billing team only and do not influence admission decisions.

### [Scholarships](/costs-aid/scholarships)

Brown Pre-College offers a variety of scholarship opportunities for admitted students. Each scholarship type has eligibility requirements which we present broadly here and in detail at each of the links below.
