### [What is a voucher?](#)

* **What is a voucher?**
    
    
  **Answer:**
    
    
  Vouchers are codes which can be redeemed to obtain Access Codes to participate in Caribou Contests. There are student vouchers and schoolwide vouchers. Sometimes Caribou Contests gives out vouchers as part of a promotion to give students or schools the chance to try out a contest for free (after the October contest, and not counting the grade 2 level, which is already free).

---

### [How do I use a voucher?](#)

* **How do I use a voucher?**
    
    
  **Answer:**
    
    
  Vouchers can be redeemed to obtain Access Codes to participate in Caribou Contests.  
    
  See the [Vouchers](https://cariboutests.com/public/vouchers.php) page for more details.
