### [NYU Tisch Summer Series: Following Your Passion](https://youtu.be/b-h_08h-LoE)

Summer at Tisch is part of the Summer in NYC program at NYU. Tisch summer courses are open to NYU students, visiting students, and working professionals. There are [undergraduate and graduate course options](/special-programs/summer-at-tisch/programs-and-courses), as well as [non-credit courses, certificates, and intensives](/special-programs/summer-at-tisch/summer-intensives-and-non-credit-certificates).

Please read the following admissions information before starting your online application. You can find important dates on the [Tisch Special Programs Admissions Calendar](/special-programs/admissions/admissions-calendar).

### How do I apply?

### [STEP 1: CONFIRM YOUR ELIGIBILITY](#)

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **REQUIREMENT** | **CRITERA** |
| **Program Dates** | * Summer Session I: May 18 - June 30, 2026  * Summer Session II: July 1 - August 12, 2026  * Please visit our [Admissions Calendar](https://tisch.nyu.edu/special-programs/admissions/admissions-calendar) to keep track of important dates and deadlines. |
| **NYU Students** | * If you are an undergraduate or graduate NYU student interested in taking a summer course at Tisch, please register via [Albert](https://admin.portal.nyu.edu/psp/paprod/EMPLOYEE/EMPL/h/?tab). |
| **Visiting Students** | * **Undergraduate Students:** Please view our eligibility requirements [here](https://www.nyu.edu/admissions/visiting-students/apply.html).  * **Graduate Students:** You must hold an undergraduate degree and be planning to take graduate (GT) credit courses. |
| **International Students** | * Please review our additional requirements for international visiting students [here](https://www.nyu.edu/admissions/visiting-students/apply/international-students.html). |
| **Housing** | * As a visiting student, you are responsible for making your own housing arrangements for the Summer.  * If you are interested in living on campus, please see the [NYU Housing website](https://www.nyu.edu/students/student-information-and-resources/housing-and-dining/on-campus-living/application-and-assignments/applying-for-housing/waitlist-housing-application.html) for more information about submitting a waitlist housing application. |

-

### [STEP 2: PREPARE YOUR APPLICATION](#)

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Application Form** | * Fill out the online [Visiting Student Application Form](https://connect.nyu.edu/register/?id=a9f502e7-59eb-4914-a14f-93a97e0afe2d).  * Provide a valid e-mail address on the form, as all communication regarding your application will be sent via email.  * Submit the form to finalize your account setup and resume your application.  * Please note: *this is a two-part process, as submission of the initial form will create login instructions to continue.* |
| **Application** | * Once you submit the Visiting Student Application Form, you will receive a link to complete your Visiting Student Application. |
| **Graduate Application** | * Click here begin your [Visiting Graduate Student Application](https://tischspecialprograms.slideroom.com/#/login).  * Email <tisch.special.admissions@nyu.edu> with any questions |
| **Transcript** | * To expedite processing time, attach an unofficial transcript to the Academic History page of the application. Official transcripts may be e-mailed to [admissions.docs@nyu.edu](/special-programs/summer-at-tisch/admissions.docs@nyu.edu), or mailed to:        New York University |

-

### [STEP 3: SUBMIT YOUR APPLICATION](#)

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Application** | * Submit the online application and all required materials. |
| **Deadline** | * All applications must be completed and submitted by the application deadline.  * Please note that domestic and international students have different deadlines:   + International Student Deadline:     - Tuesday, March 17, 2026   + U.S. Student Deadline:     - Tuesday, May 5, 2026 (Session I)     - Tuesday, June 16, 2026 (Session II)  * Please visit our [Admissions Calendar](https://tisch.nyu.edu/special-programs/admissions/admissions-calendar) to keep track of important dates and deadlines. |

-

### [STEP 4: REVIEW YOUR ADMISSIONS DECISION](#)

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Admissions Decisions** | * Visiting student admission decisions are based on the strength of an individual's academic work and life experiences.  * Please be aware that admission as a visiting student is for one term only and admission is not guaranteed for consecutive terms. Additionally, an admissions offer cannot be deferred.  * Applicants who have questions about accessing their admissions decision should contact [University Programs](https://www.nyu.edu/admissions/visiting-students/contact-us.html). |
| **Refund Policy** | * If you drop a course or withdraw from the university completely, your tuition and registration fee charges are subject to the university Refund Schedule policy. For more information on the refund policy, click [here](https://www.nyu.edu/students/student-information-and-resources/bills-payments-and-refunds/refunds-and-withdrawals.html).  * For more information about applying to summer courses, deadlines, fees, and eligibility, visit [NYU Summer](https://www.nyu.edu/admissions/visiting-students/academics/summer.html). |

-

### APPLICATION POLICIES

###### 

###### **AI USAGE**

* To make sure your application truly represents you, we strongly recommend against using AI tools.

* Your application gives us a chance to learn about you; we truly value hearing about your unique experiences, ideas, and goals.

* Please share your genuine self and let your personal story make your application stand out.

If you are having difficulty with the online Visiting Student application, please contact [University Programs](https://www.nyu.edu/admissions/visiting-students/contact-us.html) at 212-998-2292 or [university.programs@nyu.edu](mailto:university.programs@nyu.edu).

If you have questions regarding registration for Tisch courses, please contact <tisch.student.services@nyu.edu>.
