# Dates and Deadlines

Listed below are upcoming important deadlines for our programs. To see program start and end dates, visit the [individual program pages](/programs).

---

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| November  3, 2025 | Registration opens for Spring 2026 programs |
| December 1, 2025 | Applications open for Summer 2026 programs |
| December 29, 2025 | Registration closes for Spring 2026 Early College Scholars |
| January 19, 2026 | Registration closes for Spring 2026 Exploration Courses |
| February 9, 2026 | Registration opens for Summer 2026 Exploration Courses |
| March 2, 2026 | Registration closes for Spring 2026 Future Scholars Academy |
| March 2, 2026 at 4:00pm CT | Early application deadline for [High School Summer Scholars Program](https://precollege.wustl.edu/high-school-summer-scholars-program#primary) applicants |
| April 1, 2026 at 4:00pm CT | Application deadline for 2026 summer programs |
| April 8, 2026 by end of day | Admission and scholarship decisions released for summer programs |
| April 22, 2026 | Enrollment deadline for all summer programs |
| May 11, 2026 | Registration closes for Summer 2026 Exploration Courses |
| July 1, 2026 | Registration opens for Fall 2026 Early College Scholars and Exploration Courses |
| August 10, 2026 | Registration closes for Fall 2026 Early College Scholars |
| August 31, 2026 | Registration closes for Fall 2026 Exploration Courses |
