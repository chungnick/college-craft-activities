### Research Experience for High School Students

# How to Apply

**ISR Students interested in participating should contact their ISR scientist.**

## Research Experience for High School Students (REHSS)

* [Research Experience for High School Students](/cseo/programs/high-school-programs/research-experience-for-high-school-students-rehss/)
* [How to Apply](/cseo/programs/high-school-programs/research-experience-for-high-school-students-rehss/how-to-apply-rehss/)
* [Frequently Asked Questions](/cseo/programs/high-school-programs/research-experience-for-high-school-students-rehss/frequently-asked-questions-rehss/)

##### We invite **ISR students** to apply to the Research Experience for High School Students (REHSS) program in **December of their junior year**. Admission decisions take into consideration high school grades, rigor of courses taken, curriculum vitae or resume, essays, and two recommendations (one of which is your ISR teacher).
