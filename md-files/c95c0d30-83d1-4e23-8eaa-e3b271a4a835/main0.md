# 2024 Summer Science Internship

**Deadline:** Monday, March 4, 2024, at 11:59 p.m. PT

## Program Overview
The Summer Science Internship Program, sponsored by the Arthritis Foundation, provides outstanding students with the opportunity to work in leading research and clinical laboratories at the University of California, San Francisco (UCSF) and local affiliated institutions.

**Goal:** To provide hands-on experience in rheumatology and immunology, focusing on arthritis and related autoimmune diseases. Interns participate in:
*   Basic laboratory (bench) research
*   Clinical translational/epidemiological (patient outcomes-oriented) research
*   Bioinformatics (computational) research

## Important Dates
*   **Application Deadline:** March 4, 2024
*   **Internship Period:** 8-week program running between May 28 and August 30, 2024.
*   **Notification of Selection:** By April 15, 2024.
*   **Final Paper Due:** September 16, 2024.

## Eligibility Requirements
*   **Education Level:** Open to high school juniors, seniors, and college undergraduates.
*   **Age:** Applicants must be at least 17 years of age by May 28, 2024.
    *   *Note:* Applicants under 18 must provide written permission from a parent or guardian.
*   **Citizenship:** Applicants **MUST** be U.S. citizens or permanent residents with a Green Card.
*   **Commitment:** Interns are expected to work approximately 40 hours per week for a total of 8 weeks.

## Stipend & Deliverables
*   **Total Stipend:** $2,500.
*   **Payment Schedule:** Paid in two installments: half at the beginning of the internship and half upon receipt of the final paper.
*   **Poster Session:** Interns will participate in a poster session at UCSF reflecting their work.
*   **Final Paper:** A brief 2-page paper summarizing the experience must be submitted by September 16, 2024.

## Application Process
Applications and all supporting materials must be received electronically by the deadline.

1.  **Online Application:** Complete the form at [https://ucsf.co1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_9nvJZ5VFjhoYK3A](https://ucsf.co1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_9nvJZ5VFjhoYK3A)
2.  **Letters of Reference:** Two (2) letters of reference from faculty, teaching assistants, supervisors, or community members.
    *   Must be on official letterhead.
    *   Submitted as a PDF/scanned copy to `summerscience@arthritis.org`.
3.  **Transcripts:** Unofficial copies are acceptable.
    *   **High School:** Most recent transcript.
    *   **Undergraduate:** Most recent transcript (First-year undergraduates should also include a high school transcript).
    *   Submit via email to `summerscience@arthritis.org`.

## Additional Information
*   **Housing & Transportation:** No housing or transportation is provided. Interns are responsible for their own arrangements and costs.
*   **Evaluation:** Applications are competitively scored based on scientific mastery, letters of recommendation, and application responses by a subcommittee of UCSF MDs and PhDs.
*   **Contact:** For more information, email `summerscience@arthritis.org`.
