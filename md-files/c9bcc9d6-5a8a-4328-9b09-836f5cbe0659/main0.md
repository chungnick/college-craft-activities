# Dental Careers Exploration

#### **Application information                                              [Click Here To Apply](/paths/apply/)**

Apply:Nov 18, 2025 - May 2, 2026

Requirements: Online application, personal statement, one letter of reference and unofficial transcript.

[Dental Careers Exploration Flyer](/media/division-of-student-engagement-and-impact/vcu-paths/apply-box-photos/DentalCareerExplorationFlyer.pdf)

**Eligibility requirements**

Dental Careers Exploration is a summer program for Richmond area high school students who are interested in exploring a career in dentistry through interactive workshops and lab activities. ***S**tudents from outside the Metro Richmond area can also apply but will be responsible for their own housing and/or transportation to the program if accepted.***

GPA requirement: 3.0 or above. All applicants must have U.S. citizenship or Permanent Resident status.

All students must have completed 9th grade by the time the program begins to be eligible for this summer program. At the start of the program, each student should be between a rising 10th grader and a graduating senior. No applicant below or above the specified grade specifications will be considered.

**Program description**

DCE is a five-day summer program where participants explore dentistry as a health care profession.

Transportation and parking are not provided by the program.

Any materials required to participate in the program will be provided. Commitment to full attendance, including an orientation session and closing event, is required to participate.

The program is FREE and lunch is provided.

Learn about the educational requirements and job duties of each dental professional (dental hygienist, general dentist, and dental specialists)  - Lab activities - tooth carving, dental impressions, suturing, digital dentistry, and MORE!

**Dates**

July 13 - 17, 2026

**Location**

VCU School of Dentistry - 520 N. 12th St., Richmond, VA 23298
