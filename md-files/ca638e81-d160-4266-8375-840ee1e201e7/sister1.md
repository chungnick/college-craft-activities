### NYC Residential Summer

The non-refundable enrollment deposit will be credited toward the total cost of the program and is not an additional fee. ***Each*** session has a unique deposit amount and deadline based on when an admissions decision was released. The deposit due date will be included in your acceptance email. Applying to multiple sessions is possible on the application form.
