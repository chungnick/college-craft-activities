# Precollege programs for grades 4-12

## Welcome to Badger Precollege

What can young learners do at UW–Madison this summer? Just about anything. Badger Precollege offers an array of academic programs for **students ages 9-18** — from advanced learning programs and STEM classes to music clinics and college readiness conferences.

Whatever you choose, your student will enjoy the university’s outstanding facilities, resources, faculty and staff — all while getting to know the UW–Madison campus, pursuing their passions and meeting new friends. [Financial assistance](https://precollege.wisc.edu/financial-information/) is available for most programs.

International students are welcomed and encouraged to apply for our [high school programs](https://precollege.wisc.edu/international-student-resources/). We look forward to meeting your student soon!

### Badger Precollege Junior Grades 4-8

Day and residential programs designed to engage, challenge and inspire advanced learners in elementary and middle school. Students explore one area of study, build community and make lasting friendships.

* [Precollege Academic Campus Experience (PACE)](https://precollege.wisc.edu/pace/)
* [Summer Transitional Enrichment Program (STEP)](https://precollege.wisc.edu/step/)

---

### Badger Precollege Senior Grades 9-12

Immersive college-prep programs for students completing grades 9-12. Students select a course or area of interest, explore campus and learn with their peers.

* [Accelerated Learning Program (ALP)](https://precollege.wisc.edu/accelerated-learning-program/)
* [Badger Summer Scholars](https://precollege.wisc.edu/badger-summer-scholars/)
* [College Ready @ Badger Precollege](https://precollege.wisc.edu/college-ready/)
* **[Social Impact Leadership](https://precollege.wisc.edu/badger-precollege-online/#SocialImpactLeadershipOnline)**
* [Summer Arts Clinic](https://precollege.wisc.edu/summer-arts-clinic/)
* [Wisconsin International Precollege Academy](https://precollege.wisc.edu/wisconsin-international-precollege-academy/)

---

### Summer Music Clinic Grades 6-12

Programs for middle school and high school musicians providing the opportunity to learn from experienced educators, enhance their skills and create a community.

* [Learn about Summer Music Clinic](https://precollege.wisc.edu/summer-music-clinic/)
* [Senior Session](https://precollege.wisc.edu/summer-music-clinic/senior-session/)
* [Junior Session](https://precollege.wisc.edu/summer-music-clinic/junior-session/)
* [Mini Music Clinics](https://precollege.wisc.edu/summer-music-clinic/summer-music-clinic-mini-music-clinics/)

[Support Badger Precollege programs](https://precollege.wisc.edu/donate/)
