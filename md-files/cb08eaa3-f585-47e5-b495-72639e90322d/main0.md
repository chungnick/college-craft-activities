## **The 2026 National High School Mock Trial Championship**

The 2026 National High School Mock Trial Championship will be held in Des Moines, Iowa, from May 7 to 9, 2026. The first National Championship was held in Des Moines in 1984, and the Board is excited to finally be coming "home." The competition website is available [here](https://iowa2026.nhsmtc.org/).

**Future Host Sites Chosen**

The 2027 National High School Mock Trial Championship will be held in St. Louis, Missouri, from May 13 to 15, 2027.

In 2028, the National High School Mock Trial Championship will take place in Hershey, Pennsylvania, from May 4 to 6, 2028.
