# Summer Session

Why take a Summer course?  * Stay home and take your class online! * Get ahead in your studies * Satisfy Gen Ed requirements * Smaller class sizes * Take a class not offered in the Fall/Spring * Retake a course to improve your GPA * Graduate Early! |
