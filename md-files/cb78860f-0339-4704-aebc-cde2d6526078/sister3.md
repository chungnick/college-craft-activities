## Helping You Pay for College!

The Office of Financial Aid & Scholarships is dedicated to helping you successfully navigate financial aid processes.

[Contact Us Through the Rebel Success Hub](https://unlv.my.site.com/selfservice/login)

### Types of Aid

#### Scholarships

Academic-based aid that you do not repay.

[Learn More About Scholarships](/finaid/scholarships)

#### Grants

Need-based aid that you do not typically need to repay.

[Learn More About Grants](/finaid/grants)

#### Waivers

Program-specific assistance that you do not repay.

[Learn More About Waivers](/finaid/waivers)

#### Loans

Financial assistance that you must repay later with interest.

[Learn More About Loans](/finaid/loans)

#### Work Study

Need-based funding that supports employment opportunities on- and off-campus.

[Learn More About Work Study](/finaid/work-study)

#### Funding and Other Programs

Learn more about additional funding programs.

[Learn More About Other Opportunities](/finaid/funding)
