## Financial Aid is Available

Financial Aid and Scholarships is committed to helping you and your family navigate the cost of your Oakland University education. We partner with you every step of the way because this is one of the most important investments you’ll ever make.

#### Important Information

* When you [confirm your enrollment](https://www.oakland.edu/registrar/registration/enrollment-confirmation/ "Enrollment Confirmation information"), OU is able to accurately schedule and offer classes for all students.
* Stay up-to-date on [recent congressional acts](https://www.oakland.edu/financialaid/federalaidchanges/ "recent congressional acts") that will impact federal financial aid.
* Submit your [OU Annual Scholarship Application](https://www.oakland.edu/financialaid/available-financial-assistance/#tab-2 "OU Annual Scholarship Application") before March 1, 2026.
* The 2025-2026 and 2026-2027 FAFSAs are available at [studentaid.gov](https://studentaid.gov/h/apply-for-aid/fafsa "Free Application for Federal Student Aid"). The OU school code is 002307.
* 2025-2026 Financial aid offers are available to view on your MySail account. Be sure to follow the [financial aid next steps](https://www.oakland.edu/financialservices/understanding-financial-assistance/awards-and-requirements/ "financial aid next steps"). Submit a [2025-2026 Revision Form](https://www.oakland.edu/financialaid/forms/ "2025-2026 Revision Form") if you need to make changes to your expected enrollment, housing plans, or loan decision.
