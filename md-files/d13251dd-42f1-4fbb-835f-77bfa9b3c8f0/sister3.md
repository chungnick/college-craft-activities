# MITE Application

My Introduction to Engineering (MITE) is a five-day summer camp for **current high school juniors** to discover engineering through participation in an engineering team project, hands-on activities and interactions with engineering students, faculty, staff and alumni.

**Applications for MITE 2026 are now open.**

[APPLY FOR MITE](https://apps.ideal-logic.com/utexasparticipant?key=BWKT-5P785_K9KH-5PTF_cd436c7ca4bb)

## Eligibility

* Applicants must be **current high school juniors** and must **reside in the United States** at the time of application.

## Required Materials

* **Current high school transcripts** must be electronically uploaded to our Ideal Logic platform.
* Test scores are **optional**, but if available, applicants may include them in their application.

## **Academic Qualifications**

* Applicants must meet **at least one** of the following criteria by the application deadline:
  + SAT Math score of **620 or higher** (600 or higher if taken before March 2016)
  + ACT Math score of **26 or higher**
  + AP Calculus AB or BC score of **3 or higher**
  + IB HL or SL Mathematics score of **4 or higher**
  + Official college or university transcript showing **credit for Calculus I or higher** with a grade of **C or better**
  + Official high school transcript showing **credit for Calculus I or higher** with a grade of **C or better**
  + Official high school transcript showing **credit for Precalculus** with a grade of **C or better**
  + Completion of **five years or more of high school math** at or above Algebra I

*If applicants do not have test scores, they may apply using any of the other academic criteria listed above.*

## Deposit

* Once accepted into the program, participants will submit a **non-refundable $200 deposit** through Ideal Logic.

For any questions, contact [cseoutreach@utexas.edu](mailto:cseoutreach@utexas.edu).
