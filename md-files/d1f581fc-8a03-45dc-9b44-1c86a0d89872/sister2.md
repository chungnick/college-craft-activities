* ## IFAL Eligibility
* To qualify you MUST be a JUNIOR in HIGH SCHOOL IN THE SPRING!

  NC Farm Bureau IFAL Program is designed for students between 16 and 17 years of age.  Students need to be NO YOUNGER than 16 years of age NOR OLDER than 17 years of age as of April 13 of the program year.  If you have question regarding your eligibility, please call **DeShon Cromartie** at [(919) 306-6312](tel://919-306-6312/).

  **STUDENTS must COMPLETE ALL information with a parent or guardian present.  Parent must initial off on the application.**

  Applications will be accepted by your [county Farm Bureau office](https://www.ncfbins.com/member-benefits) until the end of March!  When you submit your application a copy will automatically be emailed to your County Farm Bureau.  Application status will be EMAILED to students and parents at the beginning of May prior to the IFAL Program.

  **Be careful using your school email address.**  Some schools block emails with attachments and/or hyperlinks!  You can create a free email (@gmail.com) account at [Google Create Your Account](https://accounts.google.com/lifecycle/steps/signup/name?ddm=0&dsh=S960119520:1727359728650271&flowEntry=SignUp&flowName=GlifWebSignIn&hl=en&TL=APps6eaejw4jaXohWPCL-idmnO48prBNn6MiJmCxuzWVachy83gf54bq9Z7kzsiP)!

  Additional program information is available at [NC Farm Bureau IFAL](https://www.ncfb.org/institute-for-future-agriculture-leaders-ifal/).
