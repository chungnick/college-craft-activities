# CMIMC

## WELCOME

The Carnegie Mellon Informatics and Mathematics Competition (CMIMC) is an entirely student-run organization that hosts math and computer science competitions for high school students around the world.

## NEWS

* [CMWMC](/cmwmc) happened on October 25, 2025.
* [CMIMC Math](/math) will happen at Carnegie Mellon University on March 28, 2026. [[Register now]](http://cmimc.com/register)
* [CMIMC Programming](/programming) happened online the weekend of April 18-20, 2025. [2026 Date TBD]
