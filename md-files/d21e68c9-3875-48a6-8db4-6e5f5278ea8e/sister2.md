# Schedule

Please note that these times are tentative and subject to change.

You can find the exact schedule and rooms for mini-events [here](/math/mini-events), and a map of all the relevant locations [here](https://www.google.com/maps/d/edit?mid=1r55PTB-r5O82HOb7DRuOh4y8-CwoyM0&usp=sharing).

### Note: This is the schedule for 2025. The 2026 schedule will be coming soon.

### Friday

4:00 pm  CMU Math Info Session   [Wean Hall](https://maps.app.goo.gl/c54ngMdWtX93xBmh9) 7500

6:00 pm  First mini-event     [Doherty Hall](https://goo.gl/maps/LrxeVobirgXTckRs9) 

7:30 pm  Second mini-event    [Doherty Hall](https://goo.gl/maps/LrxeVobirgXTckRs9) 

8:30 pm  Early check-in     [Doherty Hall](https://goo.gl/maps/LrxeVobirgXTckRs9) 2315

### Saturday

8:30 am  Check-in       [Doherty Hall](https://goo.gl/maps/LrxeVobirgXTckRs9) A302

9:30 am  Opening ceremony    [Doherty Hall](https://goo.gl/maps/LrxeVobirgXTckRs9), and [Porter Hall](https://maps.app.goo.gl/59thdbBue74UYXJj8)

10:00 am Team rounds     [Doherty Hall](https://goo.gl/maps/LrxeVobirgXTckRs9), [Porter Hall](https://maps.app.goo.gl/59thdbBue74UYXJj8)

10:00 am Coach talk       Posner Hall A35

12:00 pm Lunch       [Hunt Library/Baker Hall Entrance](https://maps.app.goo.gl/cfcBPxP91kfqN8Br7) 

1:00 pm  Individual rounds     [Doherty Hall](https://goo.gl/maps/LrxeVobirgXTckRs9), [Porter Hall](https://maps.app.goo.gl/59thdbBue74UYXJj8)

4:00 pm  Individual rounds end    [Doherty Hall](https://goo.gl/maps/LrxeVobirgXTckRs9), [Porter Hall](https://maps.app.goo.gl/59thdbBue74UYXJj8)

5:00 pm  Award ceremony     [Doherty Hall](https://goo.gl/maps/LrxeVobirgXTckRs9), [Porter Hall](https://maps.app.goo.gl/59thdbBue74UYXJj8)
