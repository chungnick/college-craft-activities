Go Green This Summer!

Dartmouth Precollege provides high school students the opportunity to get a head start on the college experience by exploring new academic subjects, interacting with peers from across the US and around the world, and experiencing engaging, intensive courses taught primarily by Dartmouth faculty and staff.

[Intensive Courses](/courses/campus-courses-session)

Our programs offer high school students the opportunity to explore a wide range of academic subjects in a challenging and supportive environment. Our expert instructors are passionate about teaching and are dedicated to helping students succeed. Whether you're interested in science, business, or the humanities, we have a course that's right for you.

[On-campus Engagement](/programs/precollege-summer-scholars)

Our team of experienced educators and staff work tirelessly to create an environment that fosters intellectual curiosity, personal growth and social development in our idyllic setting in Hanover, NH.

[Access from Anywhere](https://precollegeonline.dartmouth.edu/?utm_source=dartmouthprecollege&utm_medium=tile&utm_campaign=dc_yearround)

We strive to make experiencing Dartmouth accessible to students anywhere in the world, and not just in summer. Our Precollege Online courses provide a deep dive into topics with expert faculty and guest speakers on your schedule.

### Explore Courses

Summer Scholars offers a wide range of courses that highlight academic areas represented at Dartmouth. While not for grades or credits, our courses are taught at the college level and are designed to challenge you, encouraging exploration into new areas of study.

inclusivity at dartmouth

A diverse and inclusive intellectual community is critical to an exceptional education, scholarly innovation, and human creativity. The Faculty of Arts and Sciences is committed to actions and investments that foster welcoming environments where everyone feels empowered to achieve their greatest potential for learning, teaching, researching, and creating. Details of current action plans can be found in the [Arts and Sciences Diversity and Inclusion Reports and Plans](https://faculty.dartmouth.edu/dean/inclusive-excellence/inclusive-excellence-reports) and the institution-wide strategic plan [Toward Equity: Aligning Action and Accountability](https://sites.dartmouth.edu/teaaa/).
