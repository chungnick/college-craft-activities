# **Welcome to the Application Portal for UF CPET Programs**

If you are ready to apply to our programs, create an account or log back in. Please be advised that you may need to update your eligibility profile before accessing the programs you may be interested in applying for.

**Important Information for High School Programs:**  
Students, please use your own email address and name to create your SMApply account and application, DO NOT use a parent/guardian's email or name. You will need to add a parent/guardian later in the process and if the application is not under your name, it may greatly delay processing times and may affect admission to the program.

The University of Florida’s Center for Precollegiate Education and Training develops and coordinates programs to promote excellence in science, technology, engineering, arts, and math. These programs foster interdisciplinary science education throughout university research centers and forge partnerships among researchers, schools, communities, and industries. Additionally, they continually integrate research processes and product knowledge with national education goals and standards. Furthermore, these programs aim to develop the skills of research students, graduate students, and faculty to enable them to bring their work to the public.
