# High School Summer Program

### in Partnership with Stony Brook University CEWIT

### in Partnership with Stony Brook University CEWIT

### Taught by a tenured full professor

### Premium one-week program

[Register a Student](https://sunriseaitech.com/machine-learning-self-driving-summer-program-application/)

Learn From University Experts. Create Real-World Technology.

Sunrise Technology Inc., in partnership with Stony Brook University’s Center of Excellence in Wireless and Information Technology (CEWIT), offers a premium one-week high school summer program focused on artificial intelligence and self-driving technology. Students learn directly from an experienced university professor and gain hands-on experience with a patent-pending RC car platform derived from a DOE SBIR-funded research project.

**Have questions?** **Send us an email at [info@sunriseaitech.com](mailto:info@sunriseaitech.com)**

### Eligibility

Students in 9th - 12th grade

### Location

CEWIT Stony Brook University

### Duration

One week, from 9:30am to 4:30pm

### Class Size

Maximum of 24 seats per session

### Certificate

Upon completion, students are issued a certificate co-signed with CEWIT

For STEM & Non-STEM Students Alike

Whether a student is already passionate about computer science or simply curious about emerging technology, this program provides an engaging, beginner-friendly path into AI. As Sunrise Technology founder Yu Sun explains, “AI is such an up-and-coming buzz, and parents want students to be exposed to this field.” The program guarantees that exposure is meaningful, hands-on, and inspiring.

Small Classes, Individual Attention

We believe breakthrough learning happens when students feel supported. With a small class size and multiple teaching assistants, every student receives personalized guidance, accessible mentorship, and the opportunity to ask deeper questions as they explore advanced technology.

The Full Experience

Throughout the week, students move through a dynamic blend of professor-led lectures, guided computer labs, and an applied final project. By the end of the program, each participant deploys their own self-driving car model—an experience that transforms complex concepts into something tangible, exciting, and achievable.

[Enroll now](https://sunriseaitech.com/machine-learning-self-driving-summer-program-application/)

Showcase Project for College Applications

Each student completes a fully functional, personalized self-driving car project—an impressive portfolio piece they can highlight in college or internship applications. The combination of university-level instruction and hands-on engineering experience stands out in an increasingly competitive admissions landscape.

The curriculum is designed and led by a tenured university professor with extensive experience in AI and autonomous systems. Students learn real-world concepts using real-world tools, building confidence as they master advanced material in an accessible, supportive environment.
