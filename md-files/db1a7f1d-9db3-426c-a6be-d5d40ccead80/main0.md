# STEP Summer Program

The Fordham University Science and Technology Entry Program (STEP) **Summer 2025** Program is currently accepting applications and will run from **Monday, July 7th - Thursday, July 31st, 2025.**

During the program, STEP scholars will receive the following services:

* Academic enrichment for the math and science classes students will be taking during the next school year.
* Exposure to STEM-related (science, technology, engineering, and math), health, and other licensed professions.
* Mentoring and tutoring from Fordham undergraduate students.
* Advising and preparation for the academic and social rigors of high school and college life.
* Opportunities to interact and build community with other STEP scholars.
* Thursday field trips
* Service Learning and Community Based Learning week initiative

Rose Hill

**Time:** 9:15 a.m. - 4:30 PM

**Days:** Monday - Thursday

**Location:** 441 E Fordham Rd, Bronx, NY 10458

**Priority Deadline:** **Monday, May 12th 2025**

**Application Deadline:** **Monday, June 9th 2025**

[Access the Rose Hill Application](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdMPshsg1dd0IbvKAcMPwgZ3k9NM7AyTCd-yplmtKeyDnoCFQ/viewform?usp=sf_link)

Lincoln Center

**Time:** 9:30 AM - 3:40 PM

**Days:** Monday - Thursday

**Location:** 113 W 60th St, New York, NY 10023

**Priority Deadline:** **Monday, May 12th 2025**

**Application Deadline:** **Monday, June 9th 2025**

[Access the Lincoln Center Application](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfZ9li1de7mOLOvf3c2BiTNuD0Qm0d3I5nUa7zUz0qXULysog/viewform?usp=sharing)

* + Be a resident of New York State
  + Be a rising 8th - 12th grade student
  + Have approximately an 85 average in Math and Science courses and are overall good academic standing
  + **Meet the requirements for economic eligibility ([Please see eligibility chart](https://www.nysed.gov/sites/default/files/income-eligibility-criteria-21-26.pdf))**
* Thank you for your interest in the Fordham University STEP Program. To complete the application in one sitting, please have the following prepared in advance:

  + A scanned copy of your most recent report card for upload (PDF, Word, Image/Picture are acceptable).
  + The complete email address of a **math or science teacher** that will complete a recommendation form on your behalf.
  + Two short responses to the questions below.  Each response should be a minimum of 150 and no more than 500 words.
    1. What is the biggest problem that you see in your community/neighborhood? How would you solve it?
    2. What do you hope to gain from being a part of the Fordham STEP Program?
  + Resume

  **Note:** Parents will have to provide an electronic signature to verify the facts of the commitment to the STEP program.
* + Is there an application fee?
    - No, there are no fees to apply to the STEP Program.
  + Is there a cost to participate in the program?
    - Yes, there is a registration fee for the summer. Please reach out to program staff for summer pricing.
  + Are there other opportunities available for my child once they enroll as a STEP Scholar?
    - Yes, students have access to the following program components
      * Priority seating for continuing students
      * Priority seating in Kaplan SAT Program
      * Data Science/Business Analytics Internship
      * Research Program with university faculty
      * Fordham University college credit bearing course
  + How are the days structured during the STEP Summer Program?
    - **Monday - Wednesday:**  scholars will attend morning math and science classes followed by an elective class in the afternoon.
    - **Thursday** - Field Trips
  + Are there other academic offerings if I am not taking a math or science class in the summer or academic year?
    - Yes, for students who either are not taking a math/science class in the summer or academic year **or** are taking a math/science class at school that STEP does not offer, there will be a course available for those students.
  + What is the attendance policy?
    - Due to the limited number of sessions, STEP scholars are required to be present for **all** sessions and classes. There is a limit of 2 excused absences before membership in program is jeopardized.
