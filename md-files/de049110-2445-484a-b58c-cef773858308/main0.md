# Pre-Baccalaureate at Barnard

Our 7-Week Credit-Bearing Pre-College Experience

## The Pre-Baccalaureate Program

***July 6 - August 14, 2026***

Earn college credit this summer at Barnard! Get the chance to learn from renowned Barnard faculty alongside undergraduates in this commuter-only program. Rising high school juniors, seniors and gap year students are eligible to apply.

[Apply for Summer 2026](https://connect.barnard.edu/apply/)

How It Works

### Program Structure

Students enroll in a 3-credit course taught by Barnard’s renowned faculty. Each course runs for seven weeks, offering a fast-paced and rigorous academic experience on our campus. Outside of the classroom students can explore New York City, participate in college readiness workshops, and engage in career exploration panels.

[Program and Course Offerings (Summer 2025)](/pre-baccalaureate-program)

Affordability

### Tuition & Aid

Our office is dedicated to helping students achieve their dream of spending a summer at Barnard. With rolling admissions, we make it easy for students to begin planning early, and we’re proud to offer a limited amount of need-based financial aid to eligible students.
