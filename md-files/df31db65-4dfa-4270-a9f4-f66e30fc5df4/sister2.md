# STEM

## CEAT Discovery Days

The College of Engineering, Architecture and Technology (CEAT) Discovery Days provides
High School Students the opportunity to explore various engineering disciplines through
activities and presentations. The discovery days are held on Fridays in June and July
from 8:30am - 4:30pm at the OSU Stillwater Campus.

### Various disciplines are:

```
 
```

**June 6, 2025**

Electrical and Computer Engineering  
Electrical Engineering Technology/Mechatronics and Robotics

```
 
```

**June 13, 2025**  
Construction Engineering Technology

Civil and Environmental Engineering

**June 27, 2025**

Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering

Mechanical Engineering Technology

**July 11, 2025**

Biosystems Engineering

Chemical Engineering

**July 18, 2025**

Architecture  
Architecture Engineering

**July 25, 2025**

Fire Protection and Safety Engineering Technology  
Industrial Engineering and Management

Includes light breakfast, lunch and a t-shirt.

Space is limited to 30 students per session.

$30 per student for a single day.

$120 per student for all 6 days at a discount.

[Register Here](https://orange.okstate.edu/register/CEAT_discovery_days_2025)

\*Registration will close Tuesday prior to each Discovery Day session or when session
is full.

\*Information email will be sent out Sunday prior to the Discovery Day which they are
registered for to the student's email.

### Required Forms

* [Minor Form](/stem/summer-camps/supporting-docs/minor-waiver-form1.pdf)
* [Conduct Form](/stem/summer-camps/supporting-docs/conduct-form2.pdf)
* [Media Form](/stem/summer-camps/supporting-docs/minor-media-form3.pdf)
* [Medical Form](/stem/summer-camps/supporting-docs/combined-medical-form4.pdf)

All four forms should be filled out and brought with the student to their first Discovery
Day or emailed to our email below prior to their attendance.
