## Engineering Adventure

Students will learn Engineering Design Principles with interactive and fun project
based hands-on learning STEM activities. Registration includes refreshments, all STEM
supplies and a t-shirt. Engineering Adventure camps are geared for rising kindergarten through rising 5th grade students unless noted otherwise. Only 1 registration per camper for Engineering Adventure.
