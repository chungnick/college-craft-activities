# Cost

## Summary of Fees

* **2026 Summer Application fee -** $95
* **2026 Summer On Campus Program fee -** $6,580
* **2026 Summer Virtual Program fee -** $4,580
* **2026 Fall Intensive Application fee** - $75
* **2026 Fall Intensive Program fee -** $2,480

## Payment Methods

To provide the fastest, most convenient, and secure way to pay, we are now accepting payment online via credit card from our accepted participants for the program fee. Those accepted will receive an e-mail with a secure URL link to the CSI payment page.

## Special Thanks

We extend our heartfelt gratitude to our long-time donors, Atul Dhablania and Incha Kim, whose generosity has made a lasting impact on our students. Your contributions have provided invaluable opportunities for aspiring learners, allowing them to participate in our programs and gain experiences that shape their futures.

Because of your support, talented students—many of whom may not have had access otherwise—have been able to explore their passions, develop critical skills, and connect with mentors who inspire them. Your dedication not only eases financial barriers but also fosters a community of learning, growth, and innovation.

Thank you for believing in our mission and in the potential of every student we serve. Your generosity continues to empower the next generation, and we look forward to sharing the incredible stories made possible by your support.

## Atul Dhablania and Incha Kim
