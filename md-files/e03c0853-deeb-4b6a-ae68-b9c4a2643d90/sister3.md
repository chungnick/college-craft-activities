## We're here to help!

We know that for most students, financial aid is a vital part of the college experience and decision process. We're here to provide the resources and guidance you and your family need before, during, and after your time at the University of Denver.

Have a question? Call us at 303-871-4020, email us at [finaid@du.edu](mailto:finaid@du.edu), or drop by University Hall room 255.

#### Our Phone and Drop-In Hours:

### DU School Codes

Both the [Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA)](https://studentaid.gov/h/apply-for-aid/fafsa) and [CSS Profile](https://cssprofile.collegeboard.org/?excmpid=vt-00231) require school identification codes when completing the application.

* **[FAFSA](https://studentaid.gov/h/apply-for-aid/fafsa): 001371**

  Determines your eligibility for need-based federal and state financial aid.
* **[CSS Profile](https://cssprofile.collegeboard.org/?excmpid=vt-00231): 4842**

  Determines your eligibility for need-based institutional financial aid (undergraduate students only).
