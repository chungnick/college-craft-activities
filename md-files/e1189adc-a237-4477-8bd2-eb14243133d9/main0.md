Barrett Summer Scholars helps academically talented and motivated high school students prepare for success at Arizona State University and Barrett, the Honors College. Students engage in college-level coursework and with a community of peers across the state.

## Elective course: Integrated Approaches to Health Care

For Barrett Summer Scholars, the College of Health Solutions offers an elective course to 10th grade students. The course, Integrated Approaches to Health Care, introduces students to a variety of health care disciplines and settings. Current and futuristic concepts of health care are introduced, as well as multiple health care professions. By the end of the course, students will appreciate the diverse and changing nature of the health care system and the professions within it.

**Dates:** June 9-13, 2024  
**Location:** In person at the Downtown Phoenix campus  
**Ages/grades:** 10th grade  
**Application opens:** Applications have closed for the 2024 program.
