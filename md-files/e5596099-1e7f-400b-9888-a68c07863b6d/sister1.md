# How to Apply

**Important Dates:**

* 2026 Program Period: June 22, 2026 – August 14, 2026
* 2026 Program Application Period: NOW CLOSED
* Application Review Period: December 16, 2025 – February 28, 2026
* Participant Selection Notification: March 1–15, 2026

Before starting the application process, please review the [FAQ](https://neurosurgery.uw.edu/education/summer-student-programs/faq "FAQ") information for answers to frequently asked questions.

**Important Requirements for Summer Program Applicants:**

* Applicants must be at least 18 years of age or older by the first day of the program (due to minimum age requirements in facilities). *No exceptions can be made.*
* Undergraduate students may apply if they are at least 18 years of age and older by the first day of the program.
* Graduate students are not eligible.
* Medical students are not eligible.
* Applicants must be authorized to work in the US. Visa holders are not eligible.
* Applicants will be required to provide their GPA and Stem GPA on the application. A minimum of a 3.2 GPA for both are required to apply.
* Selected participants will be required to have immunizations and TB test results which are current through the program end date.
* A limited number of program-sponsored housing slots are available to applicants originating from outside of the area.
* Transportation to/from Seattle, meals, and local transportation while participating in the program are the responsibility of the applicant.
* Participants must be available to work at least 40 hours every week for the duration of the entire 8-week program.

**Required Application Materials:**

1. **Letter of Interest:** Addressed to the Selection Committee, this letter should express your interest in STEM (science, technology, engineering, and mathematics) and include a narrative explaining why you are interested in the program.
2. **Current Resume**
3. **Unofficial** **Transcript**
4. **At least one letter of recommendation**: This must be from a faculty member in STEM. Recommendations do not have a specific word or length requirement.

**Application Deadline:** All materials must be submitted via the online portal by the application deadline (\*now closed for the 2026 program). Application materials sent to [nsssp@uw.edu](mailto:nsssp@uw.edu) will not be accepted.

For **applicant assistance** (application troubleshooting and frequently asked questions), please see [HERE](https://neurosurgery.uw.edu/index.php/education/summer-student-programs/faq).

For any questions or concerns, contact:

* Julie Bould, NSSSP Program Coordinator
* Email: [nsssp@uw.edu](mailto:nsssp@uw.edu)
