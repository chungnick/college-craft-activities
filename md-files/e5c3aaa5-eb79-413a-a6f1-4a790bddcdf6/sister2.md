# UMD Engineering

## 2026 ESTEEM/SER-Quest Summer Program

THIS IS A NON-RESIDENTIAL PROGRAM  
  
This program is a competitive and intensive 4-week research experience for rising high school seniors (students who will be high school seniors in Fall 2025).   
  
Eligibility:   
  
Interested students must have a minimum of a 3.2 GPA with no C's in Math and Science Courses.  
  
Have a demonstrated interest in and aptitude for a career in engineering  
  
Have a serious interest in attending the University of Maryland College Park AND majoring in engineering  
  
Must have completed Trigonometry, Chemistry, and Pre-Calc with a B or better. The student must have completed Pre-Calculus by the summer of 12th grade.  
  
Demonstrate excellent oral and written communication skills.  
  
Be available full-time for the entire duration of the 4-week summer program.  
  
A part of the application acceptance process is for the applicant-parent/guardian to participate in a virtual interview. Information with be forthcoming.  
  
Complete all components of the application by the deadline.  
  
Important Dates: Application for Best Consideration TBD  
Virtual Interviews: TBD  
Students will be notified of Acceptance: TBD  
  
At this time, we are unable to accommodate students who are not U.S. citizens or permanent residents due to limitations of University resources.
