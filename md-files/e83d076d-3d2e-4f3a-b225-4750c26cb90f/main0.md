# NM GCM Grosvenor Discovery Program

[High School](/about-us/workforce-development/high-school-programs)

## Program Overview

The Discovery Program is designed for students interested in health care and who may have limited access to opportunities to explore related careers.
The program exposes students to a range of healthcare jobs through:

* Tours
* Guest speakers
* Group discussion
* Engaging activities

In addition, the program:

* Fosters professional development
* Provides community service and leadership experiences
* Offers opportunities to network with healthcare professionals

### Meetings

Meetings are held once a month. The meetings are typically on the third Saturday of the month from 9 am to noon during the school year. Meetings take place at the locations listed below. Meeting dates, times and topics may change.

The program schedules a community partner experience with your chapter.

### Chapters

The Discovery Program offers six chapters. Each chapter has similar but different programs. If scheduling permits, there may be activities with multiple chapters. Central Chapter hosts a two year-program, all other chapters offer a one-year program for applicants entering the 2025-2026 school year.  
  
Chapter assignments: Your chapter is based on the county where your school is located. For students who are homeschooled, your chapter is based on the county where you live. Participants are responsible for their own transportation to and from these locations.

* **Cook County (City of Chicago and North Suburban Cook): Central** Chapter is hosted at Northwestern Memorial Hospital
* **Lake County: North** Chapter is hosted at Northwestern Medicine Lake Forest Hospital
* **McHenry County: Northwest** Chapter is hosted at locations including:
  + Northwestern Medicine Huntley Hospital
  + Northwestern Medicine McHenry Hospital
  + Northwestern Medicine Woodstock Hospital
* **DeKalb, Kendall, LaSalle, Lee and Ogle counties: Greater DeKalb** Chapter is hosted at locations including:
  + Northwestern Medicine Central DuPage Hospital
  + Northwestern Medicine Kishwaukee Hospital
  + Northwestern Medicine Marianjoy Rehabilitation Hospital
  + Northwestern Medicine Proton Therapy Center
* **DuPage County and Kane County: West** Chapter is hosted at locations including:
  + Northwestern Medicine Central DuPage Hospital
  + Northwestern Medicine Kishwaukee Hospital
  + Northwestern Medicine Marianjoy Rehabilitation Hospital
  + Northwestern Medicine Proton Therapy Center
* **Will County and South Suburban Cook County: South** Chapter hosted at Northwestern Medicine Palos Hospital

## Eligibility Requirements

**First-year applicants must:**

* Be at least 15 years of age.
* Be entering sophomore or junior year of high school. Suburban chapters will accept seniors on a limited basis.
* Have an academic GPA of at least 3.0 on a 4.0 GPA scale. Students with a GPA below 3.0 can explain any unique circumstances on their application, and they may still be considered.
* Commit to the program, including attending Discovery Program meetings and community partner activities. No more than two excused absences are permitted.
* Commit to follow all Discovery Program guidelines and policies.
* Submit the completed application to the appropriate chapter.

Second-year returning students (Central Chapter only for the 2025-2026 school year) must:

* Have successfully completed the first year of the Discovery Program and maintained good standing as defined by the guidelines and policies.
* Commit to the program, including attending Discovery Program meetings and community partner activities. No more than two excused absences are permitted.

## Application

**The application will open August 4, 2025**

To apply, you must submit:

* A complete [student application and transcript](/about-us/workforce-development/high-school-programs/nm-discovery-program/student-application)
  + Submit your completed application.
  + Type your essays into a separate document and copy them into the forms. The forms do not save your progress.
  + Upload your official or unofficial transcript with your application as a PDF.
* A complete [academic recommendation form](/about-us/workforce-development/high-school-programs/discovery-program-recommendation-form)
  + Your academic reference must submit the complete form by the September 2025 deadline.
  + A teacher, counselor, principal or other adult academic professional can be your academic reference.
  + Your academic reference must include your email address, **exactly** as you included it in your application.
  + The chapter committees will only consider one letter of recommendation. If additional letters of recommendation are submitted, the committee will only consider the last one.

### Deadline

You must submit your application and school transcript by 11:59 pm CT on September 29, 2025.

The program will not make any exceptions.

### Notification

Each Chapter Committee reviews all complete applications. You will get a decision email from the Discovery Program by October 31, 2025.
