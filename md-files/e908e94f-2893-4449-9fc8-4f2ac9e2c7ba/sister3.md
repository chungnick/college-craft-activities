# Make the most of your Summer!

##### Explore Summer and September Term options to help you get ahead, stay on track, or explore a new topic or major.

The University of Chicago Summer Session invites undergraduate and graduate students worldwide to explore a range of remote or in-person, courses and programs. Participants will engage with diverse ideas in classrooms led by world-class faculty. Enrich your academic journey and unlock new opportunities by joining the vibrant Summer Session community.

### Course Finder

Find remote or in-person courses that meet your criteria. Search by subject, dates, time, and modality.

[Start Exploring](/undergraduates/undergraduate-course-finder/)
