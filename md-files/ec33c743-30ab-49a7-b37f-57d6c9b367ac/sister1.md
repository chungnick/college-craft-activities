#### 2025 Health Careers Institute Dates: July 7 - July 12, 2025

### Application Procedures and Due Dates:

* **[Application can be found here →](https://connect.tdi.dartmouth.edu/apply/?sr=85bc413b-668a-439b-a3b7-9a66ca0a1083)**
* Application Deadline - **April 1, 2025 PM EST**
* Decision notices sent to students - by USPS mail: During the third week in April, 2025. A link to payment portal will be emailed to accepted student’s email address during the fourth week in April 2025.
* $700 Non-refundable deposit(this amount will be payment towards tuition) - **Due by 11:59 PM EST on Friday, May 9, 2025.**
* Remaining balance of tuition **due by 11:59 PM EST on Monday, June 9, 2025.**

\*Note: The admissions committee will not review applications unless fully completed and accompanied by the recommendation form and/or a letter of recommendation.

[Guidelines to Creating a Competitive Application](https://healthsciences.dartmouth.edu/download_file/view/1465/816)

[Recommendation Form](https://healthsciences.dartmouth.edu/download_file/view/b0ad6da4-b2e1-472d-ba79-669a2910b962/816)

### Tuition:

Tuition for the 2025 program is:

* New Hampshire residents/students: $2,950
* Out-of-State students (non-NH residents): $3,800

Limited scholarships for New Hampshire residents are available based on financial need and availability of scholarship funds.
