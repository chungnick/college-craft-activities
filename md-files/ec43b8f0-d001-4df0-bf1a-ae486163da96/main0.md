## All Students Welcome

### Continuing UCI Students

Current UCI students can make progress toward their degree year round. Choose from over 800+ courses. [Learn More](https://summer.uci.edu/students/)

### New Incoming Students

Incoming freshman students can participate in Summer Bridge, FSSP, or M.I.T.S. to get a head start on their UCI careers and prepare for the academic year. [Learn More](https://summer.uci.edu/students/)

### Visiting UC Students

Visiting students from within the United States can take courses at UCI and earn UCI units. [Learn More](https://summer.uci.edu/students/)

### Current High School Students

Rising high school juniors and seniors are invited to enroll in college courses with other college students. [Learn More](https://summer.uci.edu/students/)
