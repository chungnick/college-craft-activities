# How to Apply

[Request Information](#rmi_form_footer)

## Ready to be a Hoya?

Study anywhere, anytime, on your schedule.

We consider highly motivated applicants who are current 8th graders, Freshmen, Sophomores, Juniors, or Seniors in high school at the time of submitting their application. Students must be in good academic standing at the time of enrollment.

Admission decisions are based on a combination of factors, including high school grades, courses taken, recommendation letter/School Official Reviewer Form, and personal statement. We admit students on a rolling basis, so we encourage you to apply early.

Learn how to apply to our [Academies](#how-to-apply-to-our-programs), [College Prep Program](#college-prep-program), or [Summer College Courses](#summer-college-courses).

Application Deadlines

|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Early Bird Deadline | January 31 | For all applications submitted by this date, the application fee will be waived. |
| Final Deadline | April 15 | Applications will not be reviewed after this date. |

## How to Apply to Our Programs

### Academies

Our Academies provide an accelerated, hands-on learning experience. By submitting the Academy application, students are applying to all available academies at once. Once an application is approved, you will be able to register for any of our Academies (as availability allows). Registration is on a first-come, first-served basis and is considered complete once tuition has been paid. Apply now or explore the application process for each Academy below.

[Apply Now](https://portal.scs.georgetown.edu/portal/applications/applicationProfile.do?method=loadApplicationIndex&applicationProfileId=62208159)
