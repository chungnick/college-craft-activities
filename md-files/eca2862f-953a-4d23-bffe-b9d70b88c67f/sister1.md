# Sessions

## Course A: Intelligent Robotics (New)

This is a new standalone 4-week session, which covers everything needed to build an autonomous driving robot. Students will be introduced to all aspects of robot design: This includes software development for the microcontroller, circuit building, and robot framework construction.

**When:** July 6 – July 31, 2026 (4-Week Session)

**Time:** 9 AM – 3:30 PM (Mon-Fri)

**Cost:** $5,000 (Includes all materials, backpack, access to a laptop, light refreshments, lunch, and program T-shirt.) Scholarships are available based on financial need.

**Eligibility:** Rising high school students in grades 9th-12th and graduating 12th graders

**Prerequisites:** Introductory Programming Experience in Python

[Learn more](https://summeracademy.ics.uci.edu/intelligent-robotics-course-description/)

For our regular 2-week sessions, please see below.

## Session I

**When:** July 6th to July 17th, 2026

**Time:** 9 AM – 3:30 PM (Mon-Fri)

**Cost:** $2500 (Includes all materials, backpack, access to a laptop, light refreshments, lunch, and program T-shirt.) Scholarships are available based on financial need.

**Eligibility:** Rising high school students in grades 9th-12th and graduating 12th graders.

Select one of the three courses listed below.

A 10% discount applies to returning participants, 2nd-course registration, and UCI staff.

[### Course B: Data Analytics

The two-week Data Analytics course will explore the fundamental principles of data science. This hands-on experience helps students understand data science methods such as statistical inference techniques and statistical thinking for decision-making under uncertainty.

Learn more](https://summeracademy.ics.uci.edu/course-b-data-analytics/)

[### Course C: Cybersecurity & Cryptography

This two-week camp will explore the foundations of modern cryptography. Students will learn about the mathematical underpinnings, fundamentals of cryptography and their applications in secure communication, data integrity, and authentication.

Learn more](https://summeracademy.ics.uci.edu/cybersecurity-cryptography-course-description/)

[### Course D: Software Engineering

This course covers the breadth of software engineering, by introducing the software development lifecycle, then offering more depth into each phase of the lifecycle, such as software requirements and architecture, software testing, software design and UI/UX design, and more.

Learn more](https://summeracademy.ics.uci.edu/software-engineering-course-description/)

## Session II

**When:** July 21st – August 1st, 2026

**Time:** 9 AM – 3:30 PM (Mon-Fri)

**Cost:** $2500 (Includes all materials, backpack, access to a laptop, light refreshments, lunch, and program T-shirt.) Scholarships are available based on financial need.

**Eligibility:** Rising high school students in grades 9th-12th and graduating 12th graders.

Select one of the three courses below.

A 10% discount applies to returning participants, 2nd-course registration, and UCI staff.

[### Course E: Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AI/ML)

This two-week AI/ML camp will explore the foundations of machine learning. Students will learn about machine learning algorithms, methods, and concepts through paper and programming approaches. They will be taught methods associated with classification, clustering, and regression testing.

Learn more](https://summeracademy.ics.uci.edu/artificial-intelligence-machine-learning-ai-ml-course-description/)

[### Course F: Game Development & Design

This course introduces fundamental concepts in game creation, including prototyping, iteration, design analysis, and collaboration. Students will create two game prototypes, one team paper prototype, and one individual digital prototype before joining final project groups where they will apply learned skills and concepts to create a game together.

Learn more](https://summeracademy.ics.uci.edu/game-development-design-course-description/)

[### Course G: Data Analytics

The two-week Data Analytics course will explore the fundamental principles of data science. This hands-on experience helps students understand data science methods such as statistical inference techniques and statistical thinking for decision-making under uncertainty.

Learn more](https://summeracademy.ics.uci.edu/course-b-data-analytics/)
