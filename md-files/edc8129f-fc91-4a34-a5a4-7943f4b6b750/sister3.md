## High-impact science. Translational research.

Tumor immunology. Genetics and genomics. Cellular and molecular biology. Epidemiology. Pharmacology. Cell stress biology.

It’s all part of the study of cancer sciences. Whether you're a student or educator, consider our array of research experiences as the launchpad into a career of research.

## Healthcare professionals

University at Buffalo Health Sciences Scholarly Project (HSSP)

With the guidance of a research mentor, you’ll complete an in-depth scholarly project in an academic area of interest related to medicine or health care — for Jacobs School of Medicine Students only.

[Learn more](/https://medicine.buffalo.edu/education/md/curriculum/well-beyond/health-sciences-scholarly-project.html)

Research Internships for US Medical Students

A limited number of summer research internships are available from medical students who have completed their 1st or 2nd year at a U.S. medical school. Eligible applicants can learn more at the link below.

[Apply here](https://redcapweb.roswellpark.org/redcap/surveys/?s=C4K7YEXYP8P4XXET)

Trocaire Scholars Program

Trocaire College students will have unique opportunities to further their education and experience in their area of study, aiming to create a career pathway from student to employee, for those in the health sciences specialty.

[Learn more](/education/high-school-undergrad/trocaire-scholars-program)

Are you an educator?

Set up an educational visit at Roswell Park for your high school or college students! Educational visits allow students and educators opportunities to explore career paths in cancer research and healthcare at Roswell Park.

[Learn more](/education/high-school-undergrad/class-visits-roswell-park)
