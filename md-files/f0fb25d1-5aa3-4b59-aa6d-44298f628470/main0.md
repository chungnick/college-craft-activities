# Explore a future in health and medicine with UCSF's PITCH Program!

The **Program for the Investigation and Training in Careers in Health** (PITCH) is an immersive three-week summer program designed to inspire and empower first-generation and low-income students interested in exploring health and medical sciences careers. Through hands-on learning and mentorship, PITCH participants will:

* **Discover the healthcare landscape:** Explore connections across health and medicine to gain a deeper understanding of the field.
* **Collaborate with UCSF mentors**: to investigate critical health issues and present their findings at the PITCH Health Symposium.
* **Prepare for college success**: Receive expert guidance on navigating postsecondary options and planning for your future.

---

### Program Information

---

**Program details:**

* **Dates:** Monday, July 6 - Friday, July 24, 2026
* **Location:** UCSF campuses (San Francisco, CA)

**Eligibility:**

* Minimum 2.5 GPA (UC/CSU GPA)
* Rising 10th and 11th graders (class of ‘28 & ‘29)

---

### How to Apply

---

**Application opens:** Monday, January 5, 2026

**Application deadline:** Sunday, March 1, 2026, at 11:59 PM PST.

**Application requirements:**

1. Complete the [online student application](https://ucsf.co1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_8jGMTEV5wUMP1aK), including [three short response questions](https://ucsf.box.com/s/wxlgxricpqqd4ii90vyzm5uky35531y7).
2. Submit a copy of your unofficial transcript.
