# PITCH Frequently Asked Questions

1. **Can I apply if I don’t meet all of the eligibility requirements?**  
   All students are welcome to apply. However, only applicants who meet all minimum eligibility requirements (being a rising 10th or 11th grader and having a minimum 2.5 UC/CSU GPA) will be considered for selection.
2. **Can I apply if I live outside of California?**  
   No. Applicants must reside in California and attend a California high school to be eligible.
3. **Do I need prior experience in science or healthcare?**  
   No prior experience in science or healthcare is required.
4. **Does the PITCH Program provide housing or transportation?**  
   No. Participants are responsible for arranging their own housing and transportation to and from program sites in San Francisco, CA.
5. **Is this a paid internship?**  
   No. The PITCH Program is an unpaid educational experience.
6. **Can I participate remotely?**  
   No. All program activities take place in person.
7. **Can I attend summer school or join another program during PITCH?**  
   No. PITCH requires full-time attendance (Monday–Friday, 9:00 AM–4:00 PM) for all three weeks, as well as participation in the PITCH Health Symposium (6:00–8:30 PM). We are unable to offer exceptions or accommodations.
8. **Where does the program take place?**  
   PITCH is hosted across multiple UCSF campuses in San Francisco, CA.
9. **Will meals be provided?**  
   Lunch will be provided daily.
10. **When will I be notified about the status of my application?**  
    Applicants will receive an update on their status by the end of April.
