How to Qualify
> Participants in the International Bridge Building Contest must have
> placed first or second in a
> [Regional
> Contest](http://bridgecontest.phys.iit.edu/public/regions/).
> A Regional Contest may send a third contestant to the International
> Contest provided the first and second place winners are from the same
> High School and the third contestant is from a different High School than
> the top two finishers.
