## Study, tuition-free, at the only graduate school of music in the Ivy League.

**Degrees + Programs**

The Yale School of Music awards the following graduate-professional degrees: 

* Master of Music (M.M.)
* Master of Musical Arts (M.M.A.)
* Doctor of Musical Arts (D.M.A.)

The School also offers the following programs:

* Certificate in Performance
* Artist Diploma program, for performers holding a minimum of a high school diploma
* Bachelor of Arts/Master of Music (B.A./M.M.) program, offered in conjunction with Yale College

Fellowship opportunities at the School include:

* String Quartet Fellowship

**Key Admissions Dates**

September 15: Applications open  
December 1: Applications due  
January 13 (latest): Prescreening results emailed to all applicants  
February 16 – 22: Live auditions and DMA Exams *by invitation only*  
April 1: Admissions decisions sent by email  
April 15: Deadline for admitted students to respond to admission offers
