## Program Information & Purpose

The Pathway Programs provide **hands-on cancer research training, professional skill-building, and exposure to careers in biomedicine.**

For more information, visit: [www.uchicagomedicine.org/cancertraining](http://www.uchicagomedicine.org/cancertraining)

**Admission Criteria:** Highly competitive. Successful applicants demonstrate a passion for science, an interest in careers in biomedicine, and the maturity and drive to perform in a professional research setting.

## Recommender Instructions

Please complete the online recommendation form, accessible here:
[https://redcap.link/PathwayProgram\_Rec2026](https://redcap.link/PathwayProgram_Rec2026)

Be prepared to provide:
1.  Your name and contact information.
2.  A description of your relationship to the applicant (i.e., your qualifications as a recommender).
3.  Responses to the following questions:
    *   What do you feel the applicant has most to gain from a program of this kind?
    *   What qualities have you observed in the applicant that suggest they are prepared to meet the demands of a professional research environment?
    *   What else is important for us to know about the applicant?
