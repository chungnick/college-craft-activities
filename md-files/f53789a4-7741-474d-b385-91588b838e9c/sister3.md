# Other OSU Summer Programs

## Other summer programs on OSU Campus are:

#### K-12 Programs:

1. [OSU KidSpirit](http://kidspirit.oregonstate.edu/):Learn, grow, play, and have fun this summer! KidSpirit has an innovative youth Summer Day Camp program, which offers two-week and one-week sessions, Monday-Friday for children grades K-12. For more information visit the official website HERE.
2. [Hatfield Marine Science Center Summer Day Camps:](https://seagrant.oregonstate.edu/visitor-center/marine-education/day-camps) Summer day camps at HMSC give young people an opportunity to interact with researchers and learn about the marine environment through hands-on programs and field trips. For more information visit the official website HERE.

#### Middle School through High School Programs:

1. [4-H Summer Conference at OSU](https://extension.oregonstate.edu/4h/4-h-summer-conference): Program goals are teaching leadership and public relations skills as well as gaining subject matter knowledge in various disciplines. Participants become acquainted with Oregon State University through on-campus +tours and involvement in educational activities. Visit the official website HERE.

#### High School Programs:

1. [Summer Experience in Science and Engineering for Youth (SESEY):](https://engineering.oregonstate.edu/CBEE/about/k-12-education/summer-experience-science-and-engineering-youth-sesey) SESEY offers a unique opportunity for high school girls and minority students with an aptitude for math and science in engineering fields. For more information visit the official website HERE.
