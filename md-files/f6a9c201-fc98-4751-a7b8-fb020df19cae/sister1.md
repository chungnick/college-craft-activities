# Apply to Tech Camp!

### Tech Camp Application

We are excited to announce that all Tech Camp 2025 summer programs will be held in-person this July on the UNH Durham campus! Commuter and residential options will be offered. To be considered for one of the available slots, please complete our online application. **Space in all programs is limited.**

* Sponsored **(FREE of cost)** programs are made possible by the generous support of the **National Institutes of Health** and the **National Science Foundation**.
* **Not all programs are sponsored.** Tuition for non-sponsored programs is **$700** per week for day students and **$1,350** per week for [residential](/outreach/tech-camp/about/campus-housing "On-Campus Housing") students.
* We have a **limited number** of scholarships available for students applying to the non-sponsored programs. You will be asked to indicate on your application if you wish to be considered for financial assistance.

**Please Note: We are no longer accepting applications for the summer of 2025. There is still limited space in several programs. For more information, contact** [**Amy Booth**](mailto:amy.booth@unh.edu).

Our goal is to review applications on a rolling basis and inform applicants of their acceptance within three weeks of receiving their application.

All applicants will be informed of their acceptance status via email. Families of accepted students will receive an email with the registration information and a link to enroll. Registrations must be completed by June 1, 2025. **Submitting an application does not automatically enroll the student into the program. If accepted, families still need to complete the online registration to complete the process.**

---

**Tech Camp is committed to providing STEM education and career information to students from all ethnic, cultural and socioeconomic backgrounds.**

*All*students are invited to apply. We especially encourage applications from students in one or more of the following groups:

* Black, African American, Indigenous, People of Color
* Low income
* First generation college (neither parent is a four-year college graduate)
* Students living in rural areas

---

## Dinah Whipple STEAM Academy Application

Thank you for your interest in the Dinah Whipple STEAM Academy: An Exploration of Pre-Engineering and the Black Experience, at UNH Tech Camp! We are excited to announce **this program will be offered at no cost to participants!** This includes on-campus housing for the week. This is made possible by the generous support of Appledore Marine Engineering, LLC. **Space in this program is limited.**  To be considered for one of the available slots, please complete our online application.

**Please Note: We are no longer accepting applications for the summer of 2025. There is still limited space in several programs. For more information, contact** [**Amy Booth**](mailto:amy.booth@unh.edu)**.**

All applicants will be informed about their acceptance status via email. Families of accepted students will receive an email with the registration information and a link to enroll. Registrations must be completed by June 1, 2025. **Submitting this application does not automatically enroll the student into the program. If accepted, families still need to complete the online registration to complete the process.**
