# About UB

### Who We Serve

Upward Bound at NC State University serves 186 students annually from five high schools:

* **[Bunn High](https://www.fcschools.net/bunnhighschool)**
* **[East Wake High](http://www.wcpss.net/eastwakehs)**
* **[Franklinton High](https://www.fcschools.net/franklintonhighschool)**
* **[Louisburg High](https://www.fcschools.net/louisburghighschool)**
* **[Vance County High](https://www.vcs.k12.nc.us/Domain/1427)**

The NC State University Upward Bound Program provides students with academic and life skills through an array of **FREE** program services that include:

### Saturday Academy

* NC Standard Course of Study/EOC
* Academic Enrichment
* PSAT/SAT/ACT Prep
* High School Survival Skills
* Participation Stipend
* Free Transportation to NC State University

### Academic Skills Development

* Computer Technology
* Time Management and Study Skills
* Tutorial Assistance
* Academic Advising
* Research Activities

### College Application and Enrollment Assistance

* Application Fee Waivers
* Financial Aid Assistance
* College Tours

### Cultural Enrichment

* Theatrical Performances
* Historical Tours
* Museums
* Athletic Events

### Summer Residential Academy

* Academic Enrichment
* Career Awareness
* Senior Forums
* Cultural Trips
* Stipend

### Summer Bridge Program

* College Credits
* Collegiate Classroom Experience
* College Residential Living
* Work-Study Opportunities
* Stipend
