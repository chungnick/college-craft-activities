# Procedure of the CEC

## How to participate & Competition Criteria

**The CEC 2026 will run from 2 March to 5 June 2026.**

Get started now with our free resources and turn your ideas into reality! Connect with entrepreneurs from around the world, share insights, and gain inspiration for your entrepreneurial journey.

[Register](https://entrepreneurship.de/en/register) or [log in now](https://entrepreneurship.de/en/login).

[Register online](/en/register) and create your account or [log in](/en/login). You will receive a confirmation email to activate your account. ### Difference between idea and project

Please note: In 2026, the CEC is purely an idea competition. Only idea submissions will be considered.Â 

**Idea**  
All entries must include an innovative solution or proposal for a business that addresses the Sustainable Development Goals. It may be in the conception, planning, or start-up phase, but must not yet have resulted in a start-up.

**Project**  
All entries must consist of innovative projects carried out by existing businesses in the form of companies, NGOs or informal programs.

**Important: Please make sure that you have the rights for everything you upload! You can find licence free images on [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com/) and [Pixabay](https://pixabay.com/). If you upload copyrighted images, audios etc., you have to cover the costs for any legal issues.**

### Status of your idea/project

You can view the status of your submission at any time in the dashboard under "My idea/project" in the particular idea/project. You can submit multiple ideas.

**Please note:** If we still have questions about your pitch in step 1, you have the option to redo and resubmit your pitch. Unfortunately, rejected submissions will not be able to participate in CEC 2025. A submission would only be possible again for the next CEC.
