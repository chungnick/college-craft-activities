# Young Scholars Program

# Young Scholars Program

Overview November 26, 2018

Welcome to the UC Davis Young Scholars Program, a summer
residential research program designed to introduce approximately
40 high-achieving high school students to the world of original
research in the biological, agricultural, environmental, and
natural sciences.

* [Learn more about the program](/ysp-about),
* [How to apply](/ysp-application), and
* [Program costs](/ysp-program-cost)

### Summer 2026 Program Dates

Program dates for summer 2026 are June 21 – August 1

Applications for summer 2026 will open on January 1,
2026. Learn more about the
application process on the [Application](/ysp-application) page
