# YSP Application

# Application

Overview August 25, 2014

#### 2026 Program Dates: June 21 - August 1

#### (Application opens January 1st.)

### Who can apply?

* Must be a **rising junior** (in your sophomore
  year when applying) or **rising senior** (in your
  junior year when applying)
* Must be 16 years of age by the first day of the program but
  not 18 years of age before the last day of the program.
* Must be a permanent US resident currently
  residing **and** attending school in the
  US

**No exceptions will be granted.** Requests for
exceptions will not receive a response.

### When is the application deadline?

* The application period is **January 1 – March
  1**, 11:59 PDT
* We can accept teacher recommendations through March 5, 11:59
  PDT
* **Late applications will not be accepted**
* Incomplete applications will not be reviewed

### **What is included in the application?**

1. Student, parent, and referring teacher contact information
2. School information, including weighted and un­weighted GPAs
3. Two teacher references submitted through the Young
   Scholars Program teacher recommendation website. The online
   recommendations will form the primary reference,
   **separate, formal** **letters of
   recommendation are not required and will not be reviewed**
   * One reference MUST be from a science teacher
   * The other must be from another teacher
   * Recommendations from counselors or mentors from outside
     of school are discouraged
   * References are due at the same time applications are
     due - We can accept references through March 5. However,
     you must plan ahead to give your teachers sufficient time
4. Summary of school activities and other interests, activities,
   and research experiences
5. Personal essays

### How do I request a program fee reduction or an application fee waiver?

See more information on our [Program
Costs](/ysp-program-cost) page

**Program Fee Reduction Requests:**Funds are
available to assist those students who might otherwise be unable
to attend due to financial constraints. To request consideration
for a fee reduction the student should
submit a letter describing parent/guardians’ financial
obligations and need for support to the director, Megan
Bettis by the application deadline [HERE](https://forms.gle/6tPTiVU6siQ1fvRx5)

**Application Fee Waiver Requests:**Applicants
can request an application fee waiver by completing a
form [HERE](https://forms.gle/rcb8vMou6uTLxdwC6).
An application fee waiver will be granted to any student who
requests one prior to the application deadline.

### How do I apply?

Application time period: January 1 - March 1

Application portal: [https://ysp-app.ucdavis.edu](https://ysp-app.ucdavis.edu/)

###### The application portal will not be available until January 1, 2026
