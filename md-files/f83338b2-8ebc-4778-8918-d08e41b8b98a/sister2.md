# [Summer Pre-College Programs](https://precollege.tcnj.edu/)

Menu

* [Home](#)

* Get an edge  
   on your future

Are you a high-achieving high school student? Do you want to explore potential careers in an intellectually stimulating environment? Then we’ve got a summer program for you.

Choose from a wide variety of options — both residential and commuter — and put your skills to the test as you take on academic challenges with your peers in state-of-the-art facilities on a beautiful college campus.

## School of the Arts and Communication

### [2D Animation Camp](https://artscomm.tcnj.edu/summercamps/2d-animation-camp/)

Bring your own characters to life, just like in your favorite cartoons! Step into the world of 2D animation and learn the skills and techniques the professionals use; no prior experience needed. (options: on-campus residential or commuter)

### [Future Music Educators Institute](https://artscomm.tcnj.edu/summercamps/future-music-educators-institute/ "Future Music Educators Institute")

Join other high school students who are interested in pursuing a career as a music teacher or conductor and spend a week learning from the best — the TCNJ Music Department. (options: on-campus residential or commuter)

### [Game Design Camp](https://artscomm.tcnj.edu/summercamps/game-design-camp/ "Future Music Educators Institute")

Learn game design fundamentals and use a creative coding tool that encourages tinkering and play to design, program, and refine a small video game. All skill levels are welcome. (options: on-campus residential or commuter)

### [Graphic Design Camp](https://artscomm.tcnj.edu/summercamps/graphic-design-camp/ "Future Music Educators Institute")

Feeling creative? Collaborate on print and digital media projects using the Adobe Creative Suite in a modern Mac lab while you learn about typography, multimedia design, potential careers, and more. (options: on-campus residential or commuter)

### [Music Technology Camp](https://artscomm.tcnj.edu/summercamps/music-technology-camp/)

Explore creative ideas in music composition and audio production. You’ll write and edit songs, learn to make beats, and produce signature tracks in a Mac lab and a professional recording studio on campus. (options: on-campus residential or commuter)

## School of Engineering

### [Artificial Intelligence and Wireless Communications Camp](https://electrical-computerengineering.tcnj.edu/ai-wireless-camp/ "Artificial Intelligence and Wireless Communications Camp")

Put your creative mind to the test and fine-tune the skills you’ll need for a career in fields like science, engineering, finance, and medicine — all of which are being transformed by wireless AI technologies — at this weeklong residential camp.

### [Engineering in Health and Medicine Camp](https://bmesummercamp.tcnj.edu/ "Engineering in Health and Medicine Summer Camp")

Dive into the world of biomedical engineering at this weeklong residential camp. Collaborate with biomedical engineers and other students on projects covering medical imaging, drug delivery, medical device design, neural engineering, and more.

### **[iSTEM Summer Academy](https://istem.tcnj.edu/istem-summer-academy/)**

Calling all young designers, innovators, and creatives — get ready for a hands-on STEM experience. Join one of our three weeklong camps for middle school or high school students: MakerCraft, Creative Design, and Robotics. (offered as a commuter-only option)

## School of Science

### [Camp Innovate and Investigate](https://science.tcnj.edu/camp-innovate-and-investigate/ "Camp Innovate and Investigate")

Discover firsthand how experiments and research drive real-world solutions in the exciting world of science, from math and physics to computer science, biology, and chemistry. (offered as a commuter-only option)
