If you are a rising sophomore -senior high school student who is interested in STEM, poultry, animals, and/or NC State, consider applying to Poultry Science Summer Institute.

Students will experience

* Poultry Science – from embryology to immunology!
* Campus life & stay at University Towers
* University iconic landmarks, farms, feed mill, and poultry industry tours
* Collaborating with peers and making new friends

Cost is just $75 per student thanks to funding provided by the U.S. Poultry & Egg Association.

[Learn more about the Poultry Science Summer Institute](https://cals.ncsu.edu/prestage-department-of-poultry-science/students/clubs-and-programs/).
