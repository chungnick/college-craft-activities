# K-12 STEM Programs

WiSTEM reaches 12,000+ K-12 students and families through STEM programming and outreach.

WiSTEM leads the following K-12 STEM programs:

* [STEM Girl Day at UT Austin](https://girlday.utexas.edu/)
* [High School Summer Camps and Virtual Academies](https://womeninstem.utexas.edu/high-school-camps/)
* [Role Model Monday Webinars](https://womeninstem.utexas.edu/role-model-monday-webinars/)

Additional WiSTEM community outreach and public STEM engagement is powered by [WiSTEM Outreach Ambassadors](https://womeninstem.utexas.edu/outreach-ambassadors/), UT Austin undergraduate STEM students trained in hands-on STEM activity facilitation, communication skills, and leadership.

[Learn more about WiSTEM K-12 STEM Outreach and how to request volunteers for your campus or community STEM event.](https://womeninstem.utexas.edu/k-12-stem-outreach/)

### Find other UT Austin K-12 STEM Programs

[STEM Starts](https://stemstarts.utexas.edu/) is a comprehensive collection of K-12 STEM opportunities for students, families, and educators. [STEM Starts](https://stemstarts.utexas.edu/) connects families and educators with quality resources, STEM programs, and opportunities offered by UT Austin programs and units.

### Find Community K-12 STEM Programs

[Science Near Me](https://sciencenearme.org/) is a free resource to help families and educators quickly find opportunities to engage in all types of science, technology, engineering, math, (STEM) events, projects, and programs near you, in person and online.

### Engage with our K-12 Community

Visit the [WiSTEM K-12 Community webpage](https://womeninstem.utexas.edu/k-12-community/) for more resources and ways to engage.
