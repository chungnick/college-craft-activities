# University of Michigan

Ann Arbor, Michigan

## Summer Programs for Students at the University of Michigan

The University of Michigan is one of the nation’s leading public universities, known for its academic rigor, research excellence, and spirited campus life. Located in Ann Arbor, it provides a stimulating environment that inspires curiosity and achievement.

Envision’s summer programs for high school students give participants the opportunity to explore their passions while getting a taste of college life. At the University of Michigan, students are introduced to the energy and innovation of a world-class institution.

Together, Michigan’s reputation for excellence and Envision’s engaging programs create an unforgettable experience. Students leave inspired, prepared, and excited about what comes next.

## Top Things to Do If Staying Nearby:

* Visit the Ann Arbor Hands-On Museum for STEM fun
* Explore the Nichols Arboretum and Matthaei Botanical Gardens
* Attend a performance at the Michigan Theater or Hill Auditorium
