# Schedule

There are four contests during the regular season.  The window during which each contest can be accessed on HackerRank is as follows. 

* Contest #1: available Oct. 20, 2025; closes Sunday January 11, 2026 @ 11:59pm EST
* Contest #2: available Jan. 5, 2026; closes Sunday March 1, 2026 @ 11:59pm EST
* Contest #3: available Feb. 2, 2026; closes Sunday April 12, 2026 @ 11:59pm EDT
* Contest #4: available March 2, 2026; closes Sunday May 17, 2026 @ 11:59pm EDT

Based on the scores during the regular season, top students in all divisions will be invited to participate in an online Finals competition:

* Invitations E-mailed: May 1, 2026 through May 21, 2026
* Invitational Finals: Saturday, May 23, 2026 (Memorial Day weekend in the United States)

There is a nominal per-student registration fee to participate in the Finals. 

More details are available on the [Finals](/finals) page.
