# Application Process and Dates

The morning session on Biostatistics and R.

---

## To apply:

* Have your parent(s) review and acknowledge that you are submitting the application.
* Ask two teachers to write recommendations for you; at least one should be a recent math teacher. Send an electronic copy of the recommendation to your teacher to complete and return to us. The form is available for download in the right column of this page. Follow up with your teachers to ensure they complete the recommendations before the deadline.
* **Application and teacher recommendation deadline: March 31, 2026.**

## Interviews:

Copy Link

* We will interview promising candidates whose applications are complete and whose recommendations we receive by the March 31 deadline.
* Interviews will be held in mid-April

## Students notified:

Copy Link

* Upon request, we will provide students with a copy of their completed application.
* We will notify teachers when we have received their applications.
* We will notify students when their applications are complete. Please contact us if the deadline is approaching and you have not received confirmation that your application is complete.
* Notification of acceptance: On or before April 30th.

Twelve students will be admitted to the program, which will run based on sufficient enrollment. In past years, the number of applicants has far exceeded the number of admitted students.
