## ARML 2026 ~ May 29 – 30, 2026

ARML 2026 More Over120 teams from the United States, Canada, China and South Korea will be invited to compete in the 2026 ARML competition, sponsored by the D.E. Shaw group. Registration for the competition will be available here in late March 2026.

ARML LOCAL 2026 will take place during the weekend of April 18-19, 2026. Registration for the competition will be available here shortly.

ARML 2025 On May 31st, 128 teams and over 1800 students from the United States, Canada, China and South Korea competed in the 2025 ARML competition, sponsored by the D.E. Shaw group. Click [here](https://docs.google.com/document/d/17KQYnstIYxyz2r1KoyYIbkl-2Ex62jd1x-Cy-z8BcG8/edit?usp=sharing) for the final results of the competition. The contest packet is [here](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1rqZg0F5VV3iAVWAR3OWMWceL9cggBd1q/view?usp=drive_link).

ARML LOCAL 2025 took place during the weekend of April 26 – 27, 2025. [Click Here](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1oyQnPifsuRalmPwq9TwXbr-BRnxthwCKwswIOEeSeuM/edit?usp=sharing) for the results of the ARML LOCAL competition.

**ARML 2024** On June 1st, 2024 113 teams and over 1600 students from the United States, Canada, China and South Korea competed in the 2024 ARML competition, sponsored by the D.E. Shaw group. Click [here](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1AE4ZQnF34jSIfIjzbCoTqJVg3rVCJefA44Us9Wt8OPs/edit?pli=1#heading=h.tzi8w4gu2tvz) for the final results of the competition. The contest file is available [here](https://drive.google.com/file/d/17hwc3NYPxPfyxxTLGTg9tzKqXWZQjx30/view).

**ARML Local 2024** took place during the weekend of April 20th and 21st, 2024.  [Click here](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1ZORwkJo2f8tacIKqxTZEYxtwBe4M3pdvaBvb_vh_BEk/edit#heading=h.tzi8w4gu2tvz) for the results from the ARML LOCAL competition.
